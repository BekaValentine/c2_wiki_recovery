An excellent comic book, the only flaws in its screen adaptation are due to the Wachowskis. OTOH it wouldn't have got made without them, so more power to them.

Portman and Weaving's performances were perfect. The main letdown is the final TheMatrix-style fight scene, which was as unnecessary as it was cliched.
