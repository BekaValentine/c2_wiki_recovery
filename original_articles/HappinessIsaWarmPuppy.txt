First published in 1962, Happiness Is a Warm Puppy was Charles M. Schulz’s first book. It was on the New York Times bestseller list soon, and has sold millions of copies since. It launched Schulz’s to stardom. 

The collector’s edition 
* re-creates the original look and feel of the title. On every spread, there’s a tiny tidbit of wisdom from one of Charlie Brown’s gang that reminds us that we will find happiness in the simple things in life, along with one of Schulz’s irresistible drawings. 
* it takes one down a cherished memory lane.
