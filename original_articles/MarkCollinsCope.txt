Mark Collins-Cope is a software development coach and programmer.

Mark is author of Agile Development with Iconix Process.

Mark is also editor of ObjectiveView magazine (www.ovmag.com) - a PDF based online magazine discussing "top-end" issues in OO software development.

Mark has been involved in software development for over 20 years, and writes articles for a variety of magazines and has contributed to a number of books (e.g. Extreme Programming Explained and Quality Web Systems).

Mark speaks regularly at conferences in Europe.
