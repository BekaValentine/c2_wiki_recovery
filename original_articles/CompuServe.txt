A pioneer in providing OnlineInformationServices: started as a skunkworks project within HRBlock as a way to make better use of offpeak network bandwidth in 1979; acquired by AmericaOnline in 1997. Provided an early feel for what was to come in the Internet with discussion groups, on-line software upgrades and trials, multimedia file posting areas, and email.
