''One of the most important, and yet potentially frustrating, tasks that is required to allow any make-based build environment to function properly is the correct listing of dependencies in the makefile. This paper by the current maintainer of GnuMake appears to be the last word on dependency-tracking for  makefiles, a surprisingly tricky thing to get right.
