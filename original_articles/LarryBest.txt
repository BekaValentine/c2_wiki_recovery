Larry Best is a senior principal at American Management 
Systems, Inc., currently serving as Director of Application
Architecture Lab of the AMS Center for Advanced 
Technologies. In this role he contributes to AMS's 
development of an
advanced Smalltalk-based foundation application 
architecture consisting of a number of generic 
application software
components. He is author of 
''Application Architecture: Modern Large-Scale Information Processing''
(Wiley, 1990), and is a
frequent writer and speaker on the topic of software 
architecture
