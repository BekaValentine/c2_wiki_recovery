LivingDocument - document that is still being revised and improved

WikiWiki, of course, is a great tool to manage LivingDocument''''s.

Examples:

	* business plan
	* training manual
	* software architecture description
	* draft of a book
	* company phone list (one Wiki page per person)
	* LooseyGoosey inventory database
