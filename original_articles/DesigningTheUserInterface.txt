'''DesigningTheUserInterface''' BenShneiderman 

(Out of Print)
[ISBN 0201165058] Subtitled ''Strategies for Effective Human-Computer Interaction'' this is one of the classics of the field. Shneiderman advocates putting people in the drivers seat, making the system responsive to their needs and as much as possible allowing the human user to directly manipulate the data.

Shneiderman's pioneering work has strongly influenced the design and functionality of many software applications.
