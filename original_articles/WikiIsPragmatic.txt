	* If some aspect of Wiki is not useful (in the eyes of the overall Wiki community), it will tend not to be reinforced since there will be little enthusiasm for it.
		* '''Examples:''' Many WikiBadge''''''s.
	* If some aspect of Wiki proves to be very useful, it will tend to be reinforced, and in many cases will attract more enthusiasts.
		* '''Examples:''' ExtremeProgramming, MustEverythingOnThisWikiBeExtreme.
	* If some aspect of Wiki is of questionable worth, the best way to find out its true worth is to try it and see if it catches on.
