''The Arcus Project  ''

http://www.objectarchitects.de/arcus/gifs/arcblogo.gif

ARCUS was a research project, trying to find architectural and design patterns for business information systems.  The project ended in September 1997, but the patterns live on.

Initially there were three pattern languages available:

* RelationalDatabaseAccessLayer
* ErrorHandling
* DecouplingOfObjectOrientedSystems

The team members were:

* KlausRenzel
* WolfgangKeller
* JensColdewey
* update - where's the team?
