Conversations can be difficult critters to maintain. It's not just a matter of you and a friend mutually rubbing your ideas together. There are all kinds of contexts and skills involved in becoming a good conversationalist.

The ConversationPatternLanguage is a place to collect together patterns useful in creating, maintaining, and tidily completing conversations.

* ListenWithQuestionRunning
* ListenWithYourAnswerRunning
* OnlySayThingsThatCanBeHeard
* ConquerWithCompassion
* SeldomAffirmNeverDenyAlwaysDistinguish
* RetireGracefully
* EprimeLanguage
* NeverApologize
* Others?
