GamaSutra is the Internet arm of the CMP Game Group which is a collection of resources, and community for the professional ComputerGamesIndustry. The CMP Game Group encompasses the Game Developer Conference (GDC), GamaSutra, and Game Developer Magazine.

GamaSutra holds a collection of news, features, and information relating to the ComputerGamesIndustry. One of the more innovative features of both GamaSutra and Game Developer Magazine, is the publicly available collection of PostMortem reports on game projects. A free login is required to access the content of GamaSutra.
