The "links" browser is a TextModeBrowser, somewhat akin to the LynxBrowser, but much newer.  Unlike the LynxBrowser, it makes some attempt to render tables reasonably.

http://links.browser.org/

http://artax.karlin.mff.cuni.cz/~mikulas/links/

----

This browser has been forked several times. My favorite fork is ELinks. As of 2005-05-03 it is actively developed.

http://elinks.or.cz/
