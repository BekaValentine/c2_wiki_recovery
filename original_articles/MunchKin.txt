A MunchKin is a RolePlayer who plays to win, rather than to play, and is generally fairly clueless about anything not explicitly laid out in the rulebooks.

MunchKin''''''s treat RPGs as games, not roleplaying.

http://acasun.eckerd.edu/~dagorhir/articles/munchkin
