The BsdUnix solution to the problem, normally addressed in Linux by a JournalingFileSystem, of unexpected shutdowns sometimes leaving file system metadata in an inconsistent state.


Design and implementation:
http://www.usenix.org/publications/library/proceedings/usenix99/full_papers/mckusick/mckusick.pdf

Comparison with journaling:
