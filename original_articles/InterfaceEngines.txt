Engines which pull in content from one system and convert it to another form.

Commonly used in integrating hospital systems.  Also been used in helping many electricity financial systems talk to each other.

Examples:

	*Datagate 
	*HCI-Link Cloverleaf 
	*Hublink 
	*OACIS Gateway++ 
	*SMS OPENLink 
	*Cerner Corp's OpenEngine
	*NEONNet
