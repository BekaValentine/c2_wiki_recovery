''BottomFeeder ''
* An RSS/Atom Viewer written in VisualWorks Smalltalk.  
* Open source, and cross platform.  
* Written to handle RSS feeds in 0.9, 0.91. 0.92, 0.93, 1.0, and 2.0 format.  
* Also supports Atom (0.2 and 0.3), as well as a large number of namespaced modules.  
* Handles aggregated feedlists in OCS (0.4 or 0.5) format.  

Have a look and try it out
* http://www.cincomsmalltalk.com/BottomFeeder
