SchematronLanguage is a rule based validation mechanism for XmlDocuments, and soon to be (in 2005) adopted by IsoOrganization as an international standard. See http://www.xmlmania.com/news_article_1669-Final-Committee-Draft-of-ISO-Schematron-Released-for-Public-Review.php

It is said SchematronLanguage can be used in conjunction with other SchemaLanguagesForXml.
