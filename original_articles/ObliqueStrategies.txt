The ObliqueStrategies are a deck of cards devised by BrianEno and PeterSchmidt to give creative workers (commonly music composers) a set of basic working principles, ideas, and ZenSlap''''''s whilst under pressure.

They advocate ThinkingOutOfTheBox, and are thus similar to a CreativeWhackPack.
