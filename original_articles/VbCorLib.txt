VBCorLib is a Visual Basic 6 (VbClassic) reimplimentation of the MicrosoftDotNet mscorlib assembly. By using the ECMA standards and documentation, the utility classes and interfaces are being recreated as closely as possible within Visual Basic 6 confines.

The Home Page can be found at http://www.kellyethridge.com/vbcorlib.
