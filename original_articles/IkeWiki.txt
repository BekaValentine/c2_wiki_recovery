''IkeWiki - the Semantic Wiki for Knowledge Workers''

Development of IkeWiki has stopped. The developers are now ( 20090709 )working on a successor: KiWi
* http://semanticweb.org/wiki/IkeWiki
* http://semanticweb.org/wiki/KiWi

IkeWiki is one of a number of prototypes of Semantic Wikis using the ResourceDescriptionFramework (RDF) and the WebOntologyLanguage (OWL).
