MIT Scheme 

The MIT version (Currently -- 7.7.1 June 2002) of Scheme Language is available:

* The primary distribution site for this software is the FTP server ftp://swiss-ftp.ai.mit.edu/pub/mit-scheme/current
** The complete distribution and the most recent release is available at the MIT address.
** 350+ page documentation is at: ftp://swiss-ftp.ai.mit.edu/pub/mit-scheme/current/doc-pdf/scheme.pdf
** SchemeObjectSystem docs: ftp://swiss-ftp.ai.mit.edu/pub/mit-scheme/current/doc-pdf/sos.pdf
** Other manuals also in same directory: ftp://swiss-ftp.ai.mit.edu/pub/mit-scheme/current/doc-pdf

See http://www.swiss.ai.mit.edu/projects/scheme/ and http://www.gnu.org/software/mit-scheme/ for more info.
