''An addition to the FederationOfYourInternet is the FederatedCard''.ThinkingOutLoud.DonaldNoyes.20130920
----
An artifact created to preserve via Federation, notes, ideas and TheOtherThings which previously and temporarilay have existed as scribblings upon a note pad or note card.

Together with other FederationIdeas, it will allow the accumulation of information of all types in all reachable locations in ones own FederatedSpace.

Definition and examples of one sort can be found at http://donaldr.noyes.com/fed/things/FederatedCard.htm
