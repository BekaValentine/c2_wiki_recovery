GeorgeGoble coauthored the paper 
A DUAL PROCESSOR VAX-11/780 which was published as 
School of Electrical
Engineering Technical Report TR-EE 81-31, Sept 1981 [1]. 

It was also published in the proceedings of The 9th
Annual Symposium on COMPUTER ARCHITECTURE, ISSN 0149-7111 IEEE catl no. 82CH1754-1 ACM order
no. 415820 IEEE COMPUTER SOCIETY ORDER NO. 411, sponsored by the IEEE Computer Society and ACM,
held in Austin, TX, April 26-29, 1982. 

TR-EE 81-31 was the EE school's most widely distributed technical report
ever, with around 2,500 being printed.
