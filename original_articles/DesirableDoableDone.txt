''When you are concentrating on turning notions into ItWorks code, you pass through three stages'' ThinkingOutLoud.DonaldNoyes 20121119 20140329
*Stage 1 - Desirable
** It is a desirable notion or feature, fulfilling a practical computing need
*Stage 2 - Doable
** It is possible to accomplish within a reasonable period of time
*Stage 3 - Doing, Done
** It becomes a working artifact, satisfying the original notions
