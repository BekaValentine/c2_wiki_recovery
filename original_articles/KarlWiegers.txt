Karl E. Wiegers is an author and lecturer in the area of software quality and software process. His work as a process engineer at Eastman Kodak laid the groundwork for his many books. He is now out on his own and maintains his software process focus. The more curious reader can find his papers and articles at http://www.processimpact.com/.

Wiegers is the author of the book ''CreatingaSoftwareEngineeringCulture''.
