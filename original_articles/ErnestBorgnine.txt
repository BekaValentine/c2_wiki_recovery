Star of stage and screen, TotemAnimal, mason, and promoter of flat tax. Bears an uncanny resemblance to GiuseppiDeGaetano.

Starred in BadDayAtBlackRock, WildBunch, TheDirtyDozen, McHalesNavy and won an Oscar for his role in "Marty".  Some know him best for his role as Dominic Santini in the television show "Airwolf".  More recently he appeared in Gattaca.
