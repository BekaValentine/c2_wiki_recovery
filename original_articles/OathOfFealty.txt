'''Oath Of Fealty''' by LarryNiven and JerryPournelle, first published 1981.

A novel set in a (relatively) near future "arcology" in Los Angeles. Best known for the first documented appearance in print of the phrase "EvolutionInAction".  Early usages of similar terms have been noted on that page.
