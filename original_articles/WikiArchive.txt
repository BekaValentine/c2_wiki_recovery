2005 marks the WardsWikiTenthAnniversary. At this juncture, it may be illuminating to browse through some of the history of the pages of this site.

To explore on your own, replace "P''''''ageTitle" with the page title of your choice in the following link:
* http://web.archive.org/web/*/http://c2.com/cgi/wiki?PageTitle

Since the WikiList shows all pages on WardsWiki, you can use archived versions of it to browse as if you are actually reading the wiki of that day. Note that if the page has been updated you'll have the new version; replace the numeric date string in the URL with an asterisk to see a list of all archived versions. Note that BackLink''''''s will work to some extent, too.
