Anne Thomas is a senior consultant with The Patricia Seybold Group. 
Anne specializes in Sun's Java, Microsoft's COM, CORBA, application 
servers, and other distributed object and component based technologies 
and strategies. She has over 20 years of experience working in distributed 
computing technology. Anne is author of a number of white papers and 
articles which have been featured in a variety of trade papers, magazines, 
and web columns. She is a frequent speaker at trade shows, industry 
seminars, and universities.
