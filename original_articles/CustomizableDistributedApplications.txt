CustomizableDistributedApplications (CDAs) are client/server applications, which GUI, workflow, distribution model and business logic can be changed at run-time according to current user needs. There is a need to have this kind of applications in domains of ERP systems, viewers/players, digital content distribution, instant messaging and games. Development of these applications with the existing technologies is a very hard task. Considered ProgrammableModularFramework (PMF) is a framework that make the development of CDAs an easy task.
