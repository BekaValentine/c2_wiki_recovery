"Scrum Methodology" may refer to the formalized version of Scrum, available from ADM (AdvancedDevelopmentMethods), which makes Scrum look like a heavyweight process.

So Scrum''''''Process is the thing you learn from the ScrumBook, and the thing that people usually mean when they say they're "doing Scrum."  Scrum''''''Methodology is a formal product available from ADM.
