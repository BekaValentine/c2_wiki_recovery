World wide postal codes are a conceptual framework for building teams. This list consists of a set of areas that people can identify themselves as living in so that when they are building teams, it should be easy to match up people living within moving or commuting distance of each other. Sizes are similar throughout the world and tend to map to areas the size of Canadian states (Provinces) American States (States) European States (Nations) African states (Countries) and similar entities throughout the world.

Special emphasis is also given to large islands or archipelagoes of small islands.
