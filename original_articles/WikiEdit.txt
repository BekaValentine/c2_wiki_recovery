'''Wiki text editor: WikiEdit'''

Visual editor for Wiki markup (used in WackoWiki).

Main features:
	* Toolbar
	* Autoindent
	* Shortcuts
	* Works in MSIE and Mozilla

You can find the project's page here: http://wackowiki.com/WikiEdit.

I'm ready to help anyone porting WikiEdit to any other markup type.
