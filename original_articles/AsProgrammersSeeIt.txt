Many of the declarations and descriptions in this wiki are views AsProgrammersSeeIt.

When the software is completed and the programming is done, it is up to management to provide the remaining parts of what it takes to make the software product economically viable. 

This begins before the software is written, when the enterprise makes the decision to produce a product which fills a niche in the market that is viewed as being potentially profitable.

It is not just a decision to 
* DoSomethingDontJustStandThere
* It involves 
** Vision
** SpaceStaffSchedule
** FollowThrough

While some of the pages in this wiki involve the three above, most are focused on producing software AsProgrammersSeeIt.
