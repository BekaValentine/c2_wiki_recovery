Helpful to remember

	''Een goed begin is het halve werk.'':	A good start is half the work.

	''Roei met de riemen die je hebt.'':	Row with the oars you've got, make do with what's available.

	''hutspot (sometimes known as huts''e''pot)'':	Mulligan stew, perhaps a good synonym for SpaghettiCode ?

	''Praten is zilver, zwijgen is goud'':	Talk is silver, silence is golden (I think GermanLanguage has this too. [''Reden ist Silber, Schweigen ist Gold.''])

	''Een schip op het strand is een baken in zee'':	A ship on the beach is a lighthouse to the sea. (As quoted by FredBrooks in TheMythicalManMonth.)

	''Doe maar gewoon, dan doe je al gek genoeg'':	Just act normally, that's crazy enough.

	''Laat hem een toontje lager zingen'':	Take him down a peg.
