SteveJorgensen works as an Agile RubyOnRails programmer for RenewableFunding in PortlandOregon, and is also taking on moonlighting clients for Rails and other kinds of Web application development and maintenance.

Steve is currently interested in techniques for achieving long-term, sustainable development in Rails by applying techniques such as Hexagonal Architecture. He is also engaged in exploration of the virtues and vices of various forms of the TellDontAsk principle.

Steve is a frequent contributor of answers on StackOverflow and is the author of VbLiteUnit.  His professional Web site is http://stevej.name, and his technical blog is at http://techblog.stevej.name.
