Catch phrase from a fictional television comedy show in the movie ''RoboCop''.

See http://web.archive.org/web/20020223222041/http://www.english.uiuc.edu/newmedia/pantumsinchai.htm

Almost certainly swiped from C.M. Kornbluth's 1951 classic ScienceFiction story "The Marching Morons" -- which, as it was written before the inflation of the 1970s, gave us the hilarious catch phrase "Would you buy that for a quarter?".
