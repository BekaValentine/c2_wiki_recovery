VisionWare PLC is the UK's leading company in providing identification tools for legacy databases. 

The tools enable the owners of any set of databases to search for, match and merge information on people and properties, independent of the places and formats that this information is stored at. The products are used by Health and Government Institutions, to create single views on patients and citizens in order to simplify processes in which these client play a role.
