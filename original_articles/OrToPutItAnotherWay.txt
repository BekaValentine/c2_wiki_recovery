Different shades of meaning in Approaches, Methods and Practices
* Approach
** Is a formulation adopted when tackling a problem.
*** Example: "his approach to every problem is to draw up a list of pros and cons" Also: attack, plan of attack] 
* Method
** Is An orderly procedure or process in which one determines a regular manner of doing anything; or put another way, a manner; a way; a mode.
*** Example: a method of teaching languages; a method of improving the mind.
* Practice
** That which one does as a habitual or customary action or act.
