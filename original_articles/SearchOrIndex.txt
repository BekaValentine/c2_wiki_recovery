In StrictOrLazy, the advantages and benefits of the two approaches are presented. In the same way, when finding information, your needs may vary as widely as your reasons for seeking it in the first place. If you want a quick, targeted bit of information, you may do so by using an Internet SearchEngine. The advantage of this method is that answers are nearly immediate and furnishes what may prove to be more valuable bits of related or located information.

One may use searches when it it not known exactly what one wants to find. Indexes on the other hand are useful if you have precise requirements and dimensions in the information needs.
