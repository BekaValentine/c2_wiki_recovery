A public university located in SeattleWashington.  UW is known for, among other things, its ComputerScience and ComputerEngineering program (http://www.cs.washington.edu), ranked among the top ten in the nation.

UW is usually pronounced "You Dub".

Famous UW graduates include:
	* Kenny G, saxophonist
	* Bruce Lee, martial artist
	* Ted Bundy, serial killer
	* Dale Chihuly, artist
