Vanilla is a wiki clone written in REBOL (see RebolLanguage). It provides a collaborative editing environment, and is fairly extensible and easy to learn.

As of December 2003, Vanilla is hosted at http://www.vanillasite.at (old home page was at: http://www.langreiter.com/space/vanilla)
