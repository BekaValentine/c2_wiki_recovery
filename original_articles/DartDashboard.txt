Dart is an OpenSource automated test system with web based result reporting.

KitWare hosts Dart, along with other cool OpenSource projects:
	* Visualization Toolkit (VTK)
	* Insight Segmentation and Registration Toolkit (ITK)
	* ParaView
	* CMake
	* GCC_XML
	* CABLE
