A wiki-based compendium of quotations project set up at http://www.wikiquote.org/.

Wikiquote is an offshoot project of WikiPedia.

It started work in June 2003. As of August 2003, it has created a few thousand quotations and are expanding quickly.

It has 23,196 articles as of February 27, 2014.
