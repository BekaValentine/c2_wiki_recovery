There seems to be a problem with text lines ending in a backslash ("\"). This mainly affects AsciiArt embedded in the page.  When the page is saved, the backslash is removed and the following line is appended.

'''WorkAround:''' Append a space after the backslash (but SyntacticallySignificantWhitespaceConsideredHarmful).

''This was quite a pain on SnuspLanguage. I imagine it would make pasting CeeLanguage multiline macro code painful as well.''
