The ManagementCycle is: 
* Identify 
* Analyze and Prioritize 
* Plan and Schedule 
* Track and Report 
* Control 
* Learn

----
This relates to BusinessManagement and the cycle should identify the ManagementIssues and provide processes and plans to minimize risks that arise from the ManagementIssues.  A lot of this can be achieved by managing teams ensuring that all ContemporaryDevelopmentRoles are practices correctly.

----
I would insert the last item between each of the foregoing items, for learning is not just about the whole thing, but about the parts as well. You do not learn about trees by looking at the forest. -- DonaldNoyes
