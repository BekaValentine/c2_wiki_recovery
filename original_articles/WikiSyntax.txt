A wiki's markup syntax, aiming to provide a terser, more natural syntax than HyperTextMarkupLanguage, but with (mostly) the same expressive power.

Examples:
* TextFormattingRules for this wiki
* MediaWiki syntax
* WikiCreole
* MarkDown

See also WikiMarkup, WikiMarkupLanguage, TextFilter
