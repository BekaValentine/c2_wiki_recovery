The '''Pattern Language of Framework Construction''' by ShaiBenYehuda was presented in PlopConference-1997.

One of the most effective form of reuse in OO technology is the framework. The PatternLanguage
introduced tries to capture some design and architectural patterns that reoccurs while
constructing OO frameworks. The pattern language is based on a different perspective, regarding
frameworks as a tool for developers.

The full paper from the PLoP-97 with the framework construction pattern language: http://st-www.cs.uiuc.edu/users/hanmer/PLoP-97/Proceedings/shai.pdf
