BoostTypeTraits is a library providing a consistent set of TraitsTemplates for the properties of types in CeePlusPlus. It is often used in conjunction with BoostMpl. It is one of the BoostLibraries.

The book CppTemplateMetaprogramming gives an introduction to its use, and the history of its origins.
