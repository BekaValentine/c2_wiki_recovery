The Korea Patterns Group meets weekly on Saturday Afternoons at a Cafe in Yeok''''''Sam-Dong.
The location is relaxing and the conversation is engaging. 

The group studies established analysis and design patterns.
We also have a plan for Korea Design Pattern Conference. 

	When::	Every Saturday 1:00pm - 3:00pm 

	Where::	Yeok''''''Sam-Dong in Seoul

	Who::	Patterns enthusiasts, any level of experience. There are no requirements for membership and regular attendance is encouraged but not required. 

	Directions::	 Exit at YeokSam Station 4. We will be meeting at Orange Cafe.

Our information can be found at: http://www.designpattern.net.
