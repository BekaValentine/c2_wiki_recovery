See DontPanic.

	* There's a decision to be made in every situation (even the decision to stop trying)
	* As long as there is a decision, you can make a good (or a bad) choice.
	* You generally make bad decisions when panicking.
	* Bad decisions generally make things worse.

''Therefore:''  ThereIsNoSituationSoBadThatYouCantMakeItWorseByPanicking ''(agreed, this is a ''very'' long name :)''
