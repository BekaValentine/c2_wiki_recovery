TeamThink is an excellent collection of tricks, games, techniques and methods to make meetings a success.

71 techniques categorized under 
	* techniques to improve meeting productivity
	* techniques to boost creativity
	* techniques to gather information
	* techniques to make decisions
	* techniques to implement decisions
with names like Two truths and a lie, Prouds and sorries.

For every technique the Whats Whens and Wheres are given.
