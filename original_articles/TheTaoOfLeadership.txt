''The Tao Of Leadership'' by JohnHeider, ISBN:0-89334-079-0. One of the BooksOnLeadership.

An application of the TaoTeChing to the topic of LeaderShip.

Outstanding wisdom.  Should be required reading for anyone responsible for leading a group of people in an endeavor - especially leading a group of software developers in a software development project.
