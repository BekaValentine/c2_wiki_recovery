Quartus Forth is a ForthLanguage compiler for PalmOs-based HandHeld computers.  It allows development of applications on the device itself.  Version 2.0 now supporting ARM based OS 5 and future OS 6 platforms.

For more information, see http://quartus.net/products/forth/ and the TwikiClone at http://quartus.net/wiki/.

''Interestingly, "quartus" is the Latin for "fourth".''
