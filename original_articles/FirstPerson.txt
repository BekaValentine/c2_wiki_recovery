FirstPerson is a literacy term referring to when a person speaks from their own opinions or experiences. Sentences that indicate FirstPerson narrative include:

 '' '''I''' was walking down the street and then...'',
 '' She told '''me''' that she didn't want to...'',
 '' '''I''' believe that the world would be a better place if...''
----
FirstPerson is commonly used in diary type literature, or [?]. It does not necessarily mean the writer's perspective (as it could be the persona they are assuming for literary effect) but this is usually the case, especially in the WikiWikiWeb.
