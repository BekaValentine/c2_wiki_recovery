Teach SelfImprovementPatterns to your team.

Most people are already doing the best they can at the moment.  Selling people on the idea that keeping a journal, having a to-do list, and so on will improve the team is a hard sell.	An important way that teams improve is by tackling shared challenges and solving them - by discovering that they ''need'' to improve.   See GrowingYourTeam.
