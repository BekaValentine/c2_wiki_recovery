This is Chapter 9 of ''TheLeadersHandbook'' [ISBN 0070580286] by PeterScholtes.

A lot of discussion on PerformanceAppraisal''''''s, bonuses, and rewards occurred on the Extreme Programming yahoo mailing list. Lot of concerns and questions raised there can be found in this chapter.
