JimmyWales is to the WikiMedia projects what WardCunningham is to the WikiWikiWeb.

Jimmy is his RealName (not Jim nor James nor Jimbo), but WikiPedia users usually call him "Jimbo".

Wales is from Alabama and was an options trader in Chicago. He lives in Florida, in Saint Petersburg, which is the home of WikiMedia.

WikiMedia was founded after the English WikiPedia, which is WikiMedia's most transited project.
