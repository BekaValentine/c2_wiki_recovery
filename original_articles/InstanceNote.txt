''A WoolenLanguage artifact'' ThinkingOutLoud.DonaldNoyes.20140824 
----
Used to denote a type of artifact which might use BackTicMarkup (also a WoolenLanguage term)
* to record an instance or even a history of instances, of stuff, or stuff-done
* they would record in an artifact the occurrences or events one wishes to record for later recall as references or as data for a matching artifactory.
* they might also be used as a means of summary of the contents of an artifact of sizes ranging from Several Paragraphs to Chapters, Volumes and Collections of Volumes, in an ArtifactRepository
