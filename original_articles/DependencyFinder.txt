Extracts information (class dependencies, metrics, etc.) from JavaLanguage class files produced by most JavaLanguage compilers. It includes a GraphicalUserInterface using JavaSwing, a CommandLineInterface with XSL stylesheets for output formatting, and a WebApplication.

You can get it from:
	* http://depfind.sourceforge.net/
	* http://sourceforge.net/projects/depfind/

Written by JeanTessier.
