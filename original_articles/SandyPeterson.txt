Originally known in the roleplaying world as the creator or co-creator of classic games like "CallOfCthulhu" and Runequest.  Sandy Petersen began computer game design with Microprose, and in 1993 he moved to ID Software, where he was a designer on "Doom", "Doom 2", and "Quake".  He is currently a Game Designer at Ensemble Studios.
