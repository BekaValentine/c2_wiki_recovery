'' What is a BodyOfWork? '' ThinkingOutLoud.DonaldNoyes.201408`4
----
For a start, it is:
* the total output of an individual, or a process, producing a collection of artifacts which can be identified as components or partitions of a much larger and more comprehensive work
* it may also be the end product, output, collective product, the things produced within a category of things
