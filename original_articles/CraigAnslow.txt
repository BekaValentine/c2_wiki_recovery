Craig completed a PhD thesis in Software Engineering in the School of Engineering and Computer Science at Victoria University of Wellington, New Zealand in 2013. Craig was a part of the ElvisSoftwareDesignResearchGroup. Craig was supervised by JamesNoble, RobertBiddle, and StuartMarshall. Craig is now working at the University of Calgary.

*Applying information visualisation techniques to software
*Creating better user interfaces for developer tools
*Building application Wikis
