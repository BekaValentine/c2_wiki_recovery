ChinaJapan.org is a web site dedicated to the study of Sino-Japanese Studies.  It is a host to projects related to this field and has a secondary goal of contributing to online resources in Asian studies more broadly speaking, especially by promoting interaction between students and scholars of East Asia within and outside the region.

* The site: http://www.chinajapan.org/
* The Modern East Asian History forums: http://forum.chinajapan.org/
* Sino-Japanese Studies WIki: http://www.chinajapan.org/wiki/

The site is created and hosted by Konrad Mitchell Lawson (http://www.muninn.net/blog/)
