http://www.pnotepad.org/

Open source (BSD license) mini-IDE / text editor for MicrosoftWindows. Has the usual features plus good integration with external tools. There's a plugin that lets you write scripts in PythonLanguage.
----
This is the one zero-cost editor I include in all my clients' boxes and everywhere else I go, too. Simple but powerful, this freebie is the editing tool of choice for the Wintel world.
