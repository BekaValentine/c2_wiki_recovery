''Object Design: Roles, Responsibilities, and Collaborations''  (ISBN:0201379430)

A new (in 2002), definitive book on ResponsibilityDrivenDesign by RebeccaWirfsBrock and AlanMcKean. Among many other things, it has a very good treatment of AllocationOfResponsibility.

It is an update of DesigningObjectOrientedSoftware  (1990, ISBN:0136298257)
