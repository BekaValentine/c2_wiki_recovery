The AgileJournal is an online magazine and developer resource center devoted to providing information about how to achieve agility in software development. AgileJournal is published by CMC Media and driven by Editor in Chief Liz Barnett. Prior to leading AgileJournal Liz Barnett was an analyst for Forrester Research and Giga Information Group. http://www.agilejournal.com
