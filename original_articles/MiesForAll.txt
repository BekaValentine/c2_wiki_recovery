MiesForAll  = Mies4All is an online architectural software.

URL: http://erational.org/software/mies4all.html

http://erational.org/software/img/mies4all_01.gif

mies4all is a tribute to the architecture of MiesVanDerRohe. 

This software enables you to build or deconstruct our own buildings with the most standard architectural elements: the slab, the wall, the column ...

You can record, modify and share your buildings on the Internet.
