The ''e'' text editor is a fast and elegant notepad replacement for Windows.

http://www.e-texteditor.com/images/e_smallscreen.png

It has a really unique undo history that visually shows the actions done and allows backtracing and branching. Another cool thing the FireFox style incremental search and the tabbed editing interface.

Homepage: http://www.e-texteditor.com
