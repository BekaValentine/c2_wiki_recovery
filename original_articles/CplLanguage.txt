Combined Programming Language (CPL)

An early-1960s programming language designed at Cambridge University and London University. A predecessor to BcplLanguage, which was a simplified version of CPL.

D.W. Barron, J.N. Buxton, D.F. Hartley, E. Nixon, C. Strachey: "The Main features of CPL". ''Computer Journal'', vol. 6 (1963), p. 134.

John Noel Buxton died 3rd November 2009, aged 75. http://www.guardian.co.uk/technology/2009/nov/12/john-buxton-obituary
