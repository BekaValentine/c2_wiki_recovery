''WikiWords can be used in a WikiWithMoreThanPages to refer to the WikiSentence'' ThinkingOutLoud.DonaldNoyes.20110819
----
The main artifact encountered in a Wiki is the WikiPage. There is the notion that artifacts may be subdivided into components including Phrases, Sentences, Paragraphs, Images, and Actions. 
----
The WikiSentence most likely defined would be that of a declaration or definitive statement. This would make context of the enlarged or complette artifact recognizeable by program as well as readers.
