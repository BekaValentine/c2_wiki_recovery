Paul Otlet (1868-1944) was a Belgian and a pioneer of the study of information. ''The father of modern documentation''

http://boxesandarrows.com/forgotten-forefather-paul-otlet/

See http://archives.mundaneum.org/en/history '''1868''' entry for more information.

One of the founders of the Mundaneum (http://www.mundaneum.org/) now located in Mons, Belgium.
