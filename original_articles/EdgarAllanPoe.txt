Edgar Allan Poe (1809-1849)                              

Often credited with inventing the mystery genre,                               
 with his stories of the detective M. Dupin.

Best known for writing short stories in the horror genre,
and for his poetry.

Some of his poems: 
	* TheRaven 
		* ''Quoth the Raven: Refactor More!''
	* Annabel Lee

Some of his short stories: 
	* The GoldBug
	* Hop-Frog (http://xroads.virginia.edu/~HYPER/POE/hop_frog.html) 

''Note:'' The "Allan" is often misspelled "Allen".
Hence the page EdgarAllenPoe.
