Published in 2002 by Manning Press:
http://www.manning.com/hatcher/
(Amazon: [ISBN: 1930110588])

Written by Erik Hatcher and SteveLoughran; certainly the largest book on Ant to date, and probably the best selling.

Tries hard to discuss Ant best practices, and encourage test-driven development and deployment, whenever appropriate.

Their AntPractices section is now hosted on the Ant site: http://wiki.apache.org/ant/TheElementsOfAntStyle
