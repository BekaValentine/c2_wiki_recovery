Jean-Marc Nerson is managing director of SOL, Paris. From 1985 to 1991 he was VP of Engineering with ISE, Santa Barbara, where he led the development of O-O tools and environments based on the Eiffel language. Since 1991, Dr. Nerson has been consulting on large-scale O-O projects worldwide and managing the development of reusable component libraries.

He is a co-author of ''SeamlessObjectOrientedSoftwareArchitecture''
