A phrase template with variables that carries its own colloquial meaning.

The name SnowClone derives from the prototypical sentence "Eskimos have 4 words for snow".  Generalized as "''$group'' have ''$number'' words for ''$concept''", it implies that slight variations of a concept are important enough to warrant their own words within a group.
