A hyperlink from one WikiPage to another. On this wiki, they are created by writing a CamelCase word. Many other wikis allow the use of FreeLink''''''s.
----
An ExternalWikiLink goes from a WikiPage to a page on a separate wiki site, whereas an InternalWikiLink links to a page in the same wiki. All these links make the WorldWideWiki. -- JeanHuguesRobert
