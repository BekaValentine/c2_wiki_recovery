* Q; What is NysLte?
* A: It is a text-page based PersonalInformationManager which utilizes locally stored and linkable information pages which can deal with your own information with simplified linking mechanisms accepting any word or group of characters enclosed by spaces at beginning and end as links. It will also use substitution token-character-strings followed by a colon ( c2: ) as substitute for a url. Linked by utilizing your browser. for example c2:DonaldNoyes will use your browser to access the page at http://c2.com/cgi/wiki?DonaldNoyes.
