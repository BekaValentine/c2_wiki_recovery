Hypertext pioneer who in the late 1980's produced and distributed programs popularizing the use of hypertext in flat-text documents. Links were identified by the file name enclosed like this: <Link01>. He also produced a configurable hypertext editor called Transtext. Another product useful in organizing ideas was called Houdini. His company which has developed in Northern California is called Maxthink. His specialty of information processing using hyperlinks concentrates on Semantics, Taxonomy, and KnowledgeAnnealing.  

He maintains a website explaining how these processes and paradigms can be utilized. (including a multimedia presentation which hyperlinks subjects covered and processes, questions and answers to those unfamiliar with the what and how of it all)
