Boston Wireless is an EastCoast organization deploying WiFi for the communication in Boston. Perhaps it is part of a national CommunicationsExplosion surely meant to change the WayThingsAreDone. A modern Boston Tea Party?

They will be setting up free wireless broadband access for boats in Boston Harbor and documenting the project
