EcksZed (XZ) is a compression tool developed within the Tukaani project (http://tukaani.org/)

* http://tukaani.org/xz/
* http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Xz 

It is being used for distribution by LLVM (LowLevelVirtualMachine) and the Clang compiler for CeePlusPlus (CeeLanguageFamilyFrontEnd)

A related compression tool is EllZip.
