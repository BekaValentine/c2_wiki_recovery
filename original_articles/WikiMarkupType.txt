Q: Shouldn't a type be accompanied with properties?

Q: If so, what are the properties of a WikiMarkupType?

A: In the case of textual types: 
* text-type (normal, bold, italics, and combinations [bold-italics])
* text-style (arial, courier, etc)
* text-color and text-background-color
* text-placement (margin, padding, border, etc.)
