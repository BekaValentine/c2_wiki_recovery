''This appears to be junk.  A discussion about microblogs would be worthy.  A collection of links (especially broken ones!) is worthless.''

It's an attempt to save the real history of microblogging in all of it's complexity. If you don't want it here, I'm going to look for a better place. But it has been here for over a year.

''The history of microblogging sounds interesting. A largely undifferentiated gaggle of links, sans any description, isn't a history -- it's meaningless.''

I use Wiki for taking notes in other to remember what I've know as services in the past... I'm already forgetting all about utterli.com and kwippy.com services I've really adored the time they were online...

Microblogging is not just Twitter, far from it.
