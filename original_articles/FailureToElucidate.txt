	* ''Well done, you've taken a small simple problem and broken it down into a large number of more difficult ones -- NickSimons''
----
A logical fallacy wherein the supporting definitions are harder to comprehend than the idea being argued. CircularReasoning may fall under this umbrella.

For more see: http://web.archive.org/web/*/http://www.intrepidsoftware.com/fallacy/failure.php (archived)
