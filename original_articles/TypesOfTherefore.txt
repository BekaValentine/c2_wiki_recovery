Combined with types of should:
	* ''Therefore we should'' - a hypothesis
	* ''Therefore we should'' - a command
Combined with types of did:
	* ''Therefore we did'' - a success story
	* ''Therefore we did'' - a horror story
More:
	* ''Therefore it is like'' - an analogy
	* ''Therefore do'' - a pattern (PortlandForm)
		* ThereforeBut
		* ThereforeThen
	* ''Therefore clearly'' - a cover-up
	* ''Therefore, a duck'' - irreversible indictment
