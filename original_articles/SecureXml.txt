''SecureXml: The New Syntax for Signatures and Encryption'', ISBN:0201756056

This book is written by DonaldEastlake, chairman of the IETF/W3C XML Digital Signature working group.  The writing style is said to be terse but is also comprehensive.

It goes into heavy topics such as creating CanonicalXml.

See XmlSecurity for a book that is easier for beginners.
