''Panel:''
Soft Issues and Other Hard Problems in Software Development
''at OopslaInSanJose''.

''Abstract:'' The key to making object technology work isn't technology. So what is it? This panel will cover
a lot of the "soft" issues about dealing with people and teams. It's partly about managing
software development, and mostly about doing it.
