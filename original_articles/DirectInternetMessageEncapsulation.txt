DirectInternetMessageEncapsulation is a WebServices Extension promoted by vendors like MicrosoftCorporation and IbmCorporation.

There were a flurry of activities in 2002 on the use of DIME to enable binary information to be passed between cooperating applications, see for example article at http://www.perfectxml.com/DIME.asp.
