''What is the idea implementation gap, and what can we do about it? '' ThinkingOutLoud.DonaldNoyes.20111025
----
Is among other things:
* the period of time between when a GoodIdea is exposed in ThinkingOutLoud or ThinkingSilently and when it becomes a real operation or an operative technology
* the time it takes to bring about the technology itself and to create an environment, an awareness that it exists, and growth of the application of it in every-day commerce and activity
