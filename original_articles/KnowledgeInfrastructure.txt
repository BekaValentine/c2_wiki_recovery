This term is defined as a combination of: 

'''CulturalInfrastructure''' - this has to do with the allowing people to work with knowledge; understand its basic terms (like PopUpKnowledge, CognitiveOverload, DispoSition, The ArithmeticOfLearning, etc.). 

'''ProcessInfrastructure''' - this has to do with looking at organizational processes in terms of knowledge. 

'''DigitalInfrastructure''' - this has to do with the technology that helps people manage their knowledge.
