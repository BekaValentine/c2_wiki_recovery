A variety of the LinuxOperatingSystem that runs on power PC computers such as those produced by Apple.

''A real sweet remedy when someone gives you a G4 and orders you to make the damned thing useful...''

LinuxPpc also runs on 
* MotorolaPowerStacks
* BeBoxes.  
Further, it runs on the 
* AppleNetworkServer (normal OS, AIX)
* RsSixThousands.
