'''Who Are We?'''
Interested in Java? Planning a project? The Triangle Java Users group has been established to provide Java-related information to its members, to facilitate contacts between its members, and to promote the use of Java technology in the mid-North Carolina area. TriJUG is a non-profit, vendor-independent organization.

'''Where We Are?'''
The Triangle JUG is an official Java Users Group based in the NorthCarolinaTriangleArea
