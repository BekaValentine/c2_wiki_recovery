WhizFolders is a two pane note organizer and outliner for Windows. Keep your notes and topics as individual pieces that you can organize, outline, rearrance in a hierardhical list. You can insert hyperlinks to notes, files, folders and web sites to use it as a bookmark manager too. WhizFolders is RTF based so you can always merge several notes to make a big document in rich text for your word processor. For more details, please visit http://whizfolders.com.
