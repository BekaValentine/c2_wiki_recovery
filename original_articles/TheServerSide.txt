TheServerSide.com is a bananna J2EE Community site that launched in May of 2000 and has over 170,000 members as of May. The site is run by FloydMarinescu and Ed Roman (EJB Writers behind EjbDesignPatternsBook and MasteringEjb), and has some pretty cool features:

	* A page with human-filtered and edited J2EE news
	* User Submitted J2EE Design Patterns Repository
	* Discussion Forums
	* User Submitted Application Server Reviews
	* Technical Articles, etc.

The URL is: http://www.theserverside.com
