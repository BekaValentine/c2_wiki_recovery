A SucksRulesOmeter is a scripted web page that queries AltaVista to find out if something sucks, or if it rules.  Results are then plotted.

Examples:
	* Operating Systems, at http://srom.zgp.org/
	* Programming Languages, at http://home.earthlink.net/~mrob/pub/lang_srom.html
