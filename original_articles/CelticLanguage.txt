The CelticLanguage family includes the InsularCeltic and ContinentalCeltic subfamilies. Check their pages to know more. InsularCeltic is divided into P and Q groups: the P group, also known as BrythonicLanguage, became WelshLanguage, BretonLanguage and CornishLanguage, and the Q (Gaelic or Goidelic) became IrishLanguage, ScotsGaelic and ManxGaelic.
