''Wiki Interface Standard '' A collaboration
----

For starters:

A wiki page shall contain:

 Header -- InterWikiLogo and Title       : Top Line
           Text and Images               : Body of Page
 Footer -- Executable Features included in a Menu : Bottom of Page
           Executable Menu Items   : EditText FindPage LikePages
                                   : InterWikiLogo presented with Links to SisterSites or sites with same Title

----

A minimum interface for InterWikiStandard:
 InterWikiPrefix
 InterWikiLogo
 InterWikiPages
