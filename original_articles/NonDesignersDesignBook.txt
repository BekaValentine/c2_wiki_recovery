''The Non-Designer's Design Book -- Design and Typographic Principles for the Visual Novice''
by RobinWilliams, Peachpit press, Berkeley, CA, ISBN: 1566091594

----

Robin gives us four design principles for improving
the pages we make with our desk-top publishing systems...

	* Proximity
	* Alignment
	* Repetition
	* Contrast

She devotes a chapter to each of these and adds a few 
more about type, life and learning.
