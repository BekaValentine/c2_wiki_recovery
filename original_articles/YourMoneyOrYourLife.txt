A national bestseller, subtitled "Transforming your relationship with money and achieving financial independence." How to live with less money, more simplicity, more time, yadayadayada. Bible of voluntary simplicity movement. Authored by Joe Dominguez and Vicki Robin, originally published 1992.

Biggest gap: reconciling this talk with human need for voluntary materialism.

----
It is not clear that the 'Biggest gap' is demonstrable, i.e. that there is a human need for voluntary materialism.  At the very least, a large share of the materialism that we see in current minority world culture is propped up, if not created, by propaganda and social conditioning.
