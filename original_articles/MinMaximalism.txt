MinMaximalism is when one sits on the fence between Maximalism and Minimalism.

Sitting on the fence is not bad, because sometimes we need minimal software and maximal software at the same time.

It sounds contradictory, but it is true.

See also MinimalismWeenie, FeatureWeenie.

Life is a compromise, and there is no black and white. Actually, there is both: black and white :-)
