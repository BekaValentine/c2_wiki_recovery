''A good way to aid in understanding of concepts is the use of examples '' DonaldNoyes.ThinkingOutLoud.20121028
----
When you introduce a concept to others not familiar with it, it is a GoodThing to use examples to clarify and explain. Using examples or demonstrations can help with concept perception and understanding.
