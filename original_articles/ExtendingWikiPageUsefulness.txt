''Grouping, Making ReachableAndSearchable, by Linking Pages, Documents and Sites into a single document '' DonaldNoyes.20100920
----
This is a notion that it is possible to make WikiPages more useful by grouping the pertinent links into a document form that ties a notion, idea or specific topic into a single linked and linkable document. One useful form of this notion is to use the PDF format to do so.
----
The ease of use of this format and the very practical way in which it works has made it an important weapon in my own InformationArsenal. By using just a few clicks of the mouse and a few keystrokes and MeaningfulName''''''s, the topic of WikiPages can be made ReachableAndSearchable by storage in my UnlimitedStorageCapacity local drives.
