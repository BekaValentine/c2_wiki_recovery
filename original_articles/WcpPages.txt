WCP stands for WikiChangeProposal, and is an open source effort to create a wiki based on several, well-articulated principles. Pages relating to this are:

* WcpSummary
* WcpMotivation 
* WcpStatus 
* WcpUseCases
* WcpSyntax 
** WcpEssExpressions 
** WcpSchema 
* WcpCriticism
