Game Neverending is an online Massively Multiplayer Online Game (former url: http://www.gne.net). The game is nonviolent and has attracted an unusually erudite community, giving birth to such sociopolitical concepts as the Gift Economy and helping hundreds of players from different sides of the globe develop relationships with each other, many of which can be carried on to the real world without a doubt.
