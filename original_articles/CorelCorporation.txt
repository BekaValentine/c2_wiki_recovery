An Ottawa, Canada company, arguably one of the very few Canadian companies that made it big internationally. Founded in 1985.
 
Products:
* WordPerfect
* CorelDraw (acquired from NovellCorporation)
* CorelLinux
* CorelPainter

----
The CEO, Mike Cowpland, was one the most vocal anti-Microsofties and ironically, a few years later, Microsoft acquired an ownership in Corel.
