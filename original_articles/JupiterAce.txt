A latecomer to the 80s microcomputer scene. Unusual in that it had ForthLanguage built-in rather than BasicLanguage.

It was similar in design to the SinclairSpectrum, probably because it was designed by the same folks: Richard Altwasser and Stephen Vickers.

''Factory configuration had only 3K of RAM!''
