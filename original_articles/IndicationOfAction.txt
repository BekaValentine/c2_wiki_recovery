'''Problem:'''

When performing an action on a system, a user may not be provided with any inherent confimation of their action as a result of the action itself.

'''Solution:'''

Provide a unique and perceptible sound immediately after the action is performed.

'''Examples:'''

* Pressing keys on a mobile phone
* Clicking a web link in Internet Explorer
