I am an associate professor of English and Head of the Division of Arts, Communications, and Humanities at Holy Family University, Philadelphia PA., USA. I hold the Ph.D. in English literature, attained at Lehigh University, Bethlehem, PA., USA., specializing in Modern and Contemporary American poetry and cultural studies. I teach courses in American and British literature, and I also write poetry. I have published a book entitled "Ideological Significance and Political Content of Twentieth Century American Poetry" as well as numerous articles. I may be contacted via email at jwoznicki@holyfamily.edu
