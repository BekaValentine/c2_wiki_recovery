''The Portable MBA'' by Robert F. Bruner (Author), Mark R. Eaker (Author), R. Edward Freeman (Author), Robert E. Spekman (Author), Elizabeth Olmsted Teisberg (Author), S. Venkataraman (Author)

http://images.amazon.com/images/P/0471222844.01._PE_PI_SCMZZZZZZZ_.jpg
ISBN: 0471222844

A good resource for a DeveloperTurnedManager or for other techies who suddenly find themselves going to lots of meetings with people wearing suits. It doesn't go into a lot of depth but it will at least help you to understand the language and concerns of business-school grads.
