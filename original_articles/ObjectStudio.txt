''ObjectStudio from CincomSystemsInc'' is a complete rapid application development suite of tools used by large organizations to design, assemble and maintain complex applications. 

''ObjectStudio'' is unique in providing integrated object modeling (visually defined objects) and object mapping (visually linked objects), within a single development environment. These capabilities enable quick development and maintenance of applications, which allows your organization to better utilize minimal development resources to capture the maximum advantage. 

http://www.cincom.com/objectstudio

A Non-Commercial version is available at
http://www.cincom.com/smalltalk/downloads.html
