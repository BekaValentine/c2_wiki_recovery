The Other Things

To be included, but not (yet) publicly defined or described for specific reasons ThinkingOutLoud.DonaldNoyes 20131121

TheOtherThings are things which are to be included, as present, not delayed challenges. Procrastination is not affordable here, these are things that must be accounted for and tackled, and which require both elaboration and implementation in the immediate, the now; some of these may not be publicized, broadcast, or streamed to other than their participants or partners.
