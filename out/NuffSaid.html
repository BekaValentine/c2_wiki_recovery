Nuff Said

A shortened and signified version of "Enough said." A response signifying that all can appreciate a statement without need for further elaboration.

The phrase was popularized by comic books. StanLee uses it frequently at the end of editorial introductions to Marvel comic book stories. Any red-blooded American boy will recognize this phrase. ['NOTE: AmericanCulturalAssumption, Very Big Time.]

  • According to http://www.straightdope.com/classics/a2_250.html, "nuff said" was part of an abbreviation fad that began in Boston in 1838. See also EtymologyOfOkay.
