Jeff Mckenna

Jeff is one of the legends of Smalltalk. A member of the original Tek Smalltalk team, Jeff used to be a contributor to the SmalltalkReport and its predecessors. Jeff has been consulting on his own for well over a decade. He's also one of the first people to investigate applying XP to large projects...
