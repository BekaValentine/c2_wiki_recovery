Meet Up

http://www.meetup.com/

Meetup is a free service that organizes local gatherings about anything, anywhere.

This is a good site if you want to meet up with people in your area who share an interest in anything: politics, pets, music, etc. and they are open to suggestions for new topics. There are a number of subjects in the Internet & Technology section that might be of interest to readers of this site.

For example:

  • Agile methods http://agile.meetup.com/
  • Scheme/Lisp programming http://scheme.meetup.com/
  • Smalltalk programming http://smalltalk.meetup.com/
  • Java programming http://java.meetup.com/

etc.
