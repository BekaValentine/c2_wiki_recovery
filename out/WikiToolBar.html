Wiki Tool Bar

The RecentChangesJunkie's utility belt:

[Image disabled until reliable host can be found]

  • wRecent - Plain bookmark to RecentChanges (or quickChanges)
  • wGo - Jump to a WikiPage. (See WikiGoBookmarklet)
  • wFind - Search WikiPage titles. (See WikiFindBookmarklet)
  • wEdit - Edit the current page (See EditPageBookmarklet)
  • wDiff - Show the QuickDiff of the current page (See QuickDiffBookmarklet)

(Yes, the names are cryptic. Choose yours however you like. There is a never-ending battle between the excessively verbose and the excessively terse; in this case, I had to let the latter triumph).
