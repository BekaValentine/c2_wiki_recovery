New World Democracy

In the face of the UsaSystemsCatastrophe it seems we'll eventually be in need a replacement for the US and UN. What would a NewWorldDemocracy be like?

  • Open, in the sense of OpenSoftware, to all men and women on earth.
  • True liberty and justice for all, in the sense of Roosevelt's FourFreedoms.
  • Informed by our experience of the WikiNature.

This page is intended to collect ideas about what that might look like.

Possibly/dubiously useful mechanisms:

  • WikiWikiGovernment
  • StoneSociety
  • Others?
