Blog Wiki

BlogWiki is a relatively simple wiki which is intended to be used as a small blog or personal website. It is released under the BSD license.

Example: http://www.danomagnum.com

SourceForge: http://sourceforge.net/projects/blogwiki/

Features:

  • Namespace Support
  • Auto-generated Contents
  • Theme Support
  • Group-based user accounts
  • Decent WikiMarkup
