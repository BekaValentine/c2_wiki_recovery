Wireless Security

WirelessSecurity is a page on security concerns for WirelessDevices. Generally speaking, security concerns that apply to NetworkSecurity are relevant, and in addition, system implementators need to be conscious of additional challenges inherent in various wireless (radio or satellite based) methods.

See also WebApplicationSecurity
