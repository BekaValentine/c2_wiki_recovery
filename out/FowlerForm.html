Fowler Form

This is the pattern form that is loosely used in both AnalysisPatterns and PatternsOfEnterpriseApplicationArchitecture. Very roughly, it amounts to:

  • Title
  • Summary of the pattern (or do this so bad stuff won't happen to you)
  • Discussion of the bad stuff you avoid by doing this pattern, and how this pattern helps you avoid the bad stuff.
