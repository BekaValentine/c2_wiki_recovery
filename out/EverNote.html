Ever Note

A really neat and free program for making notes and for clipping and storing of clipboard contents, together with online storage of data up to 2Gb, and it is free. It is available for a number of platforms.

  • http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Evernote

It is a candidate for IsAnythingBetterThanPaper. Check it out at http://evernote.com

See also NeverNote, RedNotebook
