Peter Molyneux

English VideoGameDesigner. He founded a game company, Bullfrog, in 1987 with Les Edgar and developed the concept of GodGames with the classic Populous. Other successful games he created while at Bullfrog are Syndicate, MagicCarpet, ThemePark and DungeonKeeper. When he started to lose control of his company (sold to ElectronicArts) a few years ago, he left and founded LionheadStudios to develop BlackAndWhite.
