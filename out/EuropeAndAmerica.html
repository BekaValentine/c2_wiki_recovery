Europe And America

Two big, wonderful, amazing places, both of which are the current incarnation of WesternCivilization. Although they share this culture, their citizens have differing approaches to that culture, as well as to economic and governmental integration. Even though they differ, they have more powerful common interests and concerns which can lead them to operate in a spirit of cooperation and agreeable participation, together, as partners in a family of nations.
