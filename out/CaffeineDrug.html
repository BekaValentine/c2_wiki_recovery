The G2 arrest checkpoint is part of the cell cycle after DNA replication but before cell division. Before dividing, the cell is supposed to wait until the genome has finished replicating, and also repair errors that were introduced during replication. If these checks are suppressed and the cell divides with an incomplete or mutated genome, BadThings can happen.
