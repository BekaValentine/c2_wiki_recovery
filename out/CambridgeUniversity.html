Cambridge University

British University which has made a huge contribution throughout its long history to many fields of human endeavour, computer science included. There have been 80 Nobel Prizewinners who attended Cambridge.

http://www.cam.ac.uk/

  • CharlesBabbage
  • AlanTuring
  • MartinRichards

  • ArthurNorman
