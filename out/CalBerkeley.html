Cal Berkeley

The University of California at Berkeley, http://www.berkeley.edu/, is the origin of such "dead-end" technologies as TCP/IP, vi, c shell, BsdOs, Ingres, rogue and, well, tons of other stuff. There are two major products that come out of Berkeley: LSD and UNIX. We don't believe this to be a coincidence. -- Jeremy S. Anderson

The term Cal is most commonly used in a sports context, such as "Cal is the underdog against USC next Saturday", referring to a football game. In academic conversation, people usually say "Berkeley" or maybe "UC Berkeley".
