Simplicity and Clarity
are usually related, since
thinking and
relationships that endure
are usually based
on simply understood and
accepted building blocks.
For this reason,
people prefer order to chaos, and
Since chaos usually exceeds order,
we have much to learn.

-- Donald Noyes

If you build a system on simplicity, it will find its own way, regardless of the existence or degree of order and/or chaos.
