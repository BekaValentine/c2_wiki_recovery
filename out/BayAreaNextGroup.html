Bay Area Next Group

The Bay Area NeXT Group is a longstanding technology support forum now focused on Mac OS X, Mac OS X Server, WebObjects, Legacy technologies, companies and unique individuals. Ten years after its founding, BANG remains an organization with meetings open to an interested public, and meets monthly on the Apple Campus in Cupertino. Topics for meetings vary; BANG tries to present timely information and speakers of pertinent interest to users, developers, and employees of computing and technology environments. Suggestions of meeting topics and ideas for speakers are welcome; please send them to info@bang.org.
