Norm Meyrowitz

Norm was the long time editor of the OOPSLA proceedings. I begged him to edit for me in 1989, and it worked out great. His part of the program was no problem at all.

He also gave a great demo of InterMedia at AppleComputer at around the same time. The MacApp folks were amazed at what he had done with their (at the time formative) work.

-- KentBeck
