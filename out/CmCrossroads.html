Cm Crossroads

CM Crossroads is an online community and resource center for Configuration Management hosting interactive forums, a member contributed file cabinet and a substantial resource links database. http://www.cmcrossroads.com

CM Crossroads also hosts a wiki web (Twiki) called CmWiki where they are building a Configuration Management Body of Knowledge (CMBoK) http://www.cmbok.com or http://www.cmwiki.com
