Wowbagger The Infinitely Prolonged

Bowerick (Wowbagger the Infinitely Prolonged) is a character from TheHitchhikersGuideToTheGalaxy books. After an accident with a few rubber bands, a liquid lunch and a particle accelerator, he became immortal hence the name.
