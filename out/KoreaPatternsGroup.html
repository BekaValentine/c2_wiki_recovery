Korea Patterns Group

The Korea Patterns Group meets weekly on Saturday Afternoons at a Cafe in YeokSam-Dong. The location is relaxing and the conversation is engaging.

The group studies established analysis and design patterns. We also have a plan for Korea Design Pattern Conference.

When:: Every Saturday 1:00pm - 3:00pm
Where:: YeokSam-Dong in Seoul
 Who:: Patterns enthusiasts, any level of experience. There are no requirements for membership and regular attendance is encouraged but not required.
Directions:: Exit at YeokSam Station 4. We will be meeting at Orange Cafe.

Our information can be found at: http://www.designpattern.net.
