Links Wiki

A wiki built with links and organized around a specific theme DoingStuff.DonaldNoyes.20131015

This is a type of wiki, not a specific engine, implementation or executable. The name one calls it is a matter of choice and suitability.

The content of the wiki is made to appear using the links within it.

  • A specific LinksWiki may be specified by a simple prefix:
    • NysLinksWiki
      • ... where this will be located link to be placed here eventually
  • One may even add a suffix:
    • NysLinksWikiIndex
      • ... where this will be located link to be placed here eventually
