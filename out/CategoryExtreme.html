Category Extreme

When adding two WikiWord links to ZeroIsWrong I noticed that there are lots of issues revoling around

  • zero
  • null
  • nothing
  • extreme values
  • borderline cases

Obviously the extremes draw the most attention and the most disagreement.

Examples are

  • ZeroIsWrong
  • WhatIsNull
  • ZeroMeansNull
  • NothingIsAnything.

I think there are lots of examples, but before I tag anything with this category I ask: What do you think? Should this maybe better be called CategoryVoid? Or is the existing CategoryNull enough?
