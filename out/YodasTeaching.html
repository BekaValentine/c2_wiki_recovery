Actually, the way Yoda speaks is based on Yiddish, a dialect of German (with additions from Hebrew and SlavicLanguages) spoken by Jews in Eastern Europe, and still spoken by some ultra-Orthodox (Chareidi) Jews today. The reason Yoda speaks English like that is GeorgeLucas (or maybe someone else who helped write StarWars' script, forgessen :-) ) had encountered a quote from IsaacAsimov saying that it would be impossible for one to view someone speaking English in Yiddish's grammatical order as threatening. This of course was important for creating a strange being that would look like an alien to adults but not be frightening to children.

See also RefactorEnglish, ReadsLikeGerman
