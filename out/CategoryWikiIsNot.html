Category Wiki Is Not

Things that the WikiWikiWeb isn't. Click the title to see what people are claiming. Contrast CategoryWiki.

Do we really need this category? Especially since typing "WikiIsNot" into FindPage seems to return all of the relevant pages? Except for #3 - Changes and #13 - WonderfulBenefitsOfJournals, and in the future other pages about WikiIsNot which do not contain the find page string.

The creation of this category is intended as an EditHint.

  • It is a GoodThing to define the limits of the Wiki by having this category and others like it which are helpful for those concerned with limits, restrictions and prohibitions.
  • It is a BetterThing to define what Wiki is by MakingWikiWhatItOughtToBe.
