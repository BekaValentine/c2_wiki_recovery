Bit Fatigue

BitFatigue is a temporary affliction that occurs after a developers has been reverse engineering the bits of too many hex dumps. Symptoms include mental flashes of "okay . . . wait. . . now is bit 3 a value of 4, do I mask it or check to see if it's equal. . . "

The only cure for BitFatigue is to StepAwayFromTheKeyboard for a while.
