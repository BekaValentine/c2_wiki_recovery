Rdoc Format

RDoc, the native documentation tool of RubyLanguage (though it can also be used for CeeLanguage) uses a simple wiki-like TextFilter/MarkupLanguage to produce pretty text. A subset of RDoc's format is or can be used in most Ruby-driven WikiImplementations and web tools.
