Backlink Lag

A phenomenon on WardsWiki. The database of BackLinks is only updated every 24 hours, so there may be links you can't see yet because they were created after the most recent DB update. When deleting or refactoring pages you need to pause for a day or so before acting on results of searches.

To overcome the lag, you can use the manual TwinPages option. -- FridemarPache
