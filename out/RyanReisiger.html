Ryan Reisiger

Ryan Reisiger is a software developer living in Columbus, OH. He graduated from Bowling Green State University in 2005 with a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science. After graduating he worked as a software developer/tester for Redemtech, Inc., a medium sized company specializing in Technology Change Management (TCM). In 2007 he started working as a software engineer at BMW Financial Services where is focus was ASP.NET and C# development for the dealer facing web applications. He played a significant role in converting the companies classic ASP and VB6 code to the more up to date .NET framework.
