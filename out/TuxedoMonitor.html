Tuxedo Monitor

Tuxedo is the name of a TransactionProcessingMonitor found on the Unix Environment. It was marketed by BEA, who has a JavaTwoEnterpriseEdition product called WebLogic.

TuxedoMonitor was acquired by BEA from NovellCorporation in the mid 90s, and is said to be diffrentiated from the much larger MiddleWare competitor by its capabilities in the DistributedComputing arena.

Jul05 version 9 announcement can be seen at http://www.eweek.com/print_article2/0,1217,a=156066,00.asp
