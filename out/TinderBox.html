Tinder Box

http://www.eastgate.com/tinderbox/

A HyperText tool, notes manager, WebLog publisher and hopefully soon WikiBrowser.

But maybe you were thinking of MozillaTinderbox.

I'm using a demo of Tinderbox on my MacOsx machine and I'm finding it interesting...

It's a pretty good (but not outstanding) tool that does the following:

  • fetches RichSiteSummary feeds
  • creates outlines
  • creates TopicMaps
  • creates simple diagrams
  • manages URLs/Bookmarks/Favorites

Although there seems to be lots more it can do. I'll try to summarize as I go along. -- SeanOleary
