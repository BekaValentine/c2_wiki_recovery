Objc Unit

ObjcUnit is a unit testing framework for ObjectiveCee on MacOsx. It's design was copied from JavaUnit, written by ErichGamma and KentBeck, and then adapted somewhat for Objective-C.

Version 1.2 just released!

See http://oops.se/objcunit or http://oops.se/cgi-bin/wiki?ObjcUnit.
