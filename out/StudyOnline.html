Many colleges have "Distance Learning" programs, complete with pretty certificates. For example, the University of Washington Extension program's web-site is: http://www.outreach.washington.edu/ A typical sequence is 3 one-quarter classes. When taught in a classroom, each class is typically 3 hours of lecture per week, and roughly 6 hours of homework per week, for 10 weeks.

I took the UW Extension's introductory sequence in databases and SQL. (I took the 3 classes in the classroom.) It was very worthwhile. -- JasperPaulsen
