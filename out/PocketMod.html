Pocket Mod

PocketMod - a clever product and folding scheme for printing 8 pages on one side of a page and folding it into a small booklet (2 3/4" x 4 1/4").

"PocketMod: The Free Disposable Personal Organizer"

http://www.pocketmod.com/

Web site includes flash demo to print simple sample PocketMod's.

  • http://www.pocketmod.com/v2/

Free tool to print PDFs in PocketMod format
