Zed Machine

A VirtualMachine used to run InfocomCompany style TextAdventureGames (and also graphical ones, in some cases), such as ZorkGame.

Can also be referred to as either ZeeMachine (depending on your country of origin), or ZorkMachine (which was the original title, but has gone out of fashion).

GrahamNelson's InformLanguage compiler produces code files designed to run on the ZedMachine.
