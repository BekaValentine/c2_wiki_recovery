Science Fiction New Wave

In the sixties and seventies the ScienceFictionGoldenAge authors were joined by a number of interesting new voices. These included HarlanEllison, UrsulaLeGuin, SamuelDelany, RogerZelazny, and various others. ScienceFictionNewWave was distinguished by all kinds of experiments with the form, some successful, some now somewhat painful to read.
