San Francisco Bay Area

The geographical region surrounding San Francisco Bay. It is in California, in the UnitedStatesOfAmerica.

Areas of that region include:

  • SanFrancisco
  • The Peninsula (Palo Alto, Redwood City, all of San Mateo County up to Daly City)
  • South Bay (Mountain View, Sunnyvale, Santa Clara, Cupertino, San Jose, "Silicon Valley")
  • East Bay (Fremont - sorta South Bay, Hayward, San Leandro, Oakland, Alameda, all in Alameda County, Pleasanton, Walnut Creek, Contra Costa County )
  • North Bay (Marin, Sonoma, Solano counties)
