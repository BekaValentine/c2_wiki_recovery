Manufacturing Metaphor

LinesStationsBinsParts is one of approximately eight ProvenSystemMetaphors for UserInterface design.

An AnalogyBetweenProgrammingAndManufacturing is broader. It compares

  • the entire process of designing / compiling / testing / selling a program, with
  • the entire process of designing / making / inspecting / selling a physical product.

CategoryMetaphor
