How To Digitize Whiteboard Content

How can one digitize and preserve WhiteBoard content?

  • ElectronicWhiteboard: Touch sensitive screen + projected digital content.
  • PrintableWhiteboards: Draw, print, scan the printed image. (But: Grainy, and no color)
  • WhiteboardImageCleaningSoftware: DigitalCamera + image cleaning software (WhiteboardPhoto): Draw, photograph, filter image.

See also: ElectronicPaper, LargeLcdDisplay
