--- SeriesActivityStatements

Provide a managerial overview of the work done in a WorkingSeries and information about what is going forward in a it, also how it will benefit those involved.

In these statements you will also find a list of accomplishments to date and a summary of where the Series is headed.

The SeriesOverview is a good source of generic information about the Series and will link to the background reading pages helpful information.

It will also set the scene and explain any concepts in use.
