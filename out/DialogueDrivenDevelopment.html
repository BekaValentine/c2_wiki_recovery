Dialogue Driven Development

Dialogue-Driven Development (d3) is an approach to project management that puts client interaction and communication as the highest priority in a successful project.

Dialogue-Driven Development encourages:

  • Mission statements
  • Goal planning
  • Prototyping
  • Short iterations (1-2 weeks)
  • Constant feedback loops with DomainExperts
  • Collaboration
