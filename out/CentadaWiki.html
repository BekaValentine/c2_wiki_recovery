Centada Wiki

Centada (http://www.centada.com/) is an Internet presence provider in Malaysia. It is a provider of web hosting services, domain names, and email solutions for personal and business websites. Services include Linux and Microsoft hosting, domain registrations, e-commerce hosting, and server hosting.
