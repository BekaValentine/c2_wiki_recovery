Cary Elwes

Welsh actor, best known for role as Wesley/DreadPirateRoberts in ThePrincessBride. Also played Robin Hood in the Mel Brooks spoof "Men In Tights", were he uttered the immortal line:

Unlike some other Robin Hoods, I can speak with an English accent!

Also in "Liar Liar" with Jim Carrey, "Twister" with Bill Paxton & Helen Hunt, and many other films (the list of more than 35 begins in 1979 and continues through present day). Blond, therefore unlikely ever to be a major Hollywood leading man.
