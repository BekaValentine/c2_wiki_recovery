Shoulders Corp

ShouldersCorp is a professional services company founded in 1998 specializing in "extreme software engineering(tm)". Many commercial projects have been completed in 100 days. The largest project supports tens of thousands of companies and all of their employees. The company is involved in a redesign of the core information systems for US Customs.

Based upon prior projects the company has developed reusable Java components which materially speed subsequent projects. See www.shoulderscorp.com for details.
