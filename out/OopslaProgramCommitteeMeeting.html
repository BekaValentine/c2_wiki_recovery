Oopsla Program Committee Meeting

(OopslaProgramChairPatterns)

The purpose of the OopslaProgramCommitteeMeeting is to choose the papers to be published in the proceedings and presented at the conference. The major constraints on the meeting are that it can only take a day or two, and that preparation time is limited.

I used the following patterns in 1989 and really liked them, but I haven't been emulated, near as I can tell:

  • AcceptRejectRecommendation
  • SortedPaperList
  • PaperChampion
