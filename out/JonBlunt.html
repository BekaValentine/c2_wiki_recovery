Jon Blunt

Jon Blunt is the president of Info|Ed a consulting firm specializing in information architecture. Jon can be reached at mailto:jblunt@tiac.net or mailto:jblunt@infoed.com.

Jon is the executive director of TiAC ( The Information Architect's Cooperative), a working group for corporate information architects. The focus of the group is on how to manage the technical, political, and organizational issues of applying new technology across large organizations. Jon is looking for patterns to describe common problems and solutions at this enterprise architecure level.
