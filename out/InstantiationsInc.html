Instantiations Inc

Instantiations Inc is a developer of SoftwareTools for ObjectOrientedProgramming, for JavaLanguage, SmalltalkLanguage and AJAX. Instantiations is based in PortlandOregon.

It produces the successor to IBM VisualAgeSmalltalk system, VaSmalltalk.

Tools include WindowBuilder, RcpDeveloper, WindowTester, CodePro and WidgetKit.

It is a member of the EclipseFoundation, producing the Java EclipseIde descended from IBM VisualAge.
