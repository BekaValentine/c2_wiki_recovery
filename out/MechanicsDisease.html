Mechanics Disease

Problems which when taken to the mechanic mysteriously disappear, only to reappear as soon as the mechanic is out of sight.

Probably related to the inverse phenomenon, DemoMeltdown.

Also called veterinarian's disease. There is a physiological basis: scared animals pump adrenaline that masks subtle symptoms.

Advice to programmers: stop scaring your programs...
