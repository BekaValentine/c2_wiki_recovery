Ronald Van Aken

Ronald Van Aken is a professional software developer. He is primarily active within the role of architect and project manager for innovative projects. He encourages the use of design/architectural patterns and test driven development and continuously seeks ways on how to improve and apply these techniques within a pragmatic environment. He has published numerous technical articles for Dutch magazines like Java magazine. His interests lie in the area of SOA, BPM and Rich Internet applications. see http://www.linkedin.com/in/ronaldvanaken and http://mymobilethoughts.com
