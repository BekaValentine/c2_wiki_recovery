Squeak Alice

No relation to AliceLanguage. The Alice Development Kit is an authoring environment for 3D storytelling. It is designed to allow the novice programmer, or even the non-programmer, the ability to create fully interactive and responsive worlds.

Squeak-Alice is a 3D / Animation retained-mode framework to run these 3D worlds in Squeak. It has no authoring tools and relies heavily on Alice.

As a 3D engine, Squeak Alice is a mix of extremely good and extremely pathetic. It's extremely good because it relies on the MorphicFramework to be object-oriented, which just about no other 3D engine is. It's extremely pathetic because it has just about nothing that a sophisticated 3D engine should have, such as particles, transparency, or even fog!

The pink bunny demo alone is sufficiently cute to turn people off of it forever.
