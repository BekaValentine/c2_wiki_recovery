Association Links

Grouping LinkTypes ThinkingOutLoud.DonaldNoyes.20130429

It may be a good idea to simplify the associations and relationships links have on, from, or to other pages and objects by referring to them as AssociationLinks.

Included (having references to):

  • BackLinks
  • ForwardLinks
  • CategoryLinks
  • EmailLinks
  • ExternalLinks
    • WebPages
    • other Wikis
      • SisterSites
      • Federations
  • Persons
  • Groups, Companies, GeographicalAreas
  • Polities

In organizing the structure of information presented on WikiPages, it might be well to include these on or within a SubPage
