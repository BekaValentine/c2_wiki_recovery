Harry Browne

American Economist, political scholar, and Libertarian candidate for President (1996, 2000)

Author of numerous books, including How I Found Freedom in an Unfree World (1973, 1998) [ISBN 0965603679], Why Government Doesn't Work (1995) [ISBN 0965603601], The Great Libertarian Offer (2000) [ISBN 0965603695], and Fail-Safe Investing (2000) [ISBN 031226321X]

His website includes a lot of very insightful (IMO) articles about the policies of the US government. It can be found at http://www.harrybrowne.org/.
