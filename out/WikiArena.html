Wiki Arena

The WikiArena is a place for people who insist on engaging in FlameWars on Wiki to do so, without bothering the rest of us. If WikiArena gets too full or has too much traffic (or too many changes), individual FlameWars might get their own WikiArena page (ie WikiArenaRichardVsCostin, WikiArenaTwoSeventeenVsWikiBlackList, WikiArenaRobertVsTheWorld).

Old rubbish, er, content may be purged from this page at a regular basis. By default, the contents of this page are OffTopic; once tempers cool no need to preserve it.

No need for the material here to have too much surrounding context, or be in order. This is the parking lot to keep the brawls out of the bar.

Feel free to move FlameWars from other pages here.

Please don't delete this page; it serves a purpose.
