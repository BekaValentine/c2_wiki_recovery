Can Do

Expression used when a task is assigned, usually given as a sign of cooperation or pride in one's ability to do something that others may find difficult, or be unwilling to do. Usually expressed as receiving priority over other tasks.

Is this the same as the "Yes, sir" attitude?

  • CanDo is always a positive attitutude, but the other is not necessarily so.
