Designing An Authentication System

Designing An Authentication System (http://web.mit.edu/kerberos/www/dialogue.html) tells the story of the creation of the SystemMetaphor for Kerberos (ThreeLineSystem). Some other authentication system metaphors are:

Coke machine
There's a very secure place that collects access tokens, and a less secure place that distributes goodies once the TokenCollector gives the OK.
LockoutBoard in an industrial plant
All the padlocks have to be removed before the switch can be thrown.
CreditCardTerminal
A token is sent to a central system and validated before you can finish the transaction.
MarxBrothers movie
You have to say "swordfish" to get into the room (from HorseFeathers).
