Xp Ef

An abbreviation for XpEvaluationFramework. It's pronounced 'ekspef'. XP:EF consists of

  • XP-cf (context factors)
  • XP-am (adherence metrics - pronounced 'ekspam')
  • XP-om (outcome metrics - pronounced 'ekspom')

The idea is present data in consistent yet extendable way to benefit both practitioners and researchers.
