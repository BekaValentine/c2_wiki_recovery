You And Your Research

YouAndYourResearch is a transcription of a talk RichardWesleyHamming gave at a seminar in the Bell Communications Research Colloquia series. Recommended reading, it is Hamming's personal observations and insightful comments on doing high quality research based on many years of working with people like RichardFeynman, ClaudeShannon and others of that calibre.
