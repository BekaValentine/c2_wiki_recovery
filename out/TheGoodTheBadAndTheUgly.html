The Good The Bad And The Ugly

There are so many books that talk about (or reference) Patterns coming out now. I can't seem to buy a technical book without coming across a quote from ChristopherAlexander or a reference to the GOF DesignPatterns.

Not all of these books are good. However, I think that it would be interesting to compile a list of all books that have been written under the influence of Patterns. I have chosen to place these titles in our Wiki BookList.

Keep the list growing, and if you have a review or comment on a listed book, go ahead and put it on its wiki link.
