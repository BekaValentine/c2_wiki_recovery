There Is Always Another Problem

The economy is tanking. You see a better solution to an existing problem but you hesitate. The current solution is generating revenue for you or your company.

Therefore

Remind yourself that ThereIsAlwaysAnotherProblem and that ThereIsAlwaysDemandForProblemSolvers. You identify yourself as someone who solves problems by solving them, not by prolonging them.
