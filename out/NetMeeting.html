Net Meeting

Microsoft NetMeeting allows people to share their desktops over the Internet. It also allows you to chat and video conference. It can be used for VirtualPairProgramming. It seems like a good way to PropagateXp. -- MartijnMeijering

There are other simular tools, you might want to have a look at if you're not running MicrosoftWindows.

See also VirtualNetworkComputing, GnomeMeeting
