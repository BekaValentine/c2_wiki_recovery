Sometimes Slave Oriented Programming involves threats and can seem like bondage and discipline but in fact usually the threats are left out since the slave already listens to you even without the threat.

pro makeDirectoryOrElseYouWillBeUnpluggedAndKilled(path: astr);
b

We can shorten this to just:

pro makeDir(path: astr);
b
// here we make a directory called "path".
e;

There are no "thank yous" or "pretty please". JustDoIt. Precise. To the point.

The asm token says INSERT ASSEMBLY NOW. No questions asked. No games. No fancy pretty pleases with a cherry on top.
