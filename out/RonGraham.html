Ron Graham

Mathematician formally at Lucent and BellLabs, now Chief Scientist at the California Institute for Telecommunication and Information Technology (also known as Cal-(IT)^2), and Irwin and Joan Jacobs Professor at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering of the University of California, San Diego (UCSD).

Former president of the International Jugglers Association, competition level trampolinist, and all round nice chap.

More can be found at

I have to ask - can one juggle while trampolining?

The only way I've been able to do so is by synchronising the two so that all throw and catch pairs happen near the top of the bounce. Trying to do anything under acceleration is extremely difficult. To the best of my knowledge, no one has juggled in arbitrary phase to their bouncing.
