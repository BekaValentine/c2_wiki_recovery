Wanted Page

A page-tag indicating the need for a page elaborating upon the title.

It accepts the concept of NotBuiltInOneDay, for associated topics generated from pages alreading existing, utilizing the BackLink principle of page-collections.

It is a page-list generated by back-links, constructed as a WantedChanges page.

A way of indicating subjects not yet included and also as a plan-of-action.
