Eric Gill

Eric Gill: artist and sculptor, author of AnEssayOnTypography, designer of GillSans and the seriph font Perpetua, carver of Westminster Cathedral in LondonTown's stations of the cross.

Gill belonged to the arts and crafts tradition begun by WilliamMorris and JohnRuskin.

Gill was also a friend of GkChesterton. As such the ideas he expressed probably arise from Distributism. --RaySchneider

Would it be fairer to say that Distributism arose from the ideas he (and Chesterton, and others) expressed?
