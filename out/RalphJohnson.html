Ralph Johnson

http://st-www.cs.uiuc.edu/users/johnson Ralph is interested in all kinds of patterns.

He wrote ActiveObjectModel and TransactionsAndAccounts and is still working on related topics. Please send comments on them to mailto:johnson@cs.uiuc.edu, or leave comments in-line.

He is also working on the PatternStoriesWiki and would like to promote the OnLineComputerMuseum.
