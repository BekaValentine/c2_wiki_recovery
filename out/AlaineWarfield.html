Alaine Warfield

Alaine Warfield is an Oracle and Sql Server DBA and Performance Engineer at Kronos, Inc. in Beaverton, OR.

She got her start at Tektronix, ending up at TekLabs before it got disbanded.

She then spent 12 years at Mentor Graphics working on Analog Simulation tools.

She volunteers her time playing harp to children and the elderly at hospitals and hospice and raises Guide Dog puppies for the blind.
