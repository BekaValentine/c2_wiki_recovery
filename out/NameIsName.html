Name Is Name

Adam Carolla says that when someone says NameIsName, that's code for "Name is an A-hole," because they can't think of anything nice to say.

"Jimmy's really the nicest guy you'll ever meet; he'll buy you a Christmas present based on something he remembers overhearing in June. Osvaldo's the greatest cook you'll ever meet, and he's got a great family. Chris is the most competent carpenter I've ever worked with. Ray? Well, Ray is Ray. He does his own thing."
