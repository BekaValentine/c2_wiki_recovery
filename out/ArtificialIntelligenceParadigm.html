Artificial Intelligence Paradigm

There are many simple structures that go into ArtificialIntelligenceAlgorithms. For example, the ThreeLawsOfRobotics could be implemented by integrating a SearchAlgorithm and a SubsumptionNetwork for input and output. The SubsumptionNetwork could offer multiple possible routes to take, and the SearchAlgorithm would gauge which one would score the highest against the ThreeLawsOfRobotics.
