Every Buddy

http://www.everybuddy.com/

Supports:

  • ICQ
  • AIM
  • MSN
  • Yahoo!

From the site -

Everybuddy has support for AIM, ICQ, MSN, Yahoo! and Jabber chat programs. It also has file transfer between Everybuddy users, and between users of Everybuddy and users of the official MSN and Yahoo! clients.

Everybuddy is developed by a team of volunteers under the conditions of the GNU General Public licence. Not only can you download the program itself for free, you can also download and redistribute the source code from which it is built. You are welcome to help us to improve it, and give it to anyone you like.
