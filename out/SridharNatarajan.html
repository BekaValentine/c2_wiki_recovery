Sridhar Natarajan

Sridhar, AreYouThere? Last edit 2002-03-18, this note added 2005-02-19

Sridhar has 9 years experience in architecting and developing software for the healthcare industry. He developed Electronic Medical Records software using wireless and pen computing technologies at PenKnowledge and Digital Medical Systems. Recently, he developed a security infrastructure for delivering healthcare applications over the internet with focus on HIPAA and Public Key Infrastructure(PKI) at Phyve. He holds a Masters degree in Computer Science from University of Alabama at Birmingham and a Bachelors degree in Computer Science from BITS Pilani, India
