Ad Logicam

AdLogicam is one of the FallaciousArguments where it is asserted that because an argument contains a fallacy, the conclusion must be false.

See also http://www.fallacyfiles.org/fallfall.html

The rest of this page is no longer talking about AdLogicam ...

Some teminology. Given a statement P-->Q:

  • P is the antecedent
  • Q is the consequent
  • The inverse is ~P-->~Q
  • The converse is Q-->P
  • The contrapositive is ~Q-->~P
  • P->Q <=> ~Q->~P
  • P->Q <=/=> Q-> P

None of this has anything to do with AdLogicam.
