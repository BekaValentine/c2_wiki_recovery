Grand Meeting Xp

A convivial tradition of the FrenchXpCommunity. Large meeting of XP practitioners and onlookers, held at a restaurant. (ThereMustBeFood, especially in France, where in addition ThereMustBeWine.)

  • The first GrandMeetingXp was held on June 19th, 2001.
  • The second GrandMeetingXp will be held late september-early october 2003.

Please visit the Wiki and sign up if you're going to be in the area around that time:
