Bullshit Bingo

It's also called BuzzwordBingo. Everyone gets a bingo card full of buzzwords. As the PointyHairedBoss rambles on about StrategicDirection... ThinkingOutOfTheBox..., etc., you check off the ones you have. The first person to finish their card wins.

You have to cry out the word BULLSHIT to win the match...
