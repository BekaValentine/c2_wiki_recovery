Sounds like you're talking about ontologies. The W3C is doing things in this direction for the SemanticWeb. One of those things is the WebOntologyLanguage. With regards to vocabulary analysis and classification, take a look at WordNet, at http://www.cogsci.princeton.edu/~wn/ . There's a whole world of research going on this direction right now. See also ResourceDescriptionFramework.
