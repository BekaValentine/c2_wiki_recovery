Wiki Web Dot Com

A company that sells a commercial wiki product. They have announced that they are terminating their free WikiFarm on June 30, 2001. http://www.wikiweb.com/

The site is written in 100% Smalltalk and is powered by an Oracle database. The product works with several databases is also written in 100% Smalltalk (IBM VisualAge Smalltalk 5.0).
