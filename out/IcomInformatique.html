Icom Informatique

Icom Informatique develops and sells software products which connect PCs to mainframes in various configurations, including through intranet and internet connections.

Its product line includes Winsurf Mainframe Access, a "Web-to-host" product.

It is based in Charenton, France (close to Paris)
