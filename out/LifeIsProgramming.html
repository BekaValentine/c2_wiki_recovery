Life Is Programming

LifeIsProgramming is when programming is the most salient feature of your life.

There are a variety of causes:

  • You're working on an extremely exciting project.
  • You're defining yourself by what you do (i.e. you link project success with personal worth).
  • You haven't had a taste of how good a balanced life can be. SteveMaguire talks about this in DebuggingTheDevelopmentProcess.
  • You work for a company that thinks developers working eighty-hour weeks are better for the product than those that work FortyHourWeeks.
