Harry Porters Relay Computer

HarryPorter, a professor at PortlandStateUniversity, has constructed a simple microprocessor (er... macroprocessor?) out of around 400 relays!

  • http://web.cecs.pdx.edu/~harry/Relay/

Of note: WardCunningham wrote a little simulator for it in Perl:
