Agile Business Conference 2004

  1. & 22 October, London

Where Agile Business Meets Agile Development

As the stories of failed projects continue, we strive to find better ways to deliver projects with tangible benefits in a timely manner.

This two-day conference will address how IT can deliver real business benefit fast in an ever-changing world.

Plenary Sessions:

  • David Taylor
  • Ken Schwaber
  • Barry Fazackerley
  • John Seddon
  • Lowell Lindstrom
