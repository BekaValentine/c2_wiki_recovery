Management By Kicking Ass

For those interested in the motivational theory behind this and another contrasting approach:

  • http://www.netmba.com/mgmt/ob/motivation/mcgregor/

The belief that the only way to motivate people to do what you want them to do is by the use of physical or verbal threats. Also contains the belief that anyone (over the age of about 4) actually ever does what you want them to do or that you can or need to in fact motivate people. -- 2003.24.12 PhilStubbington

My experience is that such shops end up wasting all their time covering their ass and are afraid to innovate or take potentially good risks.
