Urbana Patterns Group

There is a patterns study group that meets weekly in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign. In fact, we study more than just patterns, but we study a lot of patterns. Non-university people are welcome. See http://wiki.cs.uiuc.edu/SAG/Weekly+SAG+seminar (BrokenLink 20070524)
