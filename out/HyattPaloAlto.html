Hyatt Palo Alto

Site of the first Macintosh Consortium meetings, the Hyatt Palo Alto (on El Camino) was once a center of object-oriented energy. Containing a pool, a club, a bar, a restaurant, and easily confused with HyattRickeys (across the street), it compared to ZottsAlpineBeerGarden as a place for early OOers to see and be seen.
