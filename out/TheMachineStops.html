Incidentally, the reason you won't find it at ProjectGutenberg is that it appears to be still under copyright. If the notations at the above site are correct, the story was written and published in 1909, but copyright was last asserted (or renewed) in 1947. I'm neither a lawyer nor a copyright expert, but I think this puts it in the "95 years or 2048, whichever comes last" rule. -- TimLesher
