Extreme Programming Maturity Model

The ExtremeProgrammingMaturityModel:

  • Level -2: You couldn't care because you've never heard of it.
  • Level -1: You are considering XP and beginning to care about your level of attainment.
  • Level 0: You work according to the practices in ExtremeProgrammingExplained.
  • Level 1: You have evolved away from those practices based on your experience.
  • Level 2: You no longer care what level you have achieved.
