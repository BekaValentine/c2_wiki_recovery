If one visits questionable sites, the likelihood of encounters with such agents is magnified.

Here, on this wiki, the wikizens police such activity and have devised a number of ways of handling malcontents.

The need for such activity indicates a disharmony of purposes. A healthy wiki has few occurances of either malicious and hostile behavior and is evidenced by the presence of PositiveDialogue.
