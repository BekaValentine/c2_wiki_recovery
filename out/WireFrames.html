Wire Frames

Wireframes are a skeletal prototype of an application. Each page in the application is represented as a page in the wireframe. The wireframe is all text and includes only two sections: the RESPONSIBILITIES tell what the page does. The LINKS are the exit points from that application page to another. They correspond directly with the RESPONSIBILITIES and XFAs of FuseDocs.

It's like stack of CRC cards only it's an interactive web app
