Brain Fsck

A politer way to reference BrainfuckLanguage. Some of us don't like using the F-word, but wish make reference to the language.

This euphemism also affords some cheap allusions to fsck (tool for checking/repairing *nix file system(s)). For example:

  • After too much exposure to BrainfuckLanguage, you may feel the need to fsck your brain.
  • Some might argue that BrainfuckLanguage programs look not dissimilar to the contents of a file system that fsck failed to repair.
