John Mc Gregor

JohnMcGregor is a a Computer Science professor at ClemsonUniversity. He is well-known for his work on testing object-oriented systems. His recent work in patterns includes a two-day course that introduces basic DesignPatterns and presents patterns as a technique for describing many facets of development. He is doing research in testing patterns,and into how patterns might be used for early (design-time) system validation.

You can reach him at mailto:johnmc@cs.clemson.edu.
