What is WikLink?

WikLink is a small web application for a community of users who want to share their bookmarks online. Anyone can freely edit or modify the links.

This may sound rather simplistic, but a WikLink is a nice way to collaborate on the Web. It is also inspired by WikiWikiWeb for its philosophy and TeleFerique for the Default aesthetic.

A lot a nice features:

  • import/export bookmarks
  • RssFeeds
  • most popular, most active ...
  • XHTMLValid
  • ...

URL: http://www.erational.org/software/wiklink/
