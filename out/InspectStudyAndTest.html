Inspect Study And Test

Three ways one can validate explanation and classification artifacts:

  • Rather than becoming a parrot, mindlessly repeating mantras, established by others as substitutes for critical thinking, one should InspectStudyAndTest.
  • Inspect
    • Determine
      • if the name used is adequate, if not exact in its representation of the artifact
      • unique as well common attributes of the artifact
  • Study
    • relationships existing between it and other artifacts
  • Test
    • operational and procedural aspects of the artifact using specific and reasonable test methods
