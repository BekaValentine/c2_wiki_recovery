Daynote Gang

An informal name for an informal grouping of authors and computer consultants (and later some significant others) with journal websites that tended to correspond and crosslink a lot. To consolidate links and make things easier for regular readers to make the rounds, various URL lists were compiled, later a domained portal.

See portal site(s):

  • http://www.daynotes.org (also daynotes.net and daynotes.com)
