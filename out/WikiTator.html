Wiki Tator

WikiTator: Like a dictator: A Wiki user who thinks that it is their right and duty to control this entire Wiki. They impose their will on others, even though others/majority do not agree.

Reminder:

  • Wiki is a collective, a living and ever evolving community, whose needs, amusement, and wishes are ever changing like the air we breathe.

Not to be confused with a WikiPotato.
