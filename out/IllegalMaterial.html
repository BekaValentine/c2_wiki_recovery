Illegal Material

There may be some material which is illegal on Wiki.

The definition of IllegalMaterial in the context of Wiki is that material which is not allowed on the server due to laws where the server resides.

Examples include:

  • CopyRight - http://www.wiredsafety.org/law/copyrights/
    • In general, removal upon notification is a legitimate defense. (In other words, if someone posts infringing material here and Ward removes it upon notification, that ends the matter).
  • Child pornography; illegal throughout the UnitedStates.
    • In some parts of the United States, other forms of pornography is illegal. The state of Oregon, where WardsWiki is hosted, has perhaps the most liberal free speech laws in the United States.
