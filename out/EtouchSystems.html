Etouch Systems

eTouch Systems Corp is a solutions company based out of Fremont, California. It offers content-centric solutions and services.Its main product offerings are eTouch SamePage (check EtouchSamePage) and eTouch CMS.

eTouch SamePage is an enterprise-class wiki-based collaboration solution built on the standard J2EE platform.It provides a number of advanced features like WYSIWYG editing,project management, permissioning, administration and support for multiple domains.

eTouch CMS is a comprehensive content management solution to simplify and improve the process of creating, managing, reusing, and publishing web content.

eTouch Systems Corp was founded in 1998.
