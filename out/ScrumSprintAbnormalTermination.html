Scrum Sprint Abnormal Termination

The premature cancellation of a ScrumSprint, due to one of several causes:

  • Certainty that the SprintGoal cannot be achieved.
  • Fundamental and urgent external changes that invalidate the SprintGoal or the bulk of the functionality.
  • Failure of the ScrumProcess implementation for reasons such as:
    • fundamental team conflict
    • inability to engage ProductOwner to get answers for the team

ScrumSprintAbnormalTermination is a rare act, not to be taken lightly.
