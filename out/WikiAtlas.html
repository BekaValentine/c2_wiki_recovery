Wiki Atlas

A project to make the Wiki universe more visible. A mapping project, utilizing WikiCategories, RoadMaps, and other classification and indexing schemes to expose more of the WikiIceberg to visitors and change WikiReadingHabits. Should include a set of visual representations as in a highway atlas.

Consider the MeatBall:TourBusStop as one of these projects.

The WikiPedia is discussing a geographical WikiAtlas: http://meta.wikipedia.org/wiki/Talk:Wikipediatlas

How would or could OneBigWiki be apart of the effort? http://www.worldwidewiki.net/wiki/OneBigWiki
