Employee Satisfaction Study

The EBE group of University of Calgary carried out a world-wide study of the effects of agile methods on the lives and views of agile team members. The online survey asked for views on various job satisfaction factors as well as some demographic information. The researchers responsible for this project at University of Calgary are:

  • GrigoriMelnik
  • FrankMaurer
