Lord Byron

Very famous and well regarded poet.The embodiment of the Romanticist ideal; very promiscuous, possibly had bipolar disorder, national hero in Greece (despite the fact that he is English). Father of Lady Ada Lovelace (a.k.a. AugustaAdaByron), world's first programmer.

(Ada Lovelace's mother discouraged her from studying the Humanities, for fear she would turn out like her father)
