Wishing Makes It So

For a long time I've characterized the situation where someone comes in and demands that Engineering develop a product that is like all the best features of the others in the market but cheaper and smaller and better on an impossible schedule with too little funds. WishingMakesItSo is one of the OrganizationalPathologies. The problem is that the reality is that WishingDoesntMakeItSo. This is an instance of ItDoesntDepend--Ray Schneider
