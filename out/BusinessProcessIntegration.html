Business Process Integration

For BPI see the better known term of BusinessProcessManagement (BPM).

Use this term only if you are more concern about the integration of new processes, over the ChangeManagement of one set of integrated processes to another.

  • An example being when one is involved in the introduction of new DisruptiveTechnology into SupplyChainManagement (e.g. use of RadioFrequencyIdTags).
