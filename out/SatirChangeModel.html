Satir Change Model

Virginia Satir, a pioneer of family therapy, developed a model of how individuals experience change

The Satir Change Model says that as we cope with unexpected or significant change

  • we predictably move through four stages:
    • Late Status Quo
    • Chaos
    • Practice and Integration
    • New Status Quo
