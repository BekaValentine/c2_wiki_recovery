Drag And Select Option

Dragging with the right mousekey down, could simplify some Standard Wiki Features in a WysiwygWiki. On mousekey up a selection list pops up with:

  • DragAndFormatOption
  • DragAndListOption
  • DragAndLinkOption
  • DragAndRefactorOption
  • ...

The list is ordered by increasing complexity of the involved editing functions.

Motivation:

  • less typing
  • UnifiedUserInterfaceForWysiwygWiki
