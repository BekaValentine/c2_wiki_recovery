Nested Thread Mode Example

This is the same example sequence given on InterleavedParallelThreadMode, but using NestedThreadMode instead of regular ThreadMode and including a summary.

Stage one

Cats are nice. I don't like dogs. Oxygen is good. -- Joe'Bloggs

Stage two

Cats are nice. I don't like dogs. Oxygen is good. -- Joe'Bloggs

  • I don't agree with you about cats. They're an abomination from hell. Also, dogs are the nicest animals on the planet. And oxygen is overrated. -- FredBlaggs

Stage three-A: preparing to post his reply, JoeBloggs splits FredBlaggs' last comment across multiple points.

Cats are nice. I don't like dogs. Oxygen is good. -- Joe'Bloggs

  • I don't agree with you about cats. -- FredBlaggs
  • Cats are an abomination from hell. -- FredBlaggs
  • Dogs are the nicest animals on the planet. -- FredBlaggs
  • Oxygen is overrated. -- FredBlaggs

Stage three-B: JoeBloggs posts his reply to each of FredBlaggs' points separately

Cats are nice. I don't like dogs. Oxygen is good. -- Joe'Bloggs

  • I don't agree with you about cats. -- FredBlaggs
  • How dare you! Cats are lovely, lovely, lovely. -- JB
  • Cats are an abomination from hell. -- FredBlaggs
  • Blasphemy! -- JB
  • Dogs are the nicest animals on the planet. -- FredBlaggs
  • Apart from dogs. And weasels. And everything else. -- JB
  • Oxygen is overrated. -- FredBlaggs
  • Provided you don't care about breathing, anyway. -- JB

Stage four

Cats are nice. I don't like dogs. Oxygen is good. -- Joe'Bloggs

  • I don't agree with you about cats. -- FredBlaggs
  • How dare you! Cats are lovely, lovely, lovely. -- JB
  • What's so great about cats, then? I think they're boring. -- EthelFnord
  • Cats are an abomination from hell. -- FredBlaggs
  • Blasphemy! -- JB
  • There's nothing blasphemous about that. -- EthelFnord
  • Dogs are the nicest animals on the planet. -- FredBlaggs
  • Apart from dogs. And weasels. And everything else. -- JB
  • I don't understand. Joe, do you like weasels or not? JaneDoe
  • Oxygen is overrated. -- FredBlaggs
  • Provided you don't care about breathing, anyway. -- JB
  • Perhaps Fred breathes something other than oxygen? -- EthelFnord

Stage Five - Summary

  • Dogs, Cats, and Weasals are animals. So are people.
  • Some people like them, others are indifferent or don't like them.
  • Oxygen is one of the essential elements required by animals.
  • Some of you are ok with this, some of you are not.
