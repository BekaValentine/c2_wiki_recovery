Robert Murray

Rob Murray (rmurray@cox.net) manages a software development team at First American Corporation, a real estate corporation in Santa Ana, California. In an earlier lifetime he worked (with BjarneStroustrup, BarbaraMoo, and AndrewKoenig ) on C++ compilers, code generators, and other development tools at Bell Labs. He was the founding Editor of The C++ Report magazine, and the author of the book C++ Strategies and Tactics (Addison-Wesley 1993).

Rob is very interested in both language development and tools, as well as lightweight development processes such as XP. He's particularly looking for ways to successfully apply lightweight processes in the professional services arena.
