Make Love Not War

Popular culture suggests the best way to help a troubled culture is to drop bread and bombs on it. In no particular order.

Popular culture suggests the best way to guarantee our freedom is to monitor and control it. And if you don't like that, GetOverIt.

We in WikiDom think there are better ways to promote gentleness and goodness than this. We want SchoolsForCivilization. We want to EndTheAssCovering.

Make love and not war! 'Cause we don't need no trouble.-- BobMarley

So do your part and ensure that your make file has a target named "love".
