is another DramaticIdentity, the FriendlyPeerContributor appends to another author's signature, if s/he has done some minor corrections of typos, like changeing "athiest" into "atheist", in their contribution. After having it read, the spellchecked contributor can accept, restore or delete the change.

On the other hand if used in the list of addressees in an AddressedAndSignedMessage, it is a call for help to correct one's own typos, should there be any.
