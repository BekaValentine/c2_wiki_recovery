Tools Producing Artifact

Tools skillfully used produce Artifacts ThinkingOutLoud.DonaldNoyes.20071010.20121201

Tools are most effective when employed by one skilled in their use, they then can be used to "produce" valuable artifacts.

WeNeedExamples;

  • Consider a "skilled sculptor" with hammer and chisel and a block of stone as aids in understanding the relationship
    • The skill in the handling of tools, is developed, not inherent
    • The artifact emerges as the sculptor shapes it, by removal of fragments of stone, one by one, the right amount from the right place
    • The MentalImage of what it is to become emerges from this effort, for all to see
