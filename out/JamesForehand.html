James Forehand

James, AreYouThere? OrphanPage, last edit 2000-12-30 (addition of category)

James Forehand is a Senior Software Engineer for Rockwell Collins in Melbourne, Florida. Currently he is working on Mult-mode Radios (ILS, MLS, GPS) for commercial aviation. James has worked for multiple government contractors including McDonnell Douglas and Grumman in the past is happy to be working in the commercial world. He has worked in simulation and on the international space station and lived in Texas, Georgia, New York, and Florida.

James has a bachelors degree in Mathematics and Computer Science from SUNY College at Brockport and is currently working on his Masters of Computer Science at the Florida Institute of Technology in Melbourne.
