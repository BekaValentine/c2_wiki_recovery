Iota And Jot

Iota and Jot are two minimal FunctionalProgrammingLanguages (combinatorial, rather) aiming to be TuringComplete with the minimal number of symbols. Each of them uses only two symbols (Iota: i and *; Jot: 1 and 0). Jot makes it particularly easy to obtain the GoedelNumbering for a program.

  • http://ling.ucsd.edu/~barker/Iota/

Zot is an extension of Jot which allows input and output.

  • http://ling.ucsd.edu/~barker/Iota/zot.html
