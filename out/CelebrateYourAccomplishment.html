Celebrate Your Accomplishment

Parties are great fun! and if you hold a little party to Celebrate Your Accomplishment then everyone will remember and you won't have to DoNotWorryAboutTheCredit. --RaySchneider

One way of building a sense of community at work (and increasing employee morale thereby) is to celebrate important accomplishments and milestones in the lives of co-workers and colleagues.
