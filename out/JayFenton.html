Jay Fenton

Jay has been hangin' round the leading edge of interactive software for quite a while. He did more than any individual to bring multimedia to the Mac (and thence to the PC). His projects include:

  • MusicWorks
  • VideoWorks
  • PlayGround

He has worked at:

  • MacroMind
  • Vivariam
  • MegaloMedia
  • ElectricCommunities

He is also an arcade game programmer of note.

A description of some of the projects he's worked on can be found at http://www.communities.com/people/jay/ (BrokenLink 2006-02-27)
