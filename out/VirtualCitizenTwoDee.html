Virtual Citizen Two Dee

A kind of GlobalSupercomputer, perhaps using LinuxClustering, where the community can freely upload and download RealTime a kind of AbbreviatedAsciiUml using Mobile or more FeatureRich devices, to share ideas, information and even implement a virtual NeighborhoodWatch, allowing Whistleblowing and LawfulIntercept but generally a kind of OpenSource provision for education and the upholding of Democracy. Depicting and simulating the intricacies of relevant Financial, Social and Political Networks and Systems, yet specific enough to identify real problems and/or opportunities, the FrontPage could look something like:

  • EconomicsEngine
  • SocialEngine
  • PoliticalEngine

etc.

The pages could be hyperlinked such that users can continuously view, edit and modify content interactively, navigating as effortlessly as possible. Alerts could be sent automatically on an OptIn basis, and uploading could be done by SMS, WML or HTML. PayPerUse versions could be developed later on on this foundation, but in an Era where whole Industries are evaporating and abberations like TrafficHacking are being aimed with DeadlyForce at innocent pedestrians, the community should take CounterMeasures using available technology.

Simulating economic systems is something I've been playing with, in my spare time, for years. It's an amusing diversion. However...
