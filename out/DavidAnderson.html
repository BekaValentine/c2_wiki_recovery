David Anderson

David J. Anderson was a member of the original FDD team in Singapore working with Steve Palmer, Jeff De Luca and Peter Coad. He now works at Corbis, having previously worked at Microsoft guiding the development of the MSF for CMMI process improvement. He has run more than 20 FeatureDrivenDevelopment (FDD) projects in the last 5 years within Fortune 100 companies - Sprint and Motorola.
