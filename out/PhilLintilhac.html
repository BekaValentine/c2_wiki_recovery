Phil spent many years as a Botany prof. at UVM (University of Vermont), and established the Lintilhac Foundation and the Lintilhac Foundation Scholarship Challenge to aid UVM.

He credits UVM professors - including Fred Taylor, Jim Marvin, and Tom Sproston - for sparking his interest in plant biology through classes he took as an undergraduate.
