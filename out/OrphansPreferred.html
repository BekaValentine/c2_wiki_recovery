Orphans Preferred

The meme that says (roughly) that stereotypes can be self-perpetuating; for instance, if everyone assumes that all programmers are geeky antisocial males, only geeky antisocial males are likely to (want to) become programmers.

http://www.gamasutra.com/features/19991222/mcconnell_pfv.htm explains that OrphansPreferred was a term in an old pony express advertisement looking for expendable employees.
