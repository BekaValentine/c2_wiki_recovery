A wiki about WikiNode's.

WikiNodes is about connecting related wikis.

The wiki is currently offline, but the concept is easy to explain: Just create a page named "WikiNode" in your wiki and list the links to your neighbor-wikis. Add some introduction to your wiki and list some important pages. See also TourBusStop!! --DanielBruessler
