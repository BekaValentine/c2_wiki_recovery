Bjarne Stroustrup

Inventor of C++ [CeePlusPlus], head of the Large-Scale Programming Research Department at AT&T Research (http://www.research.att.com/info/bs) until 2002, now the College of Engineering Professor in Computer Science at Texas A&M University.

He answers many questions people frequently ask him here: http://www.research.att.com/~bs/bs_faq.html . It's an interesting read.

Author of TheDesignAndEvolutionOfCpp and TheCeePlusPlusProgrammingLanguage
