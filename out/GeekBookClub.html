What's the GeekBookClub?

It's a bunch of nerds reading books, and talking about them afterwards.

Get together with your friends/colleagues, read something, and discuss the valuable insights you gleaned from them. You never know - you might learn something.

This list is intended to be a starting point for thinking about the types of books you might want to read.

Please add your suggestions to the top of the list below, add comments, whatever, but please no Asterix books...
