Cypher Punk

Hackers deriving social background from CyberPunk, technical approach from FreeSoftware, and attitude from CryptoAnarchism. (more description please) JohnGilmore is a notable example. Of course there are many personal opinions within this very vaguely defined group. At one time this all happened on a list at toad.com, but it suffers badly from noise.

The motto: ``cypherpunks write code is a way of keeping away from the pretension and daydreaming that plagues people who only talk about CryptoAnarchism.

One important document is the CypherPunkManifesto.
