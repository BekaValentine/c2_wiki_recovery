Galileo Galilei

GalileoGalilei (1564-1642) a contemporary of Johannes Kepler, was a physicist, philosopher and significant developer of astronomy, mathematics and physics. He was also one of many scientists persecuted by the CatholicChurch. He was under house arrest for years. He authored OnMotion.
