Higher Order Entity Relationship Model

EntityRelationshipModeling by BernhardThalheim

[ISBN 3540654704] It's a consolidation of much of the research work that has gone on since Chen's introduction of the ER model. It's quite powerful, but most of the literature is rather formal and a bit difficult for non-logicians to parse (thus far).
