The Book Of Counted Sorrows

Dean Koontz quotes (his own) poetry from this, though he has never published a collection by this name. In fact, when someone collected all the quotes & issued a smallpress version, he sued to have them all destroyed.

< Need better lawyers! ;->

This book is so sought-after on the Internet that one book searcher has created an entry just to explain that it doesn't exist!
