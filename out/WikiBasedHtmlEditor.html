Wiki Based Html Editor

[From: WhichHtmlEditor]

Such an editor would allow:

  • downloading some interlinked pages of an online WikiForum
  • editing them offline {using WikiLikeWysiwyg and ease of linkgenerating}
  • uploading the changed pages as bulk

An even better way {IMHO} would be the capability to download the complete WikiForum for the first time and keep it offline uptodate by later partially downloading the pages of RecentChanges.

-- FridemarPache
