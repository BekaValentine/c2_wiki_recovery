Programmingin Paris

A common mistyping of ProgramminginPairs (now PairProgramming).

Ah, now that would be a dream job.

"Studies performed by the University of Utah in Salt Lake City have shown that Parisian programmers are 15% slower than two independent programmers from any other European city, but they produce 15% more cheese and much better bread and wine. Since eating and drinking are often many times more satisfying than programming (at least in non-XP environments), this is an impressive result."

YAPC::Europe (that's "Yet Another Perl Conference") 2003 is in Paris! See http://yapc.mongueurs.net/ .

Note that it has nothing to do with Paris Hilton ("not enough memory").
