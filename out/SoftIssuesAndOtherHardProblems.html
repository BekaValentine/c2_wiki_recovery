Soft Issues And Other Hard Problems

Panel: Soft Issues and Other Hard Problems in Software Development at OopslaInSanJose.

Abstract: The key to making object technology work isn't technology. So what is it? This panel will cover a lot of the "soft" issues about dealing with people and teams. It's partly about managing software development, and mostly about doing it.

Moderator & Panelists: PaulChisholm, LarryConstantine, WardCunningham, LukeHohmann and NormKerth
