Structured Programming With Go To Statements

A paper in which Knuth argues that well thought out, rational, controlled use of goto is not entirely harmful.

  • http://pplab.snu.ac.kr/courses/adv_pl05/papers/p261-knuth.pdf
  • Computing Surveys, Vol 6, No 4, December 1974

He does also say that uncontrolled and thoughtless use of GoTo is probably a BadThing.
