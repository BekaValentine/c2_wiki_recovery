Agile Modeling Book

Agile Modeling: Effective Practices for Extreme Programming and the Unified Process by ScottAmbler, RonJeffries

[ISBN:0471202827] In this innovative book, Software Development columnist Scott Ambler presents Agile Modeling (AM)-a technique that he created for modeling XP projects using pieces of the UML and Rational's Unified Process (RUP). Ambler clearly explains AM, and shows readers how to incorporate AM, UML, and RUP into their development projects with the help of numerous case studies integrated throughout the book. -- Amazon Review
