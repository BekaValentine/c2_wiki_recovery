Len Galasso

A renaissance man. Plays keyboards; composes music; writes code; designs circuits; draws pictures; helps his fellow man. Front man for the Jazz Quartet, Breakpoint, inventor of the Secure BIOS (US Pat. 6,148,387) and the Secure Flash Memory (US Pat. 6,598,165), author of the QuerkRtos, designer of the PracticeMan, dedicated husband and father.
