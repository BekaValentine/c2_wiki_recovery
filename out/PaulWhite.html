Paul White

Paul White was a professor at CarletonUniversity for many years before becoming one of the three founders and Vice President of TheObjectPeople. He was vice-president of TheObjectPeople until the year 2000 when the company was bought by BEA. Paul stayed on at BEA for 18 months before resigning. Paul is currently "looking for interesting projects".

Paul is an all around great human bean; he has a generally well-balanced outlook on life and frequently takes the role of "cooler head" and the VoiceOfReason in a heated conversation.
