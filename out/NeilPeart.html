Neil Peart

Neil Peart is the brilliant drummer and wordsmith of the Canadian band Rush. Rush is considered to be a cornerstone of progressive music (by Rush fans) and has reached a very respectable age.

Neil's brilliant drumming relies on a simple trick, that other drummers might someday learn. Before he drums he writes down everything he's going to do on paper. This BigDesignUpFront represents a radical departure from much of drumming theory.

Composing on paper is not an innovation.
