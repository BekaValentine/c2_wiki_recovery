Deeper Meaning Of Liff

"The Deeper Meaning of Liff" (WikiName: DeeperMeaningOfLiff) is DouglasAdams' extended version of his originally small book TheMeaningOfLiff. It includes more definitions, and illustrations, including maps of the town names which have been repurposed.
