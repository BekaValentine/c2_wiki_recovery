Microsoft Press

MicroSoft's imprint. A division of Microsoft, unlike many other "house" imprints like AdobePress and CiscoPress which are joint ventures with large tech publishers like PearsonEducation. Most of its titles are specific to Microsoft products, but it also publishes books of broader interest like ScottMcConnell's CodeComplete. Holds a significant slice of the computer book market all to itself.

Microsoft Press now participates in SafariTechBooksOnline.
