IEEE International Conference on Robotics and Automation, Washington DC, May 2002.

A further report gives more detail of the operation of the FRP code : http://www.cs.jhu.edu/CIRL/publications/pdf/DaHaPeTR2010frp.pdf

This is concerned with the implementation of FunctionalReactiveProgramming using CeePlusPlus. The paper references the work of McNamara and Smaragdakis known here as FunctoidsInCpp.
