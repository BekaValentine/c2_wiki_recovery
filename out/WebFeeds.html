Web Feeds

WebFeeds is Microsoft's cynical and monopolistic attempt to re-brand RSS generally. It can refer to either (or both) AtomSyntax and RssFeeds. This term will be used to focus on the "end user experience".

Or perhaps it can refer to a Well-Formed, Dynamic, rather than Static Presentation which can be Fed or is pushed from a WebAgent, tending to give historic, real-time, informative views of an InformationStream which may have been subscribed to or which has been requested or initiated by a user.
