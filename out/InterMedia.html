Inter Media

InterMedia is a new media and net based learning research lab located at the University of Bergen, Norway. We undertake applied research and consulting in the development and evaluation of media rich computer supported or enabled environments. See http://www.intermedia.uib.no/ for details.

AdrianMiles and KurtGeorgeGjerde are InterMedia staff.

InterMedia should not be confused with IntermediaFromIris, dating from 1985. Further information about IntermediaFromIris can be found in HyperTextHistory, MacApp, and WikiLikeThings.
