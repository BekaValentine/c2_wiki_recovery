Exoft Ware

Exoftware specialises in Agile Software Development Methods, specifically Extreme Programming (XP), a lightweight, flexible yet disciplined approach to developing software.

For over four years, Exoftware has been at the forefront of revolutionising development practices through its bespoke development services, and Agile enablement mentoring services. A leader within the XP community in Europe, Exoftware assist in organising several European Agile SIGs, Agile speciality seminars and are directors of Agile Alliance Europe.

Our team of trainers, mentors and coaches have a wide range of management and development experience to coach teams to success, and have trained hundreds of individuals in a industries as varied as telecoms, financial services and healthcare.
