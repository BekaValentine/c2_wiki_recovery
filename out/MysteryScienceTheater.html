Mystery Science Theater

"Mystery Science Theater 3000": a classic cable show where a guy and his robots were forced to watch bad movies by an evil scientist. Now defunct, but appeared on Comedy Central and the Sci-Fi Channel in the USA.

Often abbreviated "MST3K". Fans of the show call themselves "MSTies".

Also released as a movie of the same name -- which is the only episode I've ever seen, not being in the US. -- GavinLambert
