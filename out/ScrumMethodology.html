See ScrumOverview for a description of what goes on during Scrum.

"Scrum Methodology" may refer to the formalized version of Scrum, available from ADM (AdvancedDevelopmentMethods), which makes Scrum look like a heavyweight process.

So ScrumProcess is the thing you learn from the ScrumBook, and the thing that people usually mean when they say they're "doing Scrum." ScrumMethodology is a formal product available from ADM.

See also http://scrummethodology.com
