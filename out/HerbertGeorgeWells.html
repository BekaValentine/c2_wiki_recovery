Herbert George Wells

H. G. Wells 1866-1946.

British ScienceFiction writer. Particularly known for his "TheTimeMachine" (1895) and "The War of the Worlds" (1898). Less known for his popular accounts of history and science.

(Not to be confused with GeorgeOrwell, author of NineteenEightyFour)
