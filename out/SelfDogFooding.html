is a specific form of dogfooding

  • using your own creations on your own personal site that you depend on, day to day

has several required components

  • the first two of which it shares with dogfooding:
    • active creation (whether code, UX, interactive/visual/graphic design)
    • use of that creation (e.g. by your company)

use

  • you yourself personally using your creation for your own personal uses
    • it's not just part of your day job that you shut off when you go home
  • self identification/dependency
    • using your creation in some manner that represents you, your self
    • part of your primary identity on the web
    • act of creation alters an aspect of the public "self" of the creator
