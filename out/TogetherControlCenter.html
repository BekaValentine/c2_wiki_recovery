Together Control Center

The current Together Flagship product.

Features that this version adds includes full EnterpriseJavaBeans support (Including Deployment) for a variety of popular ApplicationServers.

The 'Control Center' name was introduced with version 4 of the Together product.

Other products in the Together v4 range are TogetherEnterprise and TogetherSolo.

Together Version 4 supports C++, Java and IDL with the a single version of the IDE (and in the same project).

The products are made by TogetherSoft.
