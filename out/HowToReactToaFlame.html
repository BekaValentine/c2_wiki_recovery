How To React Toa Flame

  • Don't honor it with an answer. Leave the flame unanswered or just delete it.
  • Let It Be and life goes on.
  • Try to think positive. Maybe this is an opportunity for improving Self Management.
  • SlowDownToSpeedUp and that is WhyWikiWorks. Give time for the WikiCommunity to observe the behavior of the person(s) initiating the flame, and show others there is a FlyingFingers alternative.
  • What if you want to show a steady content, for example, an account of a true story or personal experience? Time appropriately. And consider OnlySayThingsThatCanBeHeard.
  • Gasoline. It's a lotta fun.
