  • One good thing about Vietnam, is that it shows that despite what is happening today in Iraq, there is hope for the future. The US used napalm and other such weapons indiscriminately; the NVA ran the infamous HanoiHilton; and both sides were prone to destroying whole villages considered "unfriendly". Fast forward 30 years past the end of the war. The two governments have normalized relations, and outside of the occasional extremist or bigot, there is little if any animosity between the American and Vietnamese people. Many Vietnamese live here, many Americans nowadays travel there (and have nothing to fear but the occasional pickpocket). May the US and Iraq, a generation hence, be in a similar situation.
