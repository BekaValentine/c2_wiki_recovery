Top Five Visitor Pages

WikiWords that should be included on WikiPages within TheFirstFiveClicks in order to help WikiPuppys tour WardsWiki in a systematic pattern. This pattern should PrepareTheWay, discourage WikiVandals and WikiSquatting, invite programmers to join wiki, and steer non-programmers towards a WikiCommunity that best serves their needs.
