Software Architectonics

SoftwareArchitectonics is a flavour of SoftwareArchitecture that deals with DesignPatterns from the SoftwareStructure.

The paper "Introducing the Software Architectonics viewpoint" by AlessandroMaccari and GalalGalal was accepted for WICSA 3 to be held in Montreal in August 2002 - see http://wicsa3.cs.rug.nl/ for more details.
