Thinking In Cee Plus Plus

Thinking in C++, by BruceEckel, second volume co-authored by ChuckAllison. Two volumes, subtitled "Introduction to Standard C++" (which is now in its second edition), and "Practical Programming". Note that this book used to be a single volume (the first one), the first edition didn't have the "Volume 1" indication.
