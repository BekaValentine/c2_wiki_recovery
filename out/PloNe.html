Plo Ne

http://www.plone.org

The star OpenSource ContentManagementSystem based on ZopeApplicationServer

The project was founded by AlanRunyan and AlexanderLimi.

PloNe 2.0 was released in April 2004.

Plone :

  • is an out of the box application for community portals
  • is fully customizable for intra/extranets or public sites
  • has lot of insistance on usability
  • is internationalized (more than 30 languages)
  • has a framework to add supplementary content types
  • skinnable

Plone 3.0 was released in August 2007

It is possible to integrate PloNe with ZwiKi.
