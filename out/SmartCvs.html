Smart Cvs

Cool CVS client. http://www.smartcvs.com

The Good

  1. Very comprehensive view of what has changed
  2. Good sorting/searching
  3. Very fast for large projects (caches CVS results)
  4. SSH1 and SSH2 support built in
  5. Supports external SSH client

The Bad

  1. Small but annoying inconveniences left in the free version ($45 is a low license fee, though)
