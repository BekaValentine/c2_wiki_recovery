Freedom Force

An entertaining super-hero game for the PC and Mac.

http://www.myfreedomforce.com

You can extend the game by developing your own scenarios. What is most interesting about this is that the language you use to script the scenarios is the PythonLanguage.

In fact, the game comes with its own PythonLanguage installation.
