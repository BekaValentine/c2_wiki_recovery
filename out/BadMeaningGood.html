Bad Meaning Good

Bad doesn't really mean "good" literally, but the word is sometimes used to describe good qualities that have a connotation of danger or toughness to them. And it is important to remember that the word "bad", when used in this context, inflects to "badder" and "baddest", not to "worse" or "worst."
