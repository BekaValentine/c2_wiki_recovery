Tshirt Politics

Much of the political work at AppleComputer revolved around who had the coolest t-shirts. You could get folks to do you BIG favors in return for a good shirt. The group with the AppleCrayComputer made particularly effective use of this strategy.

I much prefer this brand of politics to SpaceWarPolitics, which we had in spades at TektronixInc. -- KentBeck
