Freeman Benson

We're happy to announce a guest visit from Bjorn Freeman-Benson at August 2003's monthly meeting of XPDX. Bjorn presented at this year's Agile Development Conference speaking about YP, the XP like process specifically adapted to the politically charged environment of urban planning. We've invited Bjorn to present excerpts from this talk along with whatever else he would like to share with us.

http://www.agiledevelopmentconference.com/2003/schedule/researchpapers.html#P1

In addition to his comfortable staff position at U of W, Bjorn has seen all sides of hi-tec with positions at Amazon, OTI/IBM, startups and CS faculty. Bjorn is a contributor to the Fitster Eclipse plug-in.
