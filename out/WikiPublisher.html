Wiki Publisher

WikiPublisher is an add-on to PmWiki that will neatly typeset collections of pages using LaTeX, and serve these up to a user in PDF form. It's available as free software in two packages: the PmWiki addon that causes PmWiki to generate appropriate XML, and the typesetting server, which transforms this XML into PDF format.

Available at http://www.wikipublisher.org/
