Andrew Koenig

AndrewKoenig (mailto:ark@acm.org, http://www.acceleratedcpp.com/authors/koenig) retired in July 2003 from the AT&T Shannon Laboratory.

He was Project Editor for the 1998 and 2003 editions of the ISO/ANSI C++ standard [see CppStandard].

He has recently become interested in the PythonLanguage as well.
