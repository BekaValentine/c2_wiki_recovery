Analyzing Emergent Systems

You can't perform Cartesian analysis of the rules in an emergent system. If you take the rules individually, you won't get anything like a picture of what happens when they are together.

Is all lost? No, we just have to find new forms of analysis.

  • SensitivityAnalysis
  • but when SensitivityAnalysis fails (any predominantly linear system), try ActiveNonlinearTesting
