Q: So what does "healthy, software engineering-oriented culture" mean?

A: An approach to improve the quality and effectiveness of the software development process. Contains six sections on software culture concepts, software requirements, process improvement procedures, inspection, action planning, and measures and tools, identifying culture builders and culture killers, and offering information on resources such as seminars, conferences, and publications. Includes case studies on process improvement and software metrics programs
