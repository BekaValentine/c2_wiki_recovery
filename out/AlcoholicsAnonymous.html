Alcoholics Anonymous

Famous organization in which alcoholics achieve sobriety by undergoing group therapy and following a TwelveStepProgram. Participants must be WillingToChange.

Slogans:

  • One Day At A Time - achieve sobriety in small iterations

Participants often invoke the SerenityPrayer. (I have a booklet which explains how, on at least one occasion, the program was adapted slightly to suit an atheist.)
