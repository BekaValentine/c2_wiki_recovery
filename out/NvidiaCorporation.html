Nvidia Corporation

NvidiaCorporation is a maker of computer hardware for graphics accelerators, which now find application in general purpose use. See http://www.nvidia.co.uk/page/home.html

They previously developed the CeeGeeLanguage.

Their current language for general purpose numerical computing is the ComputeUnifiedDeviceArchitecture (CUDA) which is widely used.

They provide an implementation of the OpenCl standard.
