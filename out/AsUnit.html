As Unit

ASUnit is a TestingFramework written for MacromediaFlash's ActionScript.

There is a serious-looking AsUnit framework at http://www.debreuil.com/FrameworkDocs/UnitTestingOverview.htm - I haven't tested it yet, but wanted to put up the link so people can at least try it. -- HarlanWood
