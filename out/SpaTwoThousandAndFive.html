Spa Two Thousand And Five

SpaTwoThousandAndFive (SPA2005) is the annual conference of the BCS Software Practice Advancement Group (BCS SPA) formerly known as the OT conference of the BCS Object Oriented Programming and Systems Group (BCS OOPS).

The conference is the next in a long line of successful conferences (SPA2005 will be the 13th year).

SPA2005 will run from 10th - 13th April 2005 at the Robinson Centre, Bedfordshire, England.

Keep an eye on http://www.spa2005.org as more information is published about this great conference.

The programme is now available at the conference website.
