Hopp Pattern Language

Is HalfObjectPlusProtocol a pattern language instead of a simple pattern? We seem to find other patterns that compose into HOPP:

  • RemoteProxy
  • RemoteProcedureCall
  • SymmetricalReference

And there are patterns that refine HOPP:

  • SplitTreeFromLeaves
  • RelationshipObject

Consider the following pattern language for HOPP:

  • LocalConceptPresence
  • AutonomousCopy
  • SymmetricalReference
  • RemoteProcedureCall
  • SplitTreeFromLeaves
