What Is Simplicity

This, comes from some very simple design parameters, i.e. easy to edit and fast to search. But ...

If simplicity is a\thing like absence of complexity, well i dont think anyone is going to say that a web browser, or any part of the internet at all, lacks complexity.

"What if curl is my browser?"

So what we have here is not the essence of simplicity.

Simplicity is in the eye of the beholder.

The eye is very complex. It is the ability of one to control the activity in the reticular activating system within the brain, filter peripheral stimuli, and focus on the central object that allows one to perceive the simplicity of the scene . So one gazing at the eye of the beholder could indeed perceive the simplicity therein.

I don't think simplicity is EasiestDesign, SimpleDesign or MinimalDesign. where I am, these three are often incorrectly merged as the same thing. - JonathanCrossland

Simplicity is a form of delicious perfection that is often difficult to achieve, and is usually indicative of the hand of a master. What we all should strive toward...
