Wiki Superhero Joust

Many discussions on Wiki are like the archtypical superhero battle, where dialog as to the powers and abilities of each side are exchanged during battle.

Example: My opinion is such and such ...

My opinion is the exact opposite here's why ...

Well I invented the internet and etc etc

I discovered the GeneticCode and i'm right because blah blah

...

Your AdHominem cannot harm me: my wings are like a shield of steel! (BatFink)

Till one or the other gives up.
