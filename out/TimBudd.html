Tim Budd

Tim Budd is an AssociateProfessor of ComputerScience at OregonStateUniversity and author of several books [1], including ThePlatypusBook. He is thinking about using Wiki in his classes on Object-Oriented Programming.

He is also the writer of a tiny SmalltalkLanguage implementation referenced at BuddsLittleSmalltalk, and the designer of an experimental MultiParadigmLanguage known as LedaLanguage.
