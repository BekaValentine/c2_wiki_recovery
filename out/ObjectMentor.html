Object Mentor

Object Mentor Inc. (http://www.objectmentor.com)

A company founded by RobertCecilMartin, offering mentoring, training, and outsourced development services to the international community.

They take a very pragmatic approach to software design and are firm supporters of ExtremeProgramming.

Object Mentor offers classes in OOD, UnifiedModelingLanguage, DesignPatterns, CeePlusPlus, JavaLanguage, and ExtremeProgramming.

See XpTrainingClasses and PrinciplesOfObjectOrientedDesign.
