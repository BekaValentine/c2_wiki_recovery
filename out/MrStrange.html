Mr Strange

My name is Simon Strange, but in written formats (online, press, whatnot) I've always gone by the oh-so-clever name of Mr. Strange.

I'm continuing this great tradition on Wiki.

I find it hard to search the pages here - maybe learning about the RoadMaps will help.

I'm a full-time video game designer, and I'm interested in discussing the design of VideoGames (or just general GameDesign) with folks here. The subject often becomes philosophical, or transforms itself into a discussion about systems. Both eventualities seem well-suited to the Wiki format.

I use a DvorakKeyboard, I design and fold OrigamiModels, play ComputerGames, and try to provide an IdealChildhood for my progeny.

-- MrStrange
