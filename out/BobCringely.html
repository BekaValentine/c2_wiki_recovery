Bob Cringely

What could AdamRifkin or RohitKhare say about Robert X. Cringely that hasn't already been said?

See his column "I, Cringely" at http://www.pbs.org/cringely/

This guy can be a very informative and entertaining writer.

He wrote a book called AccidentalEmpires, which is a very funny and informed view of the history of Silicon Valley through the late seventies and eighties. The book was also used as the basis for a TV series.

He also busts on ReFactoring with the usual bravado of one who delightedly claims the fully clad emperor is, in fact, naked...
