Mela Tonin

Wonderful stuff, a natural hormone made by the gut and the pituitary, available over the counter in the US. Uses:

  1. You just flew to some place >2 hours out of your timezone to do some business. You can't afford to be lagged. 6 hours before your new waking up time, pop an appropriate amount of MelaTonin. Voila, your sleep pattern is reset and you're well rested when you wake, bright-eyed and ready to work.
  1. It's 2am. Never mind how it got to be 2am, it just is. You have to be at work and really honestly awake at 9. How the hell? As above - swig down the appropriate amount of MelaTonin, set the alarm, and you'll be good by 8.30.

What's an appropriate amount? Seems to vary from person to person a lot. Also with age. Try 1-3mg and see what happens. Takes about 1/2 hour to come on. Stay away from the time-release stuff if you want to use it as above.

Warning: IamNotaDoctor.

This instruction conflicts with recommendations used in Norway, at least, where the patient is told to ingest MelaTonin approximately 12 hours before they intend to wake up again.
