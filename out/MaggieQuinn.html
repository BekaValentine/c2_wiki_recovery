Maggie Quinn

MaggieQuinn is the pen name for Margaret Sharon Olscamp who lives in New Brunswick Canada. Margaret has been using this name for many years. It was first planned to be the label for a line of wearable art collections that never made it to market. There was also a MaggiQuinn song that was never released. For several years it was used on website maggiequinn.com. For the past several years it has been the name on M S Olscamp's web log www.maggiequinn.me
