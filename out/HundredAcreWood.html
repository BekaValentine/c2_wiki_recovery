Hundred Acre Wood

According to AaMilne's original Pooh books, the last place ChristopherRobin was seen.

An example of a really good place name for children's fiction. Why is it so good? Compare it to names such as "mysterious forest", "ancient forest", and "mysterious ancient forest of the mysterious ancients". By not striving to convey awe, the name does so effortlessly. Ommmm... -- DanielKnapp
