Resulting Context Names Problems

Add to the Resulting Context so that it names the next problems you are likely to encounter. If your Resulting Contexts only says, "problem solved," you don't need a section for it. On the other hand, if you say what the reader will next encounter, you have really added some information. I have never yet seen a pattern that didn't raise or lead to new problems. Ideally, they are smaller, that is idea, after all. --AlistairCockburn
