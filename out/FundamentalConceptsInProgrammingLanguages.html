Fundamental Concepts In Programming Languages

"Fundamental Concepts in Programming Languages" (1967, available online at http://www.wkap.nl/sampletoc.htm?1388-3690+13+1/2+2000 )

This seminal paper by ChristopherStrachey discusses notions of assignment, including LeftValues and RightValues. It also coined the terms ParametricPolymorphism and AdHocPolymorphism.
