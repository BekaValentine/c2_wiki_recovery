Lisp Humor

(eq today (car (cdr life)))

-- (Author unknown)

CommonLisp has other ways of saying car and cdr that, according to taste, either wreck that joke or clarify it.

[For example:

(eq today (first (rest your-life)))

- JayOsako]

You can still say car and cdr, though... I think, by the way, that I prefer

(eq today (car (cdr (life you))))
