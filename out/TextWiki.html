Text Wiki

A text wiki is one which the text presented in the html page by the browser is as close as possible to the text as it appears in the TextEdit.

Examples of how this wiki can be made to present text in a TextWiki format follow

Example 1 ( Wrapped Text )

This is an example of what a page will look like when the edited text is textonly, i.e. has no markups:

It is clear that Inter-Wiki technology has been delivered, and is continuing to be developed, and will expand in the future.

The future appears to be less Inter-Wiki and more Inter-Net. What has been called wiki appears to be becoming the document arm of Public Internet Communication, meshing with threading friendly systems, blogs, identity services, resource sharing, and existing online communities.
