How To Get Rid Of Really Long Pages

For WikiGnomes:

Recently, someone created a page filled with over a megabyte of "deleted" (maybe more; I stopped the page load in its tracks).

To get rid of such a monstrosity (without having to load the whole thing), use the EditCopy feature. Type the following URL in:

http://c2.com/cgi/wiki?copy=PageToDelete

and replace PageToDelete with the name of the offending page. You'll get the old copy in the upper pane; you can abort the loading of the differences and still have the edit controls available.
