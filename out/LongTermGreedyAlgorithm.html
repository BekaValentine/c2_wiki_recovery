Long Term Greedy Algorithm

Sometimes being self-serving is not a short-term goal. Certain skills have a broader focus, like learning. A long term (but yet relatively 'greedy') approach is to greedily take advantage of 'Meta-Benefits' like an opportunity to learn or try something new.

So the immediate benefits are not apparent because the greediness is not about immediate, tangible results. It is about the process of creating future tangible benefit.

-- DavidCymbala
