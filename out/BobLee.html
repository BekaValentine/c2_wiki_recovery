Bob Lee

Robert E. Lee [Bob] is a 30 year veteran of Software Engineering. His areas of concern include effectiveness, technical competencies, teamwork and enhancing value. His focus has shifted from technical systems to human systems that build technical systems in recent years.

A long-time admirer of Jerry Weinberg, Bob joined Jerry's SHAPE forum in November 1999, determined to make a difference or make a pest of himself.

KnowledgeWorkers: the challenge is to improve life for society as a whole!

Current interest centers on making technology investment more comfortable, profitable and casual for non-technical users. Those who are not pioneers nor early adopters need a way to avoid becoming "buggy whip manufacturers" left behind by technology shifts. How can we make others comfortable with InternetTime rather than make life more of a pressure-filled rat-race?
