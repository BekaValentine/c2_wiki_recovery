Projects

This is a a subset of the list as on my web page; provided here to invite discussion of the topics. If anyone is interested in seeing more information on any of these, let me know and I'll expand them here or make pages for them.

Completed:

StatSort
Algorithm that in practice sorts numeric keys in O(n) time (worst-case performance is O(nlogn)). It works by doing a numeric integration of a histogram of the keys in order to predict key placement in the sorted output.
HISM
Hankins Incremental Storage Manager
A Modula-2 library for automatic incremental garbage collection. Meant for use where responsiveness trumps performance. You can find it at Google Groups with search terms "hism hankins". I'd like to know what other people think of the "percolation" algorithm.
QDFC
Quick and Dirty Flash Cards. A SpikeSolution for making a memorization program modelled after flashcards, with an algorithm to optimize re-presentation of missed cards.
