Philippines Patterns Group

The Philippines Design Patterns Study Group meets every Wednesday from 7:00pm to 9:00pm at the Tektite East Tower. We have a different topic for each meeting and someone volunteers to lead the discussion. We tend to lean more towards Java, but the meetings are open to anyone who is interested.

Check the website here: http://www.pinoyjug.ph or you can join our mailing list at http://www.yahoogroups.com/group/pinoyjug and visit us at http://www.levelupgames.ph

Our meeting schedule and meeting topics are posted at the website. Directions to the meetings are also available.
