Guerilla Coder

A member of a haphazardly assembled guerilla team which is trying to undermine aspects of organization that threaten the CowboyCoding ideal. The guerilla coder will avoid working with other coders except when such work contributes to organizational anarchy: ignoring team rules and standards, refusing to develop code that will interact with other's code, ignoring peer review, etc.
