Ellen Ullman

The author of CloseToTheMachine and TheDumbingDownOfProgramming.

She's recently published her first novel, THE BUG (ISBN:0385508603), about a programmer in 1984 struggling with a difficult bug, and the tester who found it. I've read this book and highly recommend it. It captures the drama of debugging, the feelings of a programmer under pressure, and typical software company office politics. Among other things.--ApoorvaMuralidhara
