Ode To The Granovetter Diagram

The actual title of this paper is "Capability-based Financial Instruments". The up-to-date online copy can be found at http://www.erights.org/elib/capability/ode/index.html. It explains the DistributedObjectCapabilityModel to a modern crypto audience, with examples in E (the EeLanguage). With this as a base, it goes on to explain Smart Contracts.
