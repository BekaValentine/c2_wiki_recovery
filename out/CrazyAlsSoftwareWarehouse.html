CrazyAlsSoftwareWarehouse is a shorthand I've used to describe our industry from time to time. Didja ever see one of those ads for somebody's furniture warehouse? The guy looks goofy and insane and talks really fast, and at the end he says "why are my prices so low? I must be CRAAAAAAAZY!!!". (Just for the record I am critical of CodeReuse as a driving concept for delivering software, and even ComponentBasedDesign seems to me like one more attempt to over-generalize our work.
