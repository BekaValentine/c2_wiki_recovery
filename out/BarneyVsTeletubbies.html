  • Teletubbies don't have all those pesky children running around.
  • Teletubbies have zippers up their back.
  • Teletubbies have an OnsiteCustomer (that baby-sun thing).
  • Teletubbies have an adequate snack source for task completion ceremonies (those rabbits).
  • Barney's followers never have real arguments. Teletubbies argue constantly, but using a careful semi-verbal protocol.
  • A teletubby's tummy can be used for a 21-inch monitor.
  • Teletubbies have Noo-Noo, a really neat vacuum cleaner mascot. They are tool users.
