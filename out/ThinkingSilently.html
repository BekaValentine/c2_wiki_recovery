Thinking Silently

Thinking Silently or Privately ThinkingOutLoud.DonaldNoyes.20111025

Using the same techniques as those found in ThinkingOutLoud, except in the aspect of sharing and publishing in an open, common or public forum.

Writing down, speaking, and visually presenting thinking will still occur, but will be published or recorded for later personal or group review and the carrying out of a process leading to public exposure.
