Will Stott

Will Stott is an Exoftware mentor. He has had a number of articles about ExtremeProgramming published in magazines like Visual Systems Journal and The IEE Computing & Control. He is helping to organize the XPDay3 conference in London (1-2nd Dec, keynote by Mary Poppendieck) and regularly attends the Extreme Tuesday Club (London) as well as the Agile Sussex Group. Will is a member of The IEE and serves on their Executive Committee for the Consultants Professional Network. He is a Chartered Engineer and has worked in the IT industry for 20+ years, mostly as a contractor.

Will is moving from London/West Sussex to Zurich in November 2003 so as to support his partner whose job has been transferred there. His work will take him back to the UK for a few months in the new year, but is hopeful of being able to find jobs closer to home thereafter. He hopes to make new friends in Zurich and generally help spread the Agile message.

You can contact Will by mailto:Will.Stott@iee.org.
