Shai Ben Yehuda

Shai Ben-Yehuda is the founder of ArtwareSoft (2004) and ItemField (2000).

Shai is the author of the TGP methodology (TgpMethodology) and PatternLanguage of FrameworkConstruction (http://st-www.cs.uiuc.edu/users/hanmer/PLoP-97/Proceedings/shai.pdf).

ItemField founded in 2000 (http://www.itemfield.com) provides Next-Generation Data Transformation products.

ArtwareSoft (http://www.artwaresoft.com) founded in 2004, intends to provide open source to support the usage in the TgpMethodology.
