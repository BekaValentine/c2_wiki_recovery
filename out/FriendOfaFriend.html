Friend Ofa Friend

The Friend Of A Friend (FOAF) vocabulary is a ResourceDescriptionFramework ontology that attempts to describe relationships between people in a SemanticWeb fashion.

  • http://wiki.foaf-project.org/ - FOAF wiki
