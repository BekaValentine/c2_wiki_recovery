Possible reasons:

  • Wiki is part of a (traditionaly) male dominated culture anyway (the software industry). See WomenProgrammers.
  • Wiki has a masculine group structure (see PolyAmory)
  • Wiki's communication model in practice promotes a confrontational style (although it is meant to promote collaboration) (how long has this statement been here without anybody asking what its premise was?)
  • Most women do more real work than most men, and hence don't have time to Wiki puh-leeeeease
  • Most women are more likely to have a life?
