Thwozz Thwozz Thwozz

Thwozz is the sound made as an imaginary PropellerBeanie is twirled above one's head. One may use this sound as a replacement for the rewarding of an actual propeller beanie when one is trying to feed back to a developer who is explaining an overly-complex solution.

A proper geek would keep his/her beanie well-oiled.
