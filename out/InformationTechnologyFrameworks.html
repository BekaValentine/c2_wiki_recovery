Information Technology Frameworks

A few different InformationTechnologyFrameworks exist, most are developed to help with InformationTechnologyGovernance.

From "I.T. Frameworks Demystified" at http://www.cio-today.com/infrastr/story.xhtml?story_title=I-T--Frameworks-Demystified--Avoiding-Overload&story_id=31004&category=infrastr

Frameworks are there to improve CustomerRelationshipManagement, ServiceLevelManagement, SecurityManagement, etc.

The more common IT frameworks being implemented at larger companies include:

  • InformationTechnologyServiceManagement
  • ControlObjectivesForInformationAndRelatedTechnology
  • CapabilityMaturityModelIntegration
  • ISO 17799
