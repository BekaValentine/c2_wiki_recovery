Left Selects Right

GraphicalUserInterface convention whereby selecting an item in a list on the left side of a display will cause details about that item to be displayed on the right side.

If more than two panes are provided, allowing navigation through a deep hierarchy, then proceeding from left to right goes "down" the hierarchy.
