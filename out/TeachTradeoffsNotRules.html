Teach Tradeoffs Not Rules

There is rarely a free-lunch in software engineering. WaterbedTheory abounds. When teaching students or the curious about "principles of software engineering", do not feed them absolute rules, but rather educate them about the tradeoffs, hopefully along with examples. If you have a particular preference one way or another, then state so, but don't wrap such advice up as the OneTrueWay. --top
