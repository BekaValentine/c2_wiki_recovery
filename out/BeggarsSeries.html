Beggars Series

A trilogy that is an answer to AtlasShrugged... if the "unproductive" are "worthless", then what about children, the disabled, the aged, and (of course) beggars? In these books, the "super-productive" people that are the heroes of AynRand are the genetically-engineered "Sleepless".
