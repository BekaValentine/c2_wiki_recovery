Encourage People To Know Smalltalk

You're working on a software project where people make use of ugly development tool in an ugly way. You can't argue the ugliness of the tool and the way because your you have very different experience. So EncouragePeopleToKnow, in this case SmalltalkLanguage. Immediate result is more convergent experience and better communication. Appreciated result is development of better way to use the tool, or possibly another tool considered.
