Rick Moen

Rick describes himself as:

... a recovering sysadmin, working with Linux, WANs/LANs, and sundry network servers in the San Francisco Bay Area ...

From http://www.aaronsw.com/2002/rickMoenWillBeThere :

Rick Moen is a Linux activist in the San Francisco Bay Area who has acquired a reputation for attending virtually every Linux event in the whole Bay Area -- or at least those on his comprehensive list, Bay Area Linux Events. Rick is also an instigator of the Consortium of All Bay Area Linux, where the various Bay Area Linux user groups gather. Some of the Linux User Groups have started to advertise their events with "Rick Moen will be there, will you?"

Rick also co-authored HowToAskQuestionsTheSmartWay.
