Internet Artifact

Artifacts are everywhere, if you don't believe me, consult TheInternet ThinkingOutLoud.DonaldNoyes.20131005

It has become such that everyday life has become more dependent upon TheInternet, than its originators may have ever imagined.

Inventions and Applications are developed everyday which make TheInternet more valuable and nearly indispensable.

It is the Display of the InternetArtifact that adds value and utility to an individual's life.

This artifact is usually a page from a site, containing links and/or processes producing the needful results desired by millions of users, sometime thousands of which are active in a given moment.
