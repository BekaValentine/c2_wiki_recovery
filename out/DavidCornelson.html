David Cornelson

David Cornelson, software architect, hobbyist book publisher, and author of several interactive fiction games including The Town Dragon, Cattus Atrox, and The Devil Made Me Do It. David published the Inform Designer's Manual: 4th Edition in 2001 and The Inform Beginner's Guide in 2003. A third book with the working title of Interactive Fiction Theory is scheduled for release sometime in 2004.
