Hallmark People To Come Out

They notice a disturbance in Wiki and the HallmarkPeople clasp their hands over their faces in horror. Who could have done something so horrible? Why would someone vandalize Wiki? And perhaps the most important question to ask, what strained anthropomorphic metaphor about Wiki can I squeeze out that expresses my inner sadness over this tragedy? The sappy melodramatic metaphors flow as liquid as their tears.

--JohnPassaniti
