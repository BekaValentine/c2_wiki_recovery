Ralf Lippold

RalfLippold has studied business administration and economics. He has worked as a Process Consultant for BMW putting the new plant in Leipzig to operation. His passion lies in information and communication logistics and bringing collective learning into action. Ralf Lippold is the initiator of several CoWorkingSpaces such as LockSchuppen, and CoOrpheum, in Dresden. He is dealing with future research and in particular, the implications of new communication tools on the work and life environment.
