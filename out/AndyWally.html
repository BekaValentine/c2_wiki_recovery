Andy Wally

Andy Wally is a software developer in the San Francisco Bay Area, California, USA. His interests include games, puzzles, AI, and User Interfaces as they relate to Human Computer interaction.

He was born in Sleepy Hollow, New York, USA. He graduated from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute in Troy, New York, USA in 1995 with a Bachelors in Computer Science.
