Math For The Layman

A recent paper by KenIverson using J (the JayLanguage) to investigate a wide range of math subjects. Delightful, entertaining and revealing for us math challenged, but all are guaranteed to learn something.

The paper is available at:

http://www.cs.trinity.edu/About/The_Courses/cs301/math-for-the-layman/ (working link at 2004-09-07)

http://www.jsoftware.com/pubs/mftl/mftl.htm (BrokenLink 2004-09-07)

http://www.jsoftware.com/resources/papers.htm (BrokenLink 2004-09-07) has some other papers by Iverson or relevant to J.

(The J system is also available from http://www.jsoftware.com.)
