Hamish Lawson

Hamish Lawson is a software developer in the Business Improvements unit of the University of St Andrews, Scotland. He specialises in web-based enterprise information systems. Previously he was a Research Fellow in the University's School of Computer Science, as a member of the Distributed Systems Group. Before St Andrews he was the Web Development Manager for the School of Computing & Mathematics at the University of Teesside, England.
