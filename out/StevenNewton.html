Green & Grey

Funny day -- that glary yellow thing in the sky again,
Scary blue mist above.
I'd float right up
With no moist blanket over me.

Fair days, bright highlights and deep shadows,
Strange sights.
I like the way the green world
Looks in the grey rain.
