Wiki Quicky

A wiki page which fits on your screen in its entirety. Singular of WikiQuickies.

  • easy to refactor
  • no scrolling, fast browsing
  • new content is immediately recognized
  • easy to remember what the title stands for
  • no problem with browsers that choke when editing large pages

Compare ShortMethods.

Antonym: TooBigToEdit.

-- FalkBruegmann and others
