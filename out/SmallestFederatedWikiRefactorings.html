Smallest Federated Wiki Refactorings

This is a project,now under way, which will change the way some people will be found using content first generated here.

UsefulUsableUsed

It has its beginnings with an experiment in 2011 which investigated the possibility of and then the implementation of a trial version showing its "Usefulness".

It then morphed into a demonstration, complete with a series of videos showing how Usable" and varied it can be.

Now it has passed into the "Used" phase via a full-fledged Project of multiple topical refactorings, as well as a complete Wiki refactoring.
