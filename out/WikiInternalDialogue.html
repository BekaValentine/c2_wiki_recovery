Wiki Internal Dialogue

What it sounds like when you read a Wiki discussion by anonymous authors. If you picture the Wiki as a single entity, it's like it's trying to work out a problem in its head with one side being advocate, and the other side devil's advocate. And sometimes it sounds like it's brainstorming different alternatives.
