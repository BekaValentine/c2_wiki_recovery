Wellspring Of Negativity

[Sometimes ProgrammingIsNotFun, because]

  • It is easier to complain than to fix things. You have a ChronicComplainer. (Or you 'are one.)
  • It is easier to find flaws than to create.
  • Some people feel that making positive comments is unnecessary, or "fake", so they only make corrections and negative comments.
  • Some people can't resist disagreeing, even with things they don't understand. See PointyHairedBoss.
    • The lure of unfulfilled potential for our current project blinds us to our past accomplishments.
    • What's right is simply right, that's taken for granted; what's wrong needs fixing, why haven't you fixed it already?
