Philip Wadler

Philip Wadler - Home page at http://homepages.inf.ed.ac.uk/wadler/

One of the principal designers of the HaskellLanguage and of GenericJava. He did research on ways to extend the classic HindleyMilner type systems.

Author of several introductory papers OnMonads (http://homepages.inf.ed.ac.uk/wadler/topics/monads.html) and on LinearLogic and IntuitionisticLogic (see IsFkkActuallyTrue).

Maintains a list of applications of FunctionalProgrammingInTheRealWorld and a GuideToFunctionalProgrammingOnTheWeb (http://homepages.inf.ed.ac.uk/wadler/guide.html).
