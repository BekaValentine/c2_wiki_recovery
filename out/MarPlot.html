Mar Plot

MarPlot is a FreeAsInBeer GIS software provided by the US EPA and the US Census Bureau.

To get Marplot
http://www.epa.gov/ceppo/cameo/marplot.htm
To get Maps for your area
http://www.epa.gov/ceppo/cameo/marmaps/

It's available for both Windows and MacOs. It is not as full featured as a professional GIS solution (ArcInfo), but if you want a simple street map, it's better than MapQuest because:

  1. It doesn't require an internet connection.
  2. Output is more flexibile.
