Space Untitled

Space Untitled is a cafe at 133 Greene Street in SoHo, Manhattan, NewYorkCity.

It's on the west side of Greene between Houston and Prince, two blocks west of the Broadway-Lafayette/Bleecker B/D/F/V/downtown 6 subway station. It's quite close to the Prince Street N/R station and the Bleecker uptown 6 station as well. The Metropolitan Transportation Authority's website is http://www.mta.info.
