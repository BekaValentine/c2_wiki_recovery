Promotion Is The Product

PromotionIsTheProduct is inspired by MediumIsTheMessage, and it is a synonym for PackagingIsTheContent.

As attention spans diminish due to more choices than time to think, often we get led (or misled) by promotion efforts related to a product. A product in this sense could be a concept, ideology, or even things tangible.

This is the ultimate VirtualReality and simulation of life.
