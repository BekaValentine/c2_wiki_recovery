Super Ellipse

A SuperEllipse is a curve with (Cartesian) equation:

|(x/a)|^r + |(y/b)|^r = 1

A SuperEllipse can also be described as:

x = a cos^(2/r) t = a (cos t)^(2/r)
y = b cos^(2/r) t = b (cos t)^(2/r)

PietHein called the curve with r = 5/2 and a = b "the" superellipse.
