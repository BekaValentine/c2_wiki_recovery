Rod Schmidt

Rod Schmidt is an independent developer and the founder of the one man company infiniteNIL Software (http://www.infinitenil.com).

infiniteNIL currently has two products. The first one is PhoneWord, an application that finds words or phrases spelled by a phone number, making it easier to remember. The second one is Packrat, a desktop client for 37signals Backpack web application. It allows you to use your Backpack data when your not online and keep everything in sync when you get back online.

infiniteNIL also does a good portion of the development of a well known free form information storage and retrieval application for a company that doesn't want it to be known that they farm out some of their development.
