Ambiguous Viewpoint

Both client and design team don't want to commit any point of view that may cause one or both to be obligated to providing a finished product and thereby put their career and standing in the company in danger.

This can also be used by either party to prove that the other party HasItIn for them by not producing WhatWeWanted or WhatWeAgreedOnButNotInWriting.
