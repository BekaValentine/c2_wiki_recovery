Mutual Respect

  • Don't call others names
  • Don't accuse others of ill-conceived plans or motivations
  • Don't delete other's content except to clean out name-calling or to move off-topic content to another spot (unless not computer-related, then deletion is okay).
  • Don't accuse one of "you keep doing/saying X" without providing at least 3 specific instances.
  • Not liking somebody is no reason to ignore these rules toward them. Be nice to even Hitler, at least on this Wiki.
  • Don't use Wiki as a revenge or punishment tool.
  • Apologize if you slip rather than just ignore it.
  • Complain about specific content, not the personality(s) behind it.
