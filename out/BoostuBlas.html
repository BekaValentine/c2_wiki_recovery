Boostu Blas

BoostuBlas is a matrix template library in the BoostLibraries

It is an alternative to the MatrixTemplateLibrary.

For more information and in the rationale section a discussion of some of the alternative libraries, see:

  • http://www.boost.org/doc/libs/1_56_0/libs/numeric/ublas/doc/index.htm
