Silicon Valley Patterns Group

The Silicon Valley Patterns Group meets weekly on Tuesday Evenings at a Cafe in Cupertino. The location is relaxing and the conversation is engaging.

The group studies established analysis and design patterns and is also planning Pattern Hatching and Workshopping sessions. We have had a strong secondary interest in ExtremeProgramming.

When:

Every Tuesday
7:00pm - 9:00pm

Where:

Hobee's
21265 Stevens Creek Blvd
Cupertino CA 95014
(408) 255-6010

in The Oaks mall

Who:

Patterns enthusiasts, any level of experience.
There are no requirements for membership and regular attendance is encouraged but not required.

Directions:

Follow any regional map to Hwy 85 and follow it to the Stevens Creek Blvd exit in Cupertino (this is south of I280). Proceed East on Stevens Creek to the first light (at Mary), take the left-most turn lane.(De'Anza College and the Flint Center will be on the right). Make a U-Turn at Mary and take the first driveway on the right into the Oaks Center. We will be meeting at Hobee's.

Current information is available via the mailing list, http://groups.yahoo.com/group/siliconvalleypatterns .
