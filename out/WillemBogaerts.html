The results greatly depended on the project managers I worked with. Funny enough, they made absolutely no problem whatsoever about salespeople handling technical stuff.

Oh, and I was a medieval wood- and bone worker for a year. No joke. I was working in a theme park that shows how life was in Holland in different times. What I learned there is that programming is a craft.
