Knowledge Map

A graph with vertices that are linked to concepts and edges that represent relations such as "is like" or "includes". Visual Tour function of this WikiWiki shows a portion of the knowledge, the Knowledge Mapping science abbreviates to KM.

An extensive repository of KM topics: http://www.voght.com/cgi-bin/pywiki?KnowledgeMapping
