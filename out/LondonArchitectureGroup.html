London Architecture Group

An informal group of developers in LondonTown who meet every month or so to discuss software architecture. We met and discuss ComponentBasedDevelopment, LayeredFrameworks, ThirdPartyBinding, and AbstractInteractions.

Contact CharlesWeir for more information.
