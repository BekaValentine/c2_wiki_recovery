Database Capabilities

DatabaseCapabilities is a way of integrating databases into wiki pages and having a nice, easy simple way of creating, editing, deleting data. Currently, only PWiki2 implements a usable implementation. (See: http://wiki.shortcircuit.net.au/?p=DatabaseCapabilitiesTest)

For more information about DatabaseCapabilities, please see: http://wikifeatures.wiki.taoriver.net/moin.cgi/DatabaseCapabilities
