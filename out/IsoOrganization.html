Iso Organization

"International Organization for Standardization" is better known as ISO . It has lots of influence over governments, see WikiPedia entry at http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO.

ISO has working relationships with other standard setting institutions such as ANSI (US based), ECMA (European)

Some of the ISO influences at WardsWiki include:

  • OpenSystemsInterconnect
  • IsoNineThousand
  • StandardGeneralizedMarkupLanguage (father of ExtensibleMarkupLanguage)
  • IsoSecurity
  • TopicMaps
  • ?? programming languages

Note: ISO is not an acronym; it comes from the Greek word ίσος (isos), meaning "equal".
