Expand Acronyms

WikiWikiWeb doesn't recognize acronyms as PageNames because they don't match the LinkPattern. They aren't in CamelCase or BumpyCase.

Please, before using one, show its expansion, as in:

  • CG ChrisGarrod
  • RK RichardKulisz
  • XP ExtremeProgramming

AKA ShortWikiSignaturesSmell.
