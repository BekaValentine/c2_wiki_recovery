Maintain As Usual

I've been looking at Fit (FrameworkForIntegratedTest) lately, and one phrase in the instructions really struck me: Maintain As Usual. So far, I've never been correct to treat code that way, my life would always have been simpler if I remembered to MaintainAsUsual.
