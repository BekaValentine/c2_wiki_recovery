Leaning Tower Of Pisa

Famous tower in Italy - due to settling of the wet sand under its foundation, it leans precariously to one side. Many attempts have been made to fix the tower, but it still leans.

A fine example of an elegant aesthetic design, with strong structural properties - on an utterly terrible foundation. There are many examples of LeaningTowersOfPisa in software design. (One could say this about WindowsNinetyFive, WindowsNinetyEight, and WindowsMe - though that is far too complimentary to their aesthetic and structural properties.)
