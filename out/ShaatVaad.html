Shaat Vaad

ShaatVaad is a sanskrit word. It means 'Complete Truth has not been expressed yet. There is more to say'

ThereIsMoreToSay makes the Final word in programming useless. Since there is always more to say, We should not limit the abilities of class to add and understand new things at a later date.

--RohitLodha
