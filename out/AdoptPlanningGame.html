Adopt Planning Game

Management spews platitudes about 'getting it right this time'.

  • The customer wants it all, wants it of the highest quality, wants it for $X and no more, and wants it NOW!!
  • Sales people are afraid of losing customers to the competition, so they agree to ludicrous contracts promising the moon.
  • Developers have no real concept of how long software development takes.

Therefore: Adopt the PlanningGame.
