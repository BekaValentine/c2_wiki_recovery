Patrick Mc Govern

Patrick McGovern is an architect in Unisys's office of the CTO. Patrick is active in the open standards community and enjoys working with many different technologies.

Patrick has done work implementing MOF, EMF, XMI and other standards.

Patrick was born in Brooklyn, NY. Went to Brooklyn Technical High School and the University of Maryland. In between he spent 5 years in the Navy. He is not the Billionaire that runs IDG...much to his chagrin.

If you would like to contact him his email address is patrick.mcgovern@unisys.com.
