Wiki Is Addictive

When can you be sure that you're a WikiAddict:

  1. Every morning the first thing you do is check RecentChanges.
  1. When you write any document or email you try to use WikiWords in it.
  1. When you open a document on your hard drive you expect to find

some additions and updated information.

Cure for addiction

do these twice a day for 2 weeks

  1. start seconding the deletion of spams
  1. reread pages on refactoring and then go apply it
  1. update information in old OnTopic pages
