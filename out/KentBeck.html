Kent Beck

Kent's responsible for or a major contributor to HillsideGroup, SmalltalkBestPracticePatterns, SoftwarePatterns, HotDraw, FirstClassSoftware, the TimeTravel patterns, the xUnit family of TestingFrameworks and ExtremeProgramming. His most recent book is ImplementationPatterns (http://www.amazon.com/dp/0321413091).

Kent is collecting ConsequencesOfShorteningReleaseCycles.

Here's his bio with photo from the repository: http://c2.com/ppr/about/author/kent.html. Here is his self-published bio: http://www.threeriversinstitute.org/Kent%20Beck.htm.
