A series of programming puzzles which illustrate various JavaLanguage LanguageGotchas. Or JavaSubtleties, if you prefer... As such, it's a little like Bloch's other book, EffectiveJava, and a lot like the various GotchaBooks that have come out for other ProgrammingLanguages.

Solve these puzzles and you'll never again fall prey to the counterintuitive or obscure behaviors that can fool even the most experienced programmers. -- Amazon Reviewer
