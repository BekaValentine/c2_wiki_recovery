Bicker Flag

Place the BickerFlag tag at the beginning of a paragraph that is mostly about personal interaction issues. It can be used to identify off-topic bickering so that readers know what to skip if they are not interested in personal conflict-related material.

Example:

BickerFlag: My mom does NOT wear army boots! Her ankles are merely swollen and gangrened, which makes them look like army boots.
