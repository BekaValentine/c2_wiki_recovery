Information Position

Locations of Information ThinkingOutLoud.DonaldNoyes.2007-20090512 20100523

This is a concept which gives a physical location to a piece of information.

  • A point on the earth's surface - The coordinate system of Latitude and Longitude discoverable on a map
  • A given file within a resource - The path of a file and the filename and fileextension
  • Webpage - The protocol and address of a page on the internet
