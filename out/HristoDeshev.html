Born and living in Bulgaria.

  • I am JustaProgrammer.
  • Relatively new to the IT industry - I started working as a programmer in 2000. Graduated from university in December 2002.
  • Somewhat biased towards ExtremeProgramming and Agile methodologies in general.

I started getting into ExtremeProgramming just before I graduated. I've been doing some of code'n'fix before that. Tried to delve into the SystemArchitect mindset and did not like it at all. I started looking at ExtremeProgramming as a way to make the software development a more disciplined, predictable, and enjoyable experience. My initial focus was on TestDrivenDevelopment, Refactoring and defect rate improvements. I think my view on the software development process has changed significantly thanks to the XpMailingList

I am a part of the effort of creating a Bulgarian ExtremeProgramming user group. The group has a mailing list set up at yahoo groups - http://groups.yahoo.com/group/xp-bulgaria/.
