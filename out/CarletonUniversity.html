Carleton University

http://www.carleton.ca

Based in Ottawa, Ontario, Canada, Carleton is one of the leading computer science universities in Canada. It still is one of the major object-oriented programs in the world. It was, in fact, the first university in North America to adopt the SmalltalkLanguage and ObjectOrientedProgramming. It did so in the early 80's. (But, see discussion below...)

The School of Computer Science lives at http://www.scs.carleton.ca.

Many images of the RedScreenOfDeath burn in my mind from first year. In recent years, the RSOD has been replaced with java.lang.NoClassDefFoundError errors and such.
