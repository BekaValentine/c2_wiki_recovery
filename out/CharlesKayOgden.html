Charles Kay Ogden

C. K. Ogden (1889-1957) British writer and important linguist who originated Basic English (BasicEnglishLanguage), a simplified system of the English language intended as a uniform, standardized means of international communication.

  • The Meaning of Meaning, ISBN: 1855067692, with I. A. Richards, W. Terrence Gordon
  • General Basic English Dictionary
