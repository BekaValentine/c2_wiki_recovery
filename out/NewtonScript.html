Newton Script

PrototypeBasedProgramming language for the AppleNewton line of PersonalDigitalAssistants.

Based upon the SelfLanguage. See "SELF and the Origins of NewtonScript" at http://wsmith.best.vwh.net/Self-intro.html

See http://www.cc.gatech.edu/~schoedl/projects/NewtonScript/ for a short introduction. Above link is now inactive, wayback machine copy is at http://web.archive.org/web/20041010175535/http://www.cc.gatech.edu/~schoedl/projects/NewtonScript/
