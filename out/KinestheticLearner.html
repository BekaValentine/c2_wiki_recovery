Kinesthetic Learner

A kinesthetic learner is a person who learns by doing or by muscle movments rather than thorugh the more common modes of learning by watching (VisualLearner) or learning by hearing (AuditoryLearner). Each person has a dominant learning style. It is the mode in which they learn most easily. Text is closely related to auditory learning, graphics are closely related to visual learning and GuiActionTrace GuiShorthand is closely related to kinesthetic learning. --JonGrover
