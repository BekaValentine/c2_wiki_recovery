John Pugh

Mr Pugh was an excellent professor at Carleton University's Dept. of Computer Science. My first class in Smalltalk development was with him. Hey, I kind of miss using Smalltalk.

John was one of the founding members of TheObjectPeople and was the president of the company until it was sold in 2000. John is now the president and CEO of the OttawaFury Soccer club.
