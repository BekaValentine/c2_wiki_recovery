Playing Industrial Logic's XP card game at a SF BayAreaXpUsersGroup meeting was a very good experience. It helped break the ice and it was the source of a lot of really good XP discussion. It's more of a conversation facilitator than a game. The rules are a bit tricky/different, so I'd suggest that someone tries it out at home first alone.
