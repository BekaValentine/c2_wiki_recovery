Michael Bolton is a tester, program manager, consultant, and trainer. He was a participant in the Workshop on Teaching Software Testing 2003, convened by Cem Kaner and James Bach.

You can find our more about Michael at http://www.developsense.com, and you can reach him at mb@developsense.com.

He publishes a monthly newsletter on testing and quality issues.

Michael also objects to the gratuitous use of the third person.
