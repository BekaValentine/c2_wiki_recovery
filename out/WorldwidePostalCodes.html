Worldwide Postal Codes

World wide postal codes are a conceptual framework for building teams. This list consists of a set of areas that people can identify themselves as living in so that when they are building teams, it should be easy to match up people living within moving or commuting distance of each other. Sizes are similar throughout the world and tend to map to areas the size of Canadian states (Provinces) American States (States) European States (Nations) African states (Countries) and similar entities throughout the world.

Special emphasis is also given to large islands or archipelagoes of small islands.

Perhaps this list should be renamed WorldwideCommutingAreaCodes

Codes can be obtained from sources such as the US post office the International Organization for Standardization http://www.iso.ch/iso/en/prods-services/iso3166ma/02iso-3166-code-lists/list-en1.html and the top level domain list etc.

Team building

I built this list originally to build teams. I wanted a set of areas that people could identify themselves as living in so that when they were building teams, it would be easy to match up people living within moving or commuting distance of each other. Worldwide Postal Codes was essentially a framework for building this list. -- JonGrover
