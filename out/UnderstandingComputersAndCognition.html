Understanding Computers And Cognition

by TerryWinograd and FernandoFlores. ISBN:0201112973

Stunning. This book is many things:

  • an introduction to existentialism, hermeneutics, and the theory of biology.
  • a critique of modern ArtificialIntelligence and cognitive science work.
  • a proposal for a new way to design computer software.
  • a rockin' discussion of TheRationalisticTradition.

Just read it.
