Chinese Wiki Engines

WikiEngine's that support Chinese character and/or other multi-byte languages.

See also: ChineseWiki, WikiEngines, TopTenWikiEngines

  • ChiqChaqWiki
  • OpenWiki -- ASP, Based on UseModWiki and MoinMoin
  • JspWiki -- JSP-based wiki with optional versioning, licensed under LGPL.
  • MoinMoin -- A PythonLanguage SourceForge project, based on PikiPiki (VNIHF)
  • PhpWiki -- A very popular PhpLanguage Wiki based on UseModWiki, with many features added.
  • UseModWiki -- A PerlLanguage wiki, based on Ward's original WikiWiki.
  • WakkaWiki fork -- CooCooWakka, PHP/MySQL Wiki
  • WikkiTikkiTavi (60K) -- Written in PHP, uses MySQL
  • VeryQuickWiki -- Servlets/JSPs, file-based
  • PmWiki -- A popular PhpLanguage Wiki, easy installation, simple design, nice feature list.
  • DokuWiki -- PhpLanguage, file-based
  • MediaWiki
