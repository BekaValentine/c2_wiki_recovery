World Views

The set of foundational beliefs or principles that influence how one perceives the world and interacts with it. In a broader sense, the beliefs that arise out of one's essential world view.

Religious and philosophical beliefs are typically a significant aspect of this, though it may extend more broadly to domains such as politics.
