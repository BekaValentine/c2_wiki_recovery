Wiki Prayer

Ward, grant me the serenity to accept the pages I cannot edit,
The courage to edit the pages I can,
And the wisdom to know the difference.

Magna res est vocis et silentii temperamentum! (Publilius Syrus, about 50 B.C.) "The great thing is to know when to speak and when to keep quiet!"
