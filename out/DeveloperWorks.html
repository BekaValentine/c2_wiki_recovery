Developer Works

developerWorks (http://www.ibm.com/developerWorks) IBM's worldwide resource for developers. With how-to articles, tutorials, tips, downloads, and other resources dedicated to open, industry-standard technologies (e.g. Open source, XML, Web Services, Linux, Wireless, Grid, Java, etc.), as well as IBM's developer products (including offerings from WebSphere, DB2, Rational, Lotus, Tivoli, and eServer).

developerWorks has been praised as "a developer's paradise" and "perhaps the best place to get hang of technologies such as Linux, Java, XML and even Wireless," and has won various awards, including the 2004 Jolt Product Excellence Award in the "Web Sites and Developer Networks" category. (The IBM alphaWorks emerging technologies site won the 2003 Jolt Product Excellence Award in the same category; see http://alphaworks.ibm.com).

For more information, see http://www-128.ibm.com/developerworks/aboutdw/

  • For English, go to http://www.ibm.com/developerWorks.
  • For Chinese, go to http://www.ibm.com/developerWorks/cn. The Chinese site is not only a translation of many English articles but also has many original Chinese articles by Chinese authors.
  • Similarly, developerWorks offers local sites in Japan, Korea, and Taiwan. These sites also incorporate translations of English content as well as many original articles by local authors.

See http://www-128.ibm.com/developerworks/country/ for links to all of the developerWorks local sites.
