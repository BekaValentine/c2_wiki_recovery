Apprenticeship In Education

Apprenticeship in a Software Studio: An Old and New Model for Education (http://www.rolemodelsoftware.com/moreAboutUs/publications/apprenticeshipInASoftwareStudio.php) written by NathanielTalbott and KenAuer shows us a new model for teaching ComputerScience to students. It is pretty much integrated with XP practices such as PairProgramming, which encourages apprenticeship.

If I'd had my lab classes in this form during my CS course in college... I only wish I'd had... -- JuneKim
