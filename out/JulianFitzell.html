Julian Fitzell

JulianFitzell is a SmalltalkLanguage consultant based in Canada.

In 2001, Fitzell and AviBryant created SeasideFramework, the prototypical ModalWebFramework, in SqueakSmalltalk. (Seaside has since been ported to other Smalltalks.)

His blog, Kaffee und: [Kuchen], is at http://blog.fitzell.ca/
