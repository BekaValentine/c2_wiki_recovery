Deletion Conventions

Anyone can delete anything, and anyone can restore a PageDeletion. However, it would be nice if people generally followed some of the suggested conventions below.

  • First, WhenToDeletePages.
    • AreYouThere
    • DeleteOnceRestoreOnce
    • ExponentialBackoffEditing
    • WikiSquatting
  • Then, HowToDeletePages. Deletion tags:
    • DeletedButWelcome (HowToUseDeletedButWelcome)
    • DeleteNoContent (BeforeYouDeleteNoContent)
    • DeleteNoContextLinks
    • DeleteObsolete

See also DeletionConventionsDiscussion
