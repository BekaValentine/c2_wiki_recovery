Mechanical Testing

See ProductTesting

  • Does the color change in sunlight?
  • Does the plastic survive contact with usual household chemicals?
  • Cooking Oil?
  • Detergents? Alcohol? Other cleaning chemicals?
  • What happens when someone trips over the cable? Powercable? Ethernetcable?
  • What happens when something hits it?
  • Is it dog proof?
  • Can it fall onto a concrete floor from 1 meter ten times on every corner? And still work?
  • Do the connectors survive 1000 insertions and removals?
  • Does it work during heat cycling between 20 and 65 degrees in 30 minutes intervals for two weeks?
  • Does it work after being stored in a freezer?
