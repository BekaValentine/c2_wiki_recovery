Peter Hall Effect

An ApproachAvoidanceGradient effect discovered by Peter Hall of LimpinWood.

  • Observe any cloud of midges at dusk.
    • Sing a pure tone to them and the cloud will expand.
    • Lower the tone and they contract.
    • Oscillate it and you can dance with them.

Not to be confused with the HallEffect
