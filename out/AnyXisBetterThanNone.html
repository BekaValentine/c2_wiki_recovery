Any Xis Better Than None

Sometimes you delay doing some AmeliorationPattern because you want to do it in the best way. In meantime you stand with an AntiPattern, a GreyPattern or NoPatternAtAll.

Therefore,
Start doing it immediately, as well as you can now.
However,
Be sure that having a partial X isn't worse than no X at all.

See also: DoSimpleThings, TheBestIsTheEnemyOfTheGood
