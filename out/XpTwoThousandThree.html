Xp Two Thousand Three

The Fourth International Conference on eXtreme Programming and Agile Processes in Software Engineering

JoshuaKerievksy is running an excellent session called XpWar. He draws four cards, each describing a problem, and four "celebrities" each argue why their problem is the worst. Audience members cycle through the celebrity chairs.
