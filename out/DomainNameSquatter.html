Domain Name Squatter

DomainNameSquatter: one who registers a domain name in hopes of selling it to a company or business likely to want to use that name.

DomainNameSquatter's often register ".com" names of corporations, in hopes of selling back the name at a high price when the company wants to go on-line. This may not always work for the DomainNameSquatter, as the company can take legal action to protect registered trademarks. However, legal action is also costly, so many companies will just pay the DomainNameSquatter.
