This problem is no better or worse with EvolutionaryDelivery than any other kind of software (or any other consumer product). The usual techniques apply:

  • Good marketing folks
  • A strong and clearly communicated vision of the product
  • A clear connection to real customers (asking your spouse, focus groups, etc)
