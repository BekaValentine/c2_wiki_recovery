Soa Implementation Framework

SoaImplementationFramework (SOAIF) is an approach to realizing a ServiceOrientedArchitecture (SOA), typically through the use of a EnterpriseServiceBus MessageOrientedMiddleware.

See for example, a paper from Fiorano software at http://www.fiorano.com/bitpipe/fiorano_soaif.pdf
