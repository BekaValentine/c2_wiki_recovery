The first female software developer I worked with had a very solid math and science background and came from a military, defense contractor background. Sometimes the stereotypes are just wrong.

I had the good fortune to work for a young woman, a few years ago, who was stunningly beautiful and so smart and capable in leading software development teams that just observing her gave me quite an education in just how sexist my assumptions had been. It was an education.
