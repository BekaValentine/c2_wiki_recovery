First Advanced Smalltalk Class

Held at Tektronix around 1985, this class was seminal in many ways. It was the first time WardAndKent worked as anything like peers. It spawned the PlumbinSimulation. And the student list includes several people who have stayed with Smalltalk (or at least objects) ever since.
