Question

This page has existed for a long time, but it does nothing to explain the current situation. I mean, how can Why be closed when it's right there?

It's closed because it's impossible to edit it. It's no longer a public site, just a private site. Editing is by membership only and you have to apply and be accepted to be able to, say, copy your own damned content off Why!!

There are big issues with what anyone might mean by "your own damned content", of course. But I have indicated to anyone that asks that I will allow people known to have been key contributors in to copy stuff out, if that is what they want.

But I note again Wiki's propensity to go anonymous when things get difficult in this way, both question and answer. Exactly the opposite of how it should be, in my view. But that may well be the difference between a RealNamesWiki and a FullResponsibilityWiki. -- RichardDrake
