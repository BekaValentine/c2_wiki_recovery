Cone Of Silence

A device (invented by and named after Professor Cone) from 60's show GetSmart that allowed two people to converse in private. Coincidentally its shape did vaguely resemble a cone.

I presume that the change was made partway through the series; maybe the writers decided that it was funnier if the CoS were not actually conical.
