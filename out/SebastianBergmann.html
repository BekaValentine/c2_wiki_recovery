Sebastian Bergmann

Sebastian Bergmann (http://www.sebastian-bergmann.de/) was born in 1978, lives in Siegburg, Germany and studies Computer Science at the University of Bonn (http://www.informatik.uni-bonn.de/). He spends his free time with the development of Free Software, is a member of the PHP (http://www.php.net/) Development Team and author of a variety of PHP Open Source projects like PHPUnit (http://www.sebastian-bergmann.de/PHPUnit/) and phpOpenTracker (http://www.phpopentracker.de/). He writes articles for both print and online media and is a frequent speaker at conferences.
