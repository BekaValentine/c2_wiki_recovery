Hacker Methodology

In contrast to the good points made by the AgileManifesto, the following is offered, in jest, on behalf of those who don't wear ties or khakis.

  • Tools should be superior to individuals as they are more reliable, and processes must be superfluid because you don't have the time to wait for les incompetents.
  • Working software is self-documenting (...because you're the best).
  • Customer "training" over "collaboration", with air-tight contracts to lock-in your strategy.
  • As your knowledge >> corporate knowledge, your plan includes overthrowing their nebulous "needs requirements" sheet.
  • Overtake corporate customer because with all the dialog (UserStories) you've been having, you know they're completely wrong-headed.
  • Take over world.
