More Heat Than Light

Arguments generate MoreHeatThanLight and I like this analogy.

"Heated Arguments" communicate a lot of information quickly, but it also creates lots of friction, and we end up missing out on any flashes of enlightening insights.

If you wish to use this as a WikiTag alert on what is happening in a series of rapid-fire exchanges between opposing viewpoints, I suggest one of many ways is to insert a post like this

  • "MoreHeatThanLight alert!"

Better done by a person previously uninvolved in the debates who feels that there could be points on both sides.
