ThereIsNoSituationSoBadThatYouCantMakeItWorseByPanicking.

Take an AmigaComputer, activate its narrator device, and execute this algorithm:

volume = 10
pitch = 440
speed = 2
 for n from 1 to 100
volume += 1
pitch += 10
speed *= 0.99
speak (volume, pitch, speed, "Don't Panic!")
