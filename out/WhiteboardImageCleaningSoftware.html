Whiteboard Image Cleaning Software

HowToDigitizeWhiteboardContent:

  1. Draw diagrams on an ordinary dry-erase WhiteBoard.
  2. Photograph with a DigitalCamera.
  3. Filter the images with one of the following products, to produce nice, clean digital images.
