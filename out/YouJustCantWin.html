You Just Cant Win

You can't win. You can't break even either.

Sounds like a tongue-in-cheek description of the first two laws of thermodynamics:

  • 0th law: there is a game
  • 1st law: you can't win
  • 2nd law: you can't break even
  • 3rd law: You can't even quit the game

Solutions:

  • become smaller
  • slow time
  • lose gracefully

Alternatively

  • learn to create energy
