Relaxation Response

A book from HerbertBenson.

The Relaxation Response.

ISBN: 0380006766

This book explains how to attain a rest by relaxing. A simple way, with no attachment to spirituality, to relax and be rested.

This response is mainly physical. There is another response that concerned the intellect based on a word that you repeat. I think this is Jacobson.

XP works much better when one is rested. This could be useful to a team member exhausted by PairProgramming or other demands.
