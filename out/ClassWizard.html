Class Wizard

A deliciously helpful and productive part of VisualCeePlusPlus.

This lets ignorance of C++ or MicrosoftFoundationClasses not get in the way of using the ClassWizard to produce lots of objects.

While widespread misuse and misunderstanding of ClassWizard may justify the sarcastic description above, if you know what it does and what its limitations are, ClassWizard is a useful labor-saving device. Don't refuse to use it just because the cool kids make fun of it.
