Sensitive Projects

SensitiveProjects are projects where the human problems are at least as important as the technical problems. We find this kind of problems in many disciplines. ManagingSensitiveProjects describes an lateral approach to them.

If you have examples, I'd be grateful. I am writing patterns from my practice and from experience of an airport-builder, a minister who had to cut budget...

-- MartineDevos
