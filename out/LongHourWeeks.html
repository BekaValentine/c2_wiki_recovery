Long Hour Weeks

Those weeks which should really be over by sometime on Wednesday - counting hours, anyway - and in which 5pm on Friday fails to offer a glimmer of hope, because you've still got half a day to go...

An apposite quote:

"SysadminsDoom: For those of you who thought Sandgorgon's Doom was too pleasant." -- PeterGutmann, quoted by AlistairYoung
