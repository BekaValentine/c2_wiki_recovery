Peter Glaskowsky

Peter N. Glaskowsky is an independent technology analyst, formerly editor in chief of In-Stat/MDR's Microprocessor Report. He also operates the Ideaphile website, which provides a large and growing number of completely free product design ideas. Some of them may be valuable, but they're all free.
