Revised Terminology

There have been a number of terms renamed since the original Beck book. Here is your secret DecoderRing.

  • CustomerTest replaces AcceptanceTest replaces FunctionalTest
  • ProgrammerTest replaces UnitTest
  • ProjectVelocity replaced LoadFactor
  • SustainablePace replaced FortyHourWeek
  • WholeTeam replaced OnsiteCustomer
