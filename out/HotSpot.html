Hot Spot

HotSpot has multiple definitions:

  • Sun's Java HotSpot virtual machine (HotSpotVm). Advertised as "raises the bar on performance via adaptive optimization technologies." [http://java.sun.com/products/hotspot]
  • The variable parts of a FrameWork are also called its HotSpot's -- PeterMaier
  • Short for a FrameWork design method which is called HotSpotDesign --ThomasWeidenfeller
  • A wireless access point, business travellers love it when they find a WiFi HotSpot
    • http://nodedb.com/
    • http://www.wi-fihotspotlist.com/
  • Hardware engineers try to prevent hot spots...
