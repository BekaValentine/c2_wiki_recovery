Mea Culpa

LatinLanguage phrase meaning "by my own fault". Mea maxima culpa means "by my own very great fault". Occurs in Enigma's song.

What is Enigma's song?

Its track 4 of MCMXC a.D. : http://www.geocities.com/bgenigma2000/albums/mcmxcad.htm

Many Roman Catholics or LRC (lapsed Roman Catholics) may remember the phrase " Mea Culpa, Mea Culpa, MeaMaximaCulpa " recited while "beating the chest" during the veneration of host.
