Addition Of Fixed Characters

When adding a character or characters to a known/defined word, either before its beginning (prefixed character(s), or after (postfixed characters(s), new shades of meaning are created - ThinkingOutLoud.DonaldNoyes.20141115

Examples:

Think
Thinks
Thinking
Rethink
Thinked (usually restated using "Thought")
Thought
Thoughts
Thoughtful
etc

Using this scenario within a HyperlinkedEnvironment, we can find ways of referencing artifacts within a wiki that are structurally consistant. It might be termed "WikiSemantics".

... ThreeDots -> TheOtherThings -> (another way of saying " more can and will be here added, at a later time ")
