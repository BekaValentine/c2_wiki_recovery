Kirill Osenkov

Kirill Osenkov has just finished his Master's Thesis in Computer Science at the Brandenburg University of Technology (Cottbus, Germany). The title is: "Designing, implementing and integrating a structured C# code editor". Further information can be found at http://www.osenkov.com/diplom

Kirill is very interested in innovation in developer tools, architecture of an IDE, language design, meta-programming, language-oriented programming, structured editors and intentional programming.
