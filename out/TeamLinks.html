Team Links

TeamLinks for Windows was a DigitalEquipmentCorporation product which was ahead of its time in 1990. It offered a full GUI based file-cabinet storage manager, X.400 compliant mail, a calendar and conferencing. -- sg

Unfortunately, due to a badly designed GUI, its complete inability to advance with the times and its appalling reliability, it was soon an object of scorn for anyone unfortunate enough to have to use it.
