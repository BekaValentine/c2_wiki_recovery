--- EwDijkstra - in EWD716 - A short talk to my students about money

One of the underlying assumptions concerns the proper role of the Universities: it is assumed that for the benefit of us all the campus should prepare the students for later life by familiarizing them, while still on campus, with the world outside, in which he will have to fit once he has got his degree.

It is a view.

Sometimes I fear that, within a few years, it may become the only view that will be permissible in our country.

It is not my view.
