Forge The Team

This might more appropriately be titled "MarriageGuidanceCounsellor".

  1. ) Recognise that differences of opinion within a team are an asset, not a liability.
No one person is ever the sole source of truth.
  1. ) Differences of opinion become dysfunctional when they are not resolved.
  1. ) When warring factions emerge, bring the individual chief protagonists together for marriage guidance counselling.

Ask person "A" - "explain your position" ("B" must remain silent). Now ditto for person "B".

Ask person "A" - "what would it take to convince you that person "B" is right. That is, what FACTS - not opinions - would you need.

Rod Taylor, 29 July 2002

Ditto for person "B".

  1. ) You now have an agreed framework with which you can resolve the differences. Go to it.
