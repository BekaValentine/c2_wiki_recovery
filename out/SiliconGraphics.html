Silicon Graphics

Made a few great computers, specifically, the Crimson, the Indigo and Indigo2, the Onyx and Onyx2, and the O2. Their first line of NT machines weren't bad (except for the NT part), and the second line of NT/Linux machines from them are just regular PCs in an SGI case and a tweaked OS.

They created OpenGl as an off shoot from IrisGl
