Too Much Cynicism Too Early

A quick, maybe brilliant, response to a framing question or thesis on a Wiki page that nonetheless in painting too cynical or negative a view inhibits progress being made in careful assessment of the earlier discussion.

A sophisticated form of EmotionalBurp?

A WikiMaster knows exactly how to deal with this phenomenon in gentleness and humility, continuing to simmer the ever expanding Wiki omelette without breaking a single egg.

As for me, I often want to move it to the bottom of the page, sometimes within another, much more obscure Wiki clone name space...

Or perhaps open the pages of a good book, or take a walk in the park?
