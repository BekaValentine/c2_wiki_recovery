Principles Of Object Oriented Software Development

Anton Elieens PrinciplesOfObjectOrientedSoftwareDevelopment

ISBN: 0201-39856-7

There's no superlative enough for this book on OO. The most thorough review of the current research, models, methods and practices in ObjectOriented world.

Recommended reading for all those who believe there's one and only and true OO model - and that should fit either Smalltalk or Eiffel ;-)
