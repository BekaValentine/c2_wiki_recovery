(A particle of the past... I wrote this a very long time ago- in my youth, so to speak, and it persists. I think I'll just leave it as it is. [03-22-10])

Basically, what I'm about is Freedom- not freedom as only liberty, but freedom as in the Unbounded Domain. Freedom like life is just a big place to explore. The process is to challenge thought; to confront limiting behaviour and patterns; to pose impossible juxtopositions. And to participate with life as we are already free and all else is a lie.

Required reading: Most anything by Robert Anton Wilson, Rudy Rucker, Scientific American, Fortean Times, and Tom Robbins, as well as anything else you can get your hands on.

Required listening: Bach Brandenburg Concertos (and everything else), Beethoven, Scriabin, The Lamb Lies Down On Broadway by Genesis, Legendary Pink Dots, Brian Eno, PFM. . .
