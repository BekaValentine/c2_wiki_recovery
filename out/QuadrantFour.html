Quadrant Four

"Quadrant Four" of SevenHabitsOfHighlyEffectivePeople = Activities that are neither important or urgent. (for example, entertainment)

Examples:

  • Trivia, busywork
  • Some phone calls
  • Time wasters
  • "Escape" activities
  • Irrelevant mail
  • Excessive TV watching
  • Excessive relaxation
