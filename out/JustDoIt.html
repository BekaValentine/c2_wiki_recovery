Just Do It

If you have a flexible context such as ExtremeProgramming, don't agonize over whether some approach might work. Just DoTheSimplestThingThatCouldPossiblyWork. If it works, you're done. If it doesn't, you'll quickly figure out why, and the agony will be over. You can back out the new approach, then try another or simply move on. You may find that your initial approach did not work, but you will learn something in the attempt that will allow you to refine it into an approach that will work. But don't guess and agonize, JustDoIt. -- RobWilliams
