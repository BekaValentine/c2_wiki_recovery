Choice Net

  • Computer networks, in particular the Internet
    • Represent critical infrastructure for business, government, military, and personal communication
    • Several recent trends in technology and network use have pushed the capabilities required of the Internet beyond what can be provided by the currently deployed infrastructure
    • ChoiceNet develops a new architectural design for the Internet for the near future representing a transformative shift enabling sustained innovation within the core of the network
