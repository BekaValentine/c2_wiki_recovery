Mobility First

The MobilityFirst program is founded on the premise that the Internet is approaching a historic inflection point

  • With mobile platforms and applications replacing the fixed-host/server model that have dominated the Internet since its beginning
  • And where a predictable, fundamental shift, is requiring a new design and paradigm for TheInternet
    • The wide-spread use of mobile devices and applications have created the need for changes in the service, trustworthiness, and management of internet artifacts using a new, safe architecture
