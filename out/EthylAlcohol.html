also known as ethanol or just "alcohol", ethyl alcohol is one of the most addictive, poisonous and socially damaging drugs known. Despite this statistical downside, as with many drugs the use of alcohol can be a pleasurable experience even for the non-addicted. Additionally--again as with other drugs--alcohol can even serve as a tool: for instance as a vehicle to altered states of consciousness or vantage points from which to experience and interpret "reality". Throughout most of human history consumption and production of ethyl alcohol has been legal in most jurisdictions, and often government sponsored.

As an added benefit, a solution of EthylAlcohol and HydrogenHydroxide makes an excellent organic solvent and DisInfectant.
