Complaint Pattern

You have a beef. You read about this pattern thing and it sounds like a good chance to catharize your venom and make a permanent memorial to your sublime powers of observation.

Don't blog it, don't write a letter to the editor. Declare it a pattern, because if your complaint is in fact a pattern you've Discovered Something Important instead of being just another DroneForTheMan.
