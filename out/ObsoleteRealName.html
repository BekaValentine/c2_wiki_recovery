Obsolete Real Name

Ozzy Osbourne's real name is John Osbourne. But just try and get him to turn around by saying "JOHN!"

Most of us can think of a few hundred celebrities who have changed their names. Some of them just wanted to make a bigger impression (Whoopi Goldberg) or a lesser one (Larry King). Some particularly pretentious pop stars have become one-word names, like Madonna, or Cher.

[Celebs often change their names because they have to, the ScreenActorsGuild requires names to be unique.]

Comedian Albert Brooks given name is Albert Einstein...no wonder he took a stage name.

Some people change their names when they change religions, like when Madonna became Esther, or Cassius Clay[sic?] became Mohammed Ali[sic?], or when Marshal Applewhite became Do.

But, does anyone here know an actual flesh-and-blood human being that simply does not answer to the name on his birth certificate?
