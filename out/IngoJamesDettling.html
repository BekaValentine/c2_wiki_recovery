Ingo James Dettling

My name is Ingo James Dettling and I am an independent software engineer. I studied computer science at the University of Karlsruhe, Germany. I graduated with a Masters in 1996. Before graduation I started to work as an independent software developer for DIGITAL in 1992. There I was involved in small CORBA projects. Since this time I am focused on OOA/OOD/OOP with a special interest in distributed applications. Also an area of interest are e-commerce applications for the finance business.

Kent Beck, Martin Fowler and the GoF really influenced my way of developing. The books of these guys are a must to every serious IT professional. You can get in contact with me using mailto:dettling@netscape.net
