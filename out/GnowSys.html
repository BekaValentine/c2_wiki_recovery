It is developed by Nagarjuna G. at the Homi Baba Centre For Science Education, TIFR, India

GnowSys is a free web application for development and maintenance of SemanticWeb information. It is developed in PythonLanguage and now used Django (See DjangoProject).

Older versions used Zope (See ZopeApplicationServer).

Further information: http://www.gnu.org/software/gnowsys/GNOWSYS_files/cpv2/index.html

The current released software is available at http://www.gnu.org/software/gnowsys/.
