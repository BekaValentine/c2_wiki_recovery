Another view concentrates on reviewing the document against 5 "C"s.

  • C - Correctness - Obviously it must be correct.
  • C - Conciseness - It must be to the point.
  • C - Clarity - It must be easy to read and understand.
  • C - Completeness - It must not have gaps or make undocumented assumptions.
  • C - Comprehensiveness - It must cover everything in the topic area.
