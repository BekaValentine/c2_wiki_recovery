Morse Code

A specific encoding of letters, numbers and punctuation into dots and dashes devised by Samuel Morse. Common letters are encoded into short sequences so it's fairly efficient. In fact, this form of encoding is the general basis (short codes for frequent symbols) of several CompressionAlgorithms.
