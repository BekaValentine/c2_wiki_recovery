Alexander Stepanov

One of the developers of the original AdaGenericLibrary in 1985. He was asked to develop a similar library for C++ in 1987, but C++ didn't have templates at the time.

Father of the CeePlusPlus StandardTemplateLibrary (with MengLee) and second only in C++ language-legal circles to BjarneStroustrup.

Collected works are available at: http://www.stepanovpapers.com.

Interesting interview at http://www.stlport.org/resources/StepanovUSA.html

"I think that object orientedness is almost as much of a hoax as Artificial Intelligence."
"Knuth taught me the answers. Dijkstra taught me the questions."
