Pattern Forms

See: CategoryPatternForm

a PatternForm is a format and structure used to write a pattern. Some well known forms are:

  • AlexandrianForm
  • CanonicalForm
  • CoplienForm
  • GoFform
  • CompactForm
  • CockburnPmForm
  • PortlandForm (also known as ThereforeBut)
  • BeckForm
  • FowlerForm
  • CoreJ2EEForm
