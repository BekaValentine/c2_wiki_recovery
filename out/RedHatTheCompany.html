Red Hat The Company

RedHatTheCompany develops and distributes the RedHatLinux distribution (an operating system including the Linux kernel). See http://www.redhat.com/ for more information.

Occupying subtle importance at the trunk of a small tree of Linux distributions and related projects is the RedHatPackageManager.

See: OpenSource, FreeSoftware, LinuxCommunity, FedoraProject
