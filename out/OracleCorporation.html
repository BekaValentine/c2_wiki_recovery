Oracle Corporation

OracleCorporation is the largest supplier of MidRange DataBase software for the UnixWay, and has a whole suite of EnterpriseApplications. It has also a MiddleWare project in progress called "Fusion".

In 05 a ZapThink analyst remarked that the upcoming Oracle Fusion is a "Frankinstein product", and questioned its ability to serve the objectives of a SOA building tool.

In the non OpenSource DataBase market, OracleDatabase ranks amongst the top three DB vendors, alongside with MicroSoft and BigBlue.

OracleCorporation is active in the standardization processes related to WebServices.

Its main competitor is SapCorporation.
