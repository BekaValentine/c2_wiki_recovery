Division of mega-publisher PearsonCompany that controls Pearson's large share of the computer book market among other areas. Many well-known computer imprints have become (or always were) the property of Pearson, including

  • AddisonWesley
  • PrenticeHall
  • QuePublishing
  • SamsPublishing
  • NewRiders
  • PeachpitPress

They also run CiscoPress and AdobePress in partnership with CiscoSystems and AdobeSystemsInc respectively, and they're behind the InformIt online publishing brand.

Pearson and OreillyAndAssociates are the main forces behind SafariTechBooksOnline.
