PL.8

A highly optimized PL/I subset for the IBM 801 research RISC microprocessor, whose rationale seemed to be researching opportunities for compiler optimization with a RISC architecture.

See M. Auslander &al.: "An Overview of the PL.8 Compiler", Proceedings of the SIGPLAN '82 Symposium on Compiler Writing).
