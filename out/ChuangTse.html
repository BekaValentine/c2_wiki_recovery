Chuang Tse

Along with LaoTse and SunTse one of the three original taoists, ChuangTse wrote a collection of very humorous and mysterious epigrams that have survived largely intact to this day. Try http://www.clas.ufl.edu/users/gthursby/taoism/cz-list.htm

Locally, see CookDing, ChuangTsesHorse and ChuangTsesFish.
