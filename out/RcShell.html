Rc Shell

Rc is the shell of the PlanNineFromBellLabs OperatingSystem. It is incompatible with BourneShell, but doesn't have any KillerFeatures over it. Apart from clearer syntax. BourneShell was really messy already, and its modern replacements are even messier.

More information about the rc shell: http://rc.cat-v.org
