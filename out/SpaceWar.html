Space War

http://lcs.www.media.mit.edu/groups/el/projects/spacewar/

Enable Java to try this early '70s computer game. Use these key bindings ...

  • turn left . . . [A] or [K]
  • turn right . . . [S] or [L]
  • thrust . . . [D] or [;]
  • fire . . . [F] or [']

We use to play spacewar on the imlac in the NineRoomAtPurdue. We had to populate the vectored interrupt logic before this program would run. JohnBottoms built button boxes (like modern-day game controllers) which made the game a lot more fun. (Lest you think we just goofed off, we also built a network graphics system and simulated shuttle landings.) -- WardCunningham
