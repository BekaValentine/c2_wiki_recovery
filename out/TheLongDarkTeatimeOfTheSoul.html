The Long Dark Tea-time Of The Soul by DouglasAdams

[ISBN 0671742515] A good book. Deranged eagles, exploding desks, coke machines in unexpected places, and the usual logical absurdities. Like, what do you do with retired immortals? And hateful yuppie lawyers? And that refrigerator you don't even want to look in because you feel so guilty about not cleaning it? Surprisingly, it all comes together into one answer, because everything is interconnected. Really, I'm not just saying that to get a free vacation to the tropics.

This is the second book in the series; see also DirkGentlysHolisticDetectiveAgency (which features the DirkGentlyNavigationMethod!)
