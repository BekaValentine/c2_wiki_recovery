Me Us Them Everyone

We use words, construct pages, express notions, ideas and concepts. They exist in several settings - Me/Mine, Us/Our, Them/Their, and Everyone/All - ThinkingOutLoud.DonaldNoyes.20141208

As a result of the various interpretations persons make when reading a WikiPage, misunderstandings of intentions occur. Divisions and Viewpoints are sometimes misconstrued because of assumptions we make.

So: One should pay due attention as to how and and in what framework words are used in the pages one builds.
