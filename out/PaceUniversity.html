Pace University

PaceUniversity is a moderately large comprehensive university in the NewYorkCity metropolitan area. It has several campuses in NYC and in Westchester County. The School of Computer Science and Information Systems has undergraduate and graduate programs including a practitioner's doctorate. The university web site is http://www.pace.edu. The CSIS school's site is http://www.pace.edu/seidenberg/ms-in-computer-science
