It Agile

it-agile (http://www.it-agile.com/, GermanLanguage web site only) is a german company providing services around agile software development:

  • Training and Coaching (Scrum, Kanban, XP, agile developer practices)
  • Strategic Consulting (Portfolio Management, Innovation Management)
  • Executive Coaching
  • Agile Company Organisation

The company has 30+ employees. Every employees is a shareholder of it-agile. In summary employees hold 62.5 percent of it-agile. it-agile is organized in autonomous business teams according to Beta-Codex.
