Yuki Wiki

YukiWiki is one of the WikiClones.

  • It is written in Perl5 by HiroshiYuki.
  • jcode.pl is used to handle Japanese coding systems (EUC and SJIS).
  • Simpler text formatting rules than the original Wiki.
  • YukiWiki is still under development in http://www.hyuki.com/yukiwiki/ (Written in Japanese only).
