Companies Hire Like Minded

Companies tend to hire people that think like they do (for good or bad). Thus, statements such as, "companies I worked for tend to do X", may not extrapolate to preferences of every company. We leave a lot of subtle and not so subtle clues about what tools and style we prefer and how we think in our resumes, interviews, references, social media sites, and referral channels.
