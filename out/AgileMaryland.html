Agile Maryland

AgileMaryland (formerly MarylandXP) is an XP users group based in Maryland. We are currently based in Columbia MD near Baltimore.

  • Announcements are made to a Yahoo Groups List http://groups.yahoo.com/group/MarylandXP
  • Subscribe at http://groups.yahoo.com/group/MarylandXP/join
  • We've got a wiki up at http://agilemaryland.org/moin/ (You must login to edit pages.)
  • The next meeting is announced at http://agilemaryland.org/moin/NextMeeting (You can subscribe to receive email notification of changes to this page.)
