Luke Hohmann

Luke Hohmann is the Director of Engineering at SmartPatents.

Luke was the Education Technical Director of ObjectSpace, Inc., a Dallas-based consulting firm providing object-oriented software and services. A high-energy, bi-lingual instructor (he speaks both Smalltalk and C++), his classes were in great demand. He's the author of JourneyOftheSoftwareProfessional, a book from Prentice-Hall on structure and process in organizations.
