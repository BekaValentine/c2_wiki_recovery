XP West Michigan: A User Group for Extreme Programming Enthusiasts Agile methods of software development are gaining popularity since they satisfy a number of business needs including:

  • Flexibility in requirements
  • Better quality software
  • Accurate development schedules
  • Code maintenance and extensibility
  • A better life for managers, developers, and end users

As a local response, many of us in West Michigan have embraced Extreme Programming as a superior agile software development method.

XP West Michigan was formed for individuals who wish to:

  • Share knowledge and experience with others using XP
  • Learn about new and novel modifications to specific practices
  • Evangelize XP to others that may benefit from its practices
  • Keep up-to-date on XP developments both nationally and internationally

Our website is http://xpwestmichigan.org
