Noble Eightfold Path

  1. Right View
  2. Right Thinking
  3. Right Speech
  4. Right Action
  5. Right Livelihood
  6. Right Diligence
  7. Right Mindfulness
  8. Right Concentration

Can be summarized;

See things by focusing upon them
Think clearly about what is important
Speak about what you know is true
Conform your actions to proper and orderly things
Continue looking upon, speaking about and acting upon these things
