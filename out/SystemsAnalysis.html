Systems Analysis

Work often performed by a SystemAnalyst, the planning and conduct of systems development in the context of the IndustrialSociology and StrategicPlanning of an enterprise. Their job is generally to find out who, what, where, when, why, and how many.

SystemsDevelopment is often thought of as a synonym for SoftwareDevelopment, but this is not the case. Entirely manual systems and automated (non-IT) systems are also the product of SystemsDevelopment.

A compact description of the steps of SystemsAnalysis is:

  • 1. Ask a lot of questions to collect requirements and preferences
  • 2. Propose multiple candidate solutions
  • 3. Evaluate the candidate solutions
  • 4. Pick the best candidate
