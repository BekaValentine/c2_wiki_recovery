High-density, lap-top information display device. Nonvolatile memory with zero power consumption. Able to display text and graphics at 1250 dots per inch resolution in monochrome or up to 32,000 colours on a 100 mega-pixel display. Features simple, intuitive, direct-manipulation user-interface with both sequential and random-access mechanisms. Functional at temperatures from -120 to +451 degrees F.
