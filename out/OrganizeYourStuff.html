Organize Your Stuff

Organizing information in any category scheme is a high-pressure task.

Organization of Information can be

  • Organization by Classification - grooping stuff according to mutually related classifications
  • Organization by Operations and Procedures - Associations which are about the handling and processing of your information
  • Organization in the placement and locales of information together with strategies for Storing and Recalling
  • Some information may belong to a grouping called TheOtherThings
