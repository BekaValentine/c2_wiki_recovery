Onlya Student

Original Opening Remarks: There are many readers of this wiki who are presently pursuing studies in Computer Science, Software Engineering, Computer Information Systems, Decision Systems, Mathematics, English (or Language Alternative), Chemistry, Physics, Philosophy, or other discipline, who may from time to time participate in comments on pages, or initiate pages (such as this one). It is to be remembered that they can be classified as OnlyaStudent, and that they represent those who have undertaken learning from the Subject Matter Experts, and will develop their own thoughts, ideas and conclusions on the validity and worth of such training as their Life Long Learning Experience continues.

Contributions they make may range from insightful to ridiculous (in keeping with most other participants).

If you fit the above categorization and wish to take on such a DramaticIdentity, consider instead of remaining anonymous, the use of the page title as your own signature to contributions. -- OnlyaStudent
