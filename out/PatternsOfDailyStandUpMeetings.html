Patterns Of Daily Stand Up Meetings

Daily StandUpMeetings meetings are one of the most wide-spread agile practises and one of the first things people try to implement.

Even though the recipe is very simple, I have seen stand-up meetings that didn't work at all.

JasonYip has proposed a number of patterns that have helped me find the right form for stand-up meetings:

  • http://martinfowler.com/articles/itsNotJustStandingUp.html.

I emphasize the underlying purpose of the meetings: self-organization.

Some of the patterns I have used recently are:

  • DontReportToTheLeader
  • LastArrivalSpeaksFirst
  • PassTheToken
