Cosmic Two By Four

From SpaceOdyssey:

The mysterious alien monolith from "2001, A Space Odyssey". The actual physical dimensions were (according to the book) in the ratio 1 x 4 x 9, the squares of the first three integers. The book mentions that the sequence does not stop at three dimensions.
