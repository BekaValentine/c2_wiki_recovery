Latin Language

The language of the Romans, and from which RomanceLanguages are descended. The EnglishLanguage still retains many Latin phrases; some of these are:

  • AdHominem
  • AdVerecundiam
  • MeaCulpa
  • PostHocErgoPropterHoc
  • MorituriTeSalutant
  • CuiBono
  • CaveatEmptor

For a long list, see see
