Mail Wiki

A collaborative method which facilitates the building, maintaining and updating of PersonalWikiPageBases between individuals, groups, or companies using Compatible WikiEngines.

Can supply a useful alternate method of submission of PersonalWiki pages to a PublicWiki, by separating the display of pages from the modification of pages. Thus defeating any form of WikiSpam. Content is moderated and displayed for reading only, any updating requiring the separate submission process. This is acceptable in the maintenance of Wikis intending to contain authorized, accurate and source-defined information for PublicNavigation.
