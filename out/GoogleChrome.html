Google Chrome

GoogleChrome is a WebBrowser developed by Google: http://www.google.com/chrome

It is fast and reliable.

Most people would consider Chrome a piece of well-designed software. Also they have fast iteration cycles. Instead of analyzing BigDesignUpFront vs Bottom-Up, or Agile-like methodologies by themselves, can we do it the other way around and talk about how was GoogleChrome built?

It is now the most used browser on the www by a small margin - http://blogs.wsj.com/digits/2012/05/21/googles-chrome-edges-past-microsoft-web-browser-in-usage/
