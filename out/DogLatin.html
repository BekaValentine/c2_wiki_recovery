Dog Latin

"Dog Latin" is a term often used for phrases that sound like Latin, but are more or less garbled English phrases. Some examples:

  • "Illegitimi non carborundum" - Don't let the bastards wear you down
  • "Quando Omni Flunkus Moritati" - When All Else Fails, Play Dead - motto of the Possum Lounge of the RedGreenShow
  • "Gustatus similis pullus" - Tastes like chicken
  • "Fabricati Diem, Pvnc" - Make my day, punk
  • "Sodomy non sapiens" - Bugger if I know
