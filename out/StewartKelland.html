Stewart Kelland

Stewart, AreYouThere? OrphanPage September 27, 2003; last edit 2001-02-14

Stewart Kelland is a developer of document reading software. In the process he has built statistical pattern classification engines and contributed to the development of neural networks.

In a previous life he made some small contributions to the field of statistical mechanics.

His other interests include astronomy, mathematics, and whether or not a computer can be aesthetically pleasing.
