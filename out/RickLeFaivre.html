A former Apple executive who most recently ran the von Liebig Center for Entrepreneurism and Technology Advancement at the University of California-San Diego has joined OVP Venture Partners - one of the region's oldest venture capital firms.

Rick LeFaivre, the former vice president of Apple's Advanced Technology Group, will start full time next month at the Kirkland firm.
