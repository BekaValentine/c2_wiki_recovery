Masters Business Enterprise And Technology

The Masters of Business, Enterprise and Technology (MBET) is a newly introduced graduate degree at the UniversityOfWaterloo to "rival" the traditional MasterOfBusinessAdministration (MBA). For more information, you can read about the program and faculty at http://www.cbet.uwaterloo.ca/MBET.html. The program is brought to students by the Center for Business, Enterprise, and Technology. --JoscelynKleingeld
