Also known as TriPLUG, this study group is a venue to learn more about software related design patterns in the greater NorthCarolinaTriangleArea (Raleigh/Durham/Cary/Chapel Hill, NC). We meet on the 2nd and 4th Thursday of each month at Borders Books in Cary to discuss specific design pattern papers.

Find out more about this group by going to its home page: http://www.rolemodelsoft.com/TriPLUG

General Schedule

  • Place: Borders Books in Cary, NC http://www.bordersstores.com/stores/index.html)
