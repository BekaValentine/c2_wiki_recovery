Manufacturing Management

ManufacturingManagement is the branch of management concerned with topics of interest mostly to ... wait for it ...manufacturing managers. Topics include but are not limited to:

  • ProcessImprovement (QualityControl, TheoryOfConstraints),
  • Scheduling (MaterialRequirementsPlanning, ClosedLoopMRP, MaterialResourcePlanning),
  • InventoryManagement (EconomicOrderQuantity, MaterialRequirementsPlanning, JustInTime),
  • MaintenanceManagement, and related
  • QuantitativeMethods (OperationsResearch, ManagementScience, LinearProgramming, ForecastingMethods, StatisticalSimulation)

. --GeorgeBrower
