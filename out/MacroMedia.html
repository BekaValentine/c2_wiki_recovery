Macro Media

Macromedia (with only one capital M) is a software company which produces

  • MacromediaContribute
  • MacromediaDirector
  • MacromediaDreamweaver
  • MacromediaFlash
  • ShockWave

URL: http://www.macromedia.com/

On December 5, 2005, Adobe (http://www.adobe.com) announced the acquistion of Macromedia. Macromedia's products have been merged into Adobe's product line.
