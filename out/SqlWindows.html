What makes it one of the better ones?

Google groups on SqlWindows and VisualBasic, and let me know if the VisualBasic info is out of date. Basically in SqlWindows it is much easier/possible to create base classes for forms and screen widgets and inherit from them, so it is much less work to create similar behaving forms/widgets/etc. VisualBasic is better for small applications, and much cheaper, but SqlWindows comes with more features out of the box.
