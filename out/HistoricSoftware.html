To make Ward's point more carefully: I would say that at least some of the volunteer/paid staff at places like the ComputerHistoryMuseum [OnLineComputerMuseum] actually are aware that such individuals exist, but they don't believe that their primary audience - the average museum visitors - have any such interest.
