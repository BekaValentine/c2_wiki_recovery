I'm assuming you're referring to Dynamo's resemblance to an ExoKernel. However, only someone entirely ignorant of OperatingSystemsDesign can think that exokernel implies eliminating operating systems. You can't eliminate operating systems, since all LanguagesAreOperatingSystems.

However, because sometimes LanguageIsAnOs, you *can* eliminate the dichotomy of the language and the OS being two completely separate things.
