Social Annotation

SocialAnnotation is public annotation of arbitrary Webpages. Currently the following public free of charge service providers offer this service to the general public:

  • DiiGo
  • TrailFire
  • WikAlong
  • AmpliFy
  • ClipMarks
  • SideWiki
  • ReframeIt
  • ShiftSpace
  • ..
