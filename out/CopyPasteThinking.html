Copy Paste Thinking

CopyPasteThinking is the act of using a predefined or commonly accepted thought process without analyzing it prior to use. The original thought process can originate from an individual, or from a group.

--JavidJamae (though GeoffSobering and LeviCook deserve credit for coining the term)
