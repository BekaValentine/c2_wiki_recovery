Helpful Critical Guy Syndrome

ScottAdams' term for those of us who succumb to our urges for constant, good-natured nitpicking... a condition that is plainly evidently an epidemic here on WikiWikiWeb.

http://dilbertblog.typepad.com/the_dilbert_blog/2006/04/helpful_critica.html

I think criticism is a fundamental part of C2. What is bothersome is rudeness delivered with it. One can politely state their opinion without insulting the other's intelligence, mother, and dog. It is the delivery, not the existence of criticism that is the problem in my opinion. Some unfortunately believe in CriticizeBluntly.
