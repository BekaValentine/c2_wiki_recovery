All Clear

AllClear is a cool little affordable program from Clear Software, 385 Elliot St., Newton, MA 02164

It does

  • Flow Charts
  • Process Flow Diagrams
  • Organizational Charts
  • Fishbone Diagrams
  • Cause and Effect Diagrams
  • Decision Trees
  • Hierarchical Charts
  • Data Flow Diagrams

with a neat text/graphic dual representation ... you enter text and get out graphics. Very cool! but still an example of IncompleteNotations --RaySchneider
