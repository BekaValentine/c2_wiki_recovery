Release Management

Release Management ties in with the concept of ProductLinePractice.

Basically, how we enhance, re-test and re-release software to our customers in a controlled fashion:

  1. without confusing them to bits by introducing new functionality faster than they can be retrained
  2. being able to retest the entire solution before release

How will we release different "versions" of applications in the Web Services world, where services can be dynamically linked at run-time...? -- MikeMat@nedcor.com
