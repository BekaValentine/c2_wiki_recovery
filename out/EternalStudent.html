Eternal Student

This is the attitude of someone who's always learning, always looking for NewInsights, new ways to look at TheWorld (or LifeTheUniverseAndEverything).

There's an internal aspect - that is, how can I make sense out of this world, how can I understand it?

There's also an external aspect - how can we communicate with each other, where I can understand what you see (your MentalModel) and vice versa?

-JeffMantei 2000-11-21

See JustaStudent, optimistic sense.

Contrast with ProfessionalStudent --PeteHardie
